* [[UFDC]] rebels attack the [[Chad]]ian city of [[Ade, Chad|Ade]] and battle the [[Military of Chad]]. Both the UFDC and the [[Government of Chad|Déby administration]] claim to have control over the city, stating that opposing forces have fled.  [http://www.cnn.com/2006/WORLD/africa/07/03/chad.reut/index.html?section=cnn_latest (CNN)] [[United Nations Secretary General]] [[Kofi Annan]] issues a report warning that ongoing fighting in Chad, [[Sudan]], the [[Central African Republic]], and [[Cameroon]] are increasingly destabilized and that borders are loosely enforced. [http://allafrica.com/stories/200607040050.html (allAfrica.com)]
* [[North Korea]] [[North Korean Missile Test (2006)|test-launches]] at least six missiles which landed in the [[Sea of Japan]]. An official from the [[United States Department of State]] claims that one of the missiles was a long-range [[Taepodong 2]] missile which failed 35 seconds after takeoff.[http://www.cnn.com/2006/WORLD/asiapcf/07/04/korea.missile/index.html (CNN)], [http://mdn.mainichi-msn.co.jp/national/news/20060705p2a00m0na006000c.html (Mainichi Shimbun)]
* A [[Qassam rocket]] hits a [[High school]] at [[Ashkelon]], an [[Israel]]i city with a population exceeding 117,000. The rocket was launched by [[Hamas]] militants from the town of Beit Hanun in the northern [[Gaza strip]]. [http://www.haaretz.com/hasen/spages/734605.html (Haaretz)]
* The [[Space Shuttle Discovery|Space Shuttle ''Discovery'']] takes off from [[Cape Canaveral]] for the [[International Space Station]] at 2:38pm [[North American Eastern Time Zone|EDT]] (1838 UTC) after its liftoff had been postponed twice. [http://www.cnn.com/2006/TECH/space/07/04/shuttle.launch/index.html (CNN)]
* A bomb scare in the arrivals area of [[Dublin Airport]] results in a complete closure of the main building. The man who is responsible for the scare has been arrested; he claims to be a member of [[al-Qaeda]].  Approximately 9,000 passengers and 50 flights were affected during the two-hour disruption. [[wikinews:Bomb scare in Dublin Airport|(Wikinews)]]
