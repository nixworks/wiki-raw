* Talks between the [[European Union]] and the [[United States]] [[Department of Homeland Security]] on the transfer of airline passenger data break down. The US demands over 30 items of data on each passenger, an earlier agreement which had been deemed to break European privacy laws. Midnight tonight was the deadline for reaching a new agreement. Airlines face losing landing rights in the US if they do not provide the information, and heavy fines in the EU if they do. [http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/world/europe/5395928.stm (BBC)]
