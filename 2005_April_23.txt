* [[Israeli-Palestinian conflict]]:
** The [[Association of University Teachers]], the leading [[United Kingdom|British]] [[academic]] group, has voted to boycott two [[Israel]]i universities, [[Haifa University]]  and [[Bar-Ilan University]], over their alleged involvement in "illegal activity" in the occupied territories. The AUT said it voted for the boycott in response to a plea for action by a group of [[Palestinian people|Palestinian]] academics. It was condemned by the Israeli Embassy, the British Ambassador in Israel, by Jewish Human Rights groups and by [[Universities UK]]. [http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/uk_news/education/4472169.stm (BBC)], [http://observer.guardian.co.uk/uk_news/story/0,6903,1469015,00.html (Guardian)]
** [[Israeli security forces]] arrest 3 suspected Palestinian militants on the Geha Highway near [[Ra'annana]], [[Israel]]. When the police arrived, the 3 suspects were barricaded in their car, while a 4th suspect fled the scene. Earlier in the night, the [[Israeli Police]] put roadblocks and checkpoints after it received high alert on "rolling" [[suicide bombing]] attack. [http://www.haaretz.com/hasen/spages/568452.html (Haaretz)], [http://www.ynetnews.com/articles/0,7340,L-3076684,00.html (Ynet)]
** A 17-year-old [[Palestinian people|Palestinian]] is caught carrying 4 [[pipe bomb]]s in Beit Furik checkpoint. [http://www.haaretz.com/hasen/spages/568452.html (Haaretz)]
** A Qassam rocket was launched toward northern Gaza community. No casualties were reported. Also, Palestinian militants stabbed a soldier near Ganim settlement, injuring him moderately. [http://www.haaretz.com/hasen/spages/568452.html (Haaretz)]
* A rodent species representing a new family of [[mammal]]s, [[Laonastidae]], is discovered in [[Laos]]. This is the first new family of mammals discovered since [[1974]]. [http://journals.cambridge.org/bin/bladerunner?REQUNIQ=1114272586&REQSESS=6569750&118000REQEVENT=&REQINT1=292893&REQAUTH=0 (Cambridge Journals)]
* [[Bangladesh]] accuses the [[Border Security Force|BSF]] of gunning down two farmers who had strayed into [[no man's land]] at [[Hairharpur]] on the [[India|Indo]]-Bangla border. Indian officials say the guards had only shot at and injured two Bangladeshis trying to sneak into [[West Bengal]].
