*[[2015 Hindu Kush earthquake]]
**An [[earthquake]]  measuring 7.5 on the [[Moment magnitude scale]] (MMS) strikes in the [[Hindu Kush]] [[mountain range]] in northeastern [[Afghanistan]],  158 miles from [[Kabul]], in the remote province of [[Badakhshan Province|Badakhshan]], one of Afghanistan’s most inaccessible regions. Tremors reached as far as [[New Delhi]], [[India]]. At least 263 are dead in Afghanistan and nearby northern Pakistan with more than 1,000 injured. The death toll will likely climb since communications in much of the affected areas are cut, and rescue teams had not yet reached districts closest to the [[epicenter]], including some that are controlled by [[Taliban]] insurgents.
