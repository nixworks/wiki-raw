*[[2016 Orlando nightclub shooting]]
**In what is the deadliest [[mass shooting]] and the worst terror attack since [[September 11 attacks|9/11]] to occur in the United States, a mass shooting at a [[Pulse (nightclub)|gay nightclub]] in the [[United States|U.S.]] city of [[Orlando, Florida]], kills at least 50 people, including the shooter, and injures 53. [http://www.bbc.com/news/world-us-canada-36512308 (BBC)] [http://www.cnn.com/2016/06/12/us/orlando-nightclub-shooting/ (CNN)] [http://www.abc.net.au/news/2016-06-13/live-blog-orlando-nightclub-shooting/7504096 (Australian Broadcasting Co.)]
**ISIL's Amaq News Agency claims that the assault, "... was carried out by an Islamic State fighter". [http://www.reuters.com/article/us-florida-shooting-claim-idUSKCN0YY0VU (Reuters)] [http://newyork.cbslocal.com/2016/06/12/orlando-nightclub-shooting/ (CBS New York)]
**The FBI identifies the deceased gunman as [[Omar Mateen|Omar Mir Seddique Mateen]], a 29-year-old American citizen born in [[New York]] to [[Afghan]]i parents, and living in [[Port St. Lucie, Florida]]. Mateen called [[9-1-1]] during the attack and pledged allegiance to [[Islamic State of Iraq and the Levant|ISIL]]. [http://newyork.cbslocal.com/2016/06/12/orlando-nightclub-shooting/ (CBS New York)]
**Mateen's father, Seddique Mir Mateen, says, "We are saying we are apologizing for the whole incident. This had nothing to do with religion." Seddique  says that during a recent outing in [[Miami]], his son got very angry when he saw men kissing and touching each other. According to the elder Mateen, Omar said, 'Look at that. In front of my son they are doing that.' [http://www.nbcnews.com/storyline/orlando-nightclub-massacre/terror-hate-what-motivated-orlando-nightclub-shooter-n590496 (NBC News)]
**The [[Orlando,_Florida#Government|City of Orlando]] requests, via [[Twitter]], citizens, "... hold off on vigils&nbsp;— they represent a serious strain on our limited resources, which we need to dedicate to law enforcement and victims." [http://www.nytimes.com/live/orlando-nightclub-shooting-live-updates/ (''The New York Times'')]

[http://www.dailynews.com/general-news/20160612/man-arrested-with-weapons-explosive-materials-wanted-to-harm-people-at-la-pride (''Los Angeles Daily News'')]
