*[[Crimean referendum, 2014]]
**Voters in [[Crimea]] vote overwhelmingly to [[Declaration of Independence of Crimea and Sevastopol|leave Ukraine]] and [[Republic of Crimea|rejoin Russia]]. Ukraine, the [[European Union]], and the [[United States]] condemn the referendum, which did not give voters the option of the status quo. [http://www.bbc.com/news/world-europe-26598832 (BBC)] [http://hosted2.ap.org/APDEFAULT/3d281c11a96b4ad082fe88aa0db04305/Article_2014-03-16-Ukraine/id-ea1ac91021854438abc5635e9a984e30 (Associated Press)]
*[[Serbian parliamentary election, 2014]]
**Voters in [[Serbia]] go to the polls for an early  parliamentary election. The centre right [[Serbian Progressive Party|SNS]] is reported to be receiving around 50% of the popular vote. [http://www.chron.com/news/world/article/Serbia-votes-in-early-parliamentary-election-5321684.php (AP via ''Houston Chronicle'')], [http://www.straitstimes.com/breaking-news/world/story/serbias-centre-right-wins-snap-poll-landslide-estimate-20140317 (Straits Times)]
