*[[Ecuadorian general election, 2017]]
**Voters head to the polls   to select the [[National Assembly (Ecuador)|National Assembly]] and a new president with former vice-president [[Lenín Moreno]] facing off against former bank president [[Guillermo Lasso]], and six other opposition candidates. There will be an  April 2   runoff unless the leader receives  40 percent of the votes and is at least 10 percentage points ahead of the others. In addition, there's a [[Ecuadorian tax haven referendum, 2017|tax haven referendum]]. [http://www.reuters.com/article/us-ecuador-election-idUSKBN15Y05A (Reuters)]
