* Talks on the new [[European Union|EU]] budget may not be completed under the United Kingdom's presidency, the [[United Kingdom|UK]]'s minister for Europe warns. [http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/uk_politics/4494452.stm (BBC)]
* [[ROC local elections, 2005]]: [[Republic of China]] (Taiwan) opposition party [[Kuomintang]] (Chinese Nationalist Party) makes major gains in municipal elections, taking 14 of 23 mayor or county magistrate seats. Ruling [[Democratic Progressive Party]] takes six seats. [[People First Party (Republic of China)|People First Party]] and [[New Party (Republic of China)|New Party]] each takes one seat, and an independent wins one seat. DPP chairman [[Su Tseng-chang]] resigns to take responsibility for his party's defeat.
