*[[Free University of Berlin]] chemists confirm that [[carbon]] can bond with more than four atoms, previously seen as its limit because carbon  has only four shareable electrons. The researchers used [[X-rays]] to, for the first time, map the molecule —  a carbon atom bonded to six other carbon atoms. [https://www.sciencenews.org/article/carbon-can-exceed-four-bond-limit (''Science News'')], [https://www.zmescience.com/science/news-science/carbon-six-bond-molecule/  (ZME Science)]
