* [[Arab–Israeli conflict]]:
** [[Israel]]i jets bomb the [[PFLP-GC]] base in [[Naameh]], [[Lebanon]], a few miles outside [[Beirut]], wounding two people, in retaliation for a rocket attack that hit [[Qiryat Shemona]]. Israeli warplanes then fly over southern Lebanon and the western Bekaa Valley in reconnaissance flights, drawing anti-aircraft fire from the Lebanese army. The PFLP-GC denies responsibility for the rockets that hit Kiryat Shmona. Major General [[Udi Adam]] has not ruled out targeting installations in [[Syria]].
