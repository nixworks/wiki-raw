*[[2015 Pacific hurricane season]]
**[[Hurricane Patricia]], which hit the Mexican coast as a [[Saffir–Simpson hurricane wind scale|Category 5]] storm, appeared to leave remarkably little damage as it moved rapidly inland over mountainous [[western Mexico]] and is now a tropical depression (35 mph/55 kph). The storm is currently centered northeast of [[Zacatecas City|Zacatecas, Mexico]], moving  northeast at 24 mph/39 kph.   While there are no  reports of casualties or substantial widespread damage, authorities warn  deadly floods and mudslides remain possible.
