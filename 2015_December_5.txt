*[[Colombia]]n President [[Juan Manuel Santos]] announces the discovery of the [[Spain|Spanish]] [[galleon]] [[Wager's Action|San José]] which sunk off that country’s coast over 300 years ago. The wreck’s cargo is placed somewhere in the $4 to $17 billion range. [http://www.npr.org/sections/thetwo-way/2015/12/05/458586878/wreck-of-legendary-spanish-galleon-is-found-colombia-says (NPR)]
