*[[Icelandic parliamentary election, 2016]]
**[[Iceland]]’s incumbent, centre-right [[Independence Party (Iceland)|Independence Party]] surprised pollsters by winning 29 percent of the vote (21 seats), thereby putting leader [[Bjarni Benediktsson (born 1970)|Bjarni Benediktsson]] in position to organize another coalition government.  The [[Progressive Party (Iceland)|Progressive Party]], the current ruling partner,  garnered 11.5% and eight positions, a loss of  11 seats. The new [[Pirate Party (Iceland)|Pirate Party]] didn't perform as predicted with 14.5% of the ballots, though it did get 10 seats, an increase of seven more positions in the 63-seat [[Althing|parliament (Althing)]]. [http://www.nbcnews.com/news/world/iceland-s-radical-pirate-party-falls-short-parliamentary-election-n675516 (NBC News)] [http://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2016-10-30/pirate-party-surge-falls-short-as-icelanders-back-stability (Bloomberg)] [https://www.theguardian.com/world/2016/oct/30/iceland-elections-ruling-centre-right-party-pirate-party (''The Guardian'')] [http://icelandreview.com/news/2016/10/29/election-facts (''Iceland Review'')]
**[[Prime Minister of Iceland|Prime Minister]] [[Sigurður Ingi Jóhannsson]], leader of  the Progressive Party, resigns. [http://www.independent.co.uk/news/world/europe/iceland-prime-minister-resigns-sigurdur-ingi-johannsson-pirate-party-a7387846.html (''The Independent'')]
**Icelandic voters elect 30 women to parliament, the most ever for the island nation. Voter turnout was under 80 percent, the lowest ever. [http://www.npr.org/sections/thetwo-way/2016/10/30/499973354/women-win-30-seats-in-icelands-parliament-more-than-any-party (NPR)] [http://nymag.com/thecut/2016/10/women-now-make-up-almost-half-of-icelands-parliament.html (''New York'')]

*[[2016 World Series]]
**The [[Chicago Cubs]] defeat the [[Cleveland Indians]] 3 runs to 2 at Illinois' [[Wrigley Field]] in Game 5 of the 2016 World Series to avoid elimination and force a Game 6 with the series standing at 3 games to 2 in favor of the Cleveland Indians.
