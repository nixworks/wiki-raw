* It appears that [[Stardust (spacecraft)|Stardust]] has successfully flown past [[Comet Wild 2]] collecting samples that it will return to Earth in two years time. This is the first sample return mission to a comet and the first time that samples have returned to Earth from any celestial body since 1974. The spacecraft also took detailed images of the comet's icy nucleus.
