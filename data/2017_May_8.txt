*The journal ''[[Genes & Development]]'' publishes research from the [[University of Texas Southwestern Medical Center]] in a [[mice|mouse]]-model study (which may translate over to humans) alluding as to why [[hair]] turns [[Human hair color#Gray and white hair|gray]] and goes [[Hair loss|bald]]. In mice, these two proteins are important for baldness and graying. [http://time.com/4770554/grey-hair-loss/ (''Time'')]
