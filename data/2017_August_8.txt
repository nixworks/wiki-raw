*[[Battle of Marawi]]
**[[United States Department of Defense|The Pentagon]] is considering conducting airstrikes in [[Marawi]], which if approved would later put U.S. troops on the ground to battle the ISIS-affiliated [[Maute group]] as well as related Islamist terror groups. A spokesperson later denied the claims as well as clarified that the [[Philippines]] has yet to make a request. *[[North Korea–United States relations]]
**U.S. President [[Donald Trump]] promises "fire and fury like the world has never seen" if North Korea threatens the United States. [http://www.nbcnews.com/politics/white-house/trump-vows-north-korea-could-be-met-fire-fury-n790896 (NBC News)]

;Law and crime
*[[Burial of Ferdinand Marcos]]
**The [[Supreme Court of the Philippines]] has junked all petitions against the burial of former [[President of the Philippines]] [[Ferdinand Marcos]] at the [[Heroes' Cemetery|Libingan ng mga Bayani]], re-affirming its earlier ruling on 8 November 2016. [https://cnnphilippines.com/news/2017/08/08/SC-affirms-ruling-marcos-burial.html (CNN Philippines)]

;Politics and elections
*[[Kenyan general election, 2017]]
**Voters in [[Kenya]] go to the polls to elect the [[President of Kenya|President]], as well as members of the [[National Assembly (Kenya)|National Assembly]] and [[Senate of Kenya|Senate]]. [http://www.bbc.com/news/world-africa-40855040 (BBC)]
*[[Corruption in South Africa]]
**[[President of South Africa|President]] [[Jacob Zuma]] survives his eighth no-confidence motion in parliament via a 198–177 secret ballot vote with nine abstentions. The [[South African rand|rand]] fell by 1 percent on the announcement of the outcome.  [http://www.reuters.com/article/us-safrica-politics-idUSKBN1AN2JY?il=0 (Reuters)]

;Sports
*[[PGA Championship]]
**In [[golf]], the [[Professional Golfers' Association of America|PGA of America]] and the [[PGA Tour]] jointly announce that the [[PGA Championship]], currently the last of the four [[men's major golf championships|men's major championships]] to be held each year, will move from its current August date to May effective in 2019. In the same year, [[The Players Championship]] will move from May to March. [https://www.nytimes.com/2017/08/08/sports/golf/pga-championship-may.html (Reuters via ''The New York Times'')]
