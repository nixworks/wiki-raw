* [[Burial of Ferdinand Marcos]]
** The [[Supreme Court of the Philippines|Supreme Court]] has junked all petitions against the burial of former [[President of the Philippines]] [[Ferdinand Marcos]] at the [[Heroes' Cemetery|Libingan ng mga Bayani]], re-affirming its earlier ruling on 8 November 2016.
