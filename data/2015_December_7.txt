*[[Iraqi Civil War (2014–present)]]
**[[Turkey]] refuses to withdraw its [[Turkish Armed Forces|forces]] from [[Iraqi Kurdistan|northern Iraq]] despite being given a 48 hour ultimatum by [[Iraq]] to leave the country. The Iraqi government says it never invited such a force, and will take its case to the [[United Nations]] if they are not pulled out. *[[Venezuelan parliamentary election, 2015]]
**[[Venezuela|Venezuela's]] [[National Electoral Council (Venezuela)|National Electoral Council]] announces  the  opposition [[Democratic Unity Roundtable]] (MUD) has won at least  99 seats in the 167-member [[National Assembly]] in Sunday's elections.
