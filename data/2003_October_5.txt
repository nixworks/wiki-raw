* [[Maher Arar]] is reported to have been freed from a [[Syria]]n jail. The Canadian engineer was deported to Syria by the United States as he changed planes in New York, over a year ago.<ref>[http://www.cbc.ca/stories/2003/10/05/arar031005] {{webarchive |url=https://web.archive.org/web/20060307044153/http://www.cbc.ca/stories/2003/10/05/arar031005 |date=March 7, 2006 }}</ref> He will arrive in [[Montreal]] the following afternoon.<ref>[http://www.cbc.ca/stories/2003/10/06/arar_back03] {{webarchive |url=https://web.archive.org/web/20050418064304/http://www.cbc.ca/stories/2003/10/06/arar_back03 |date=April 18, 2005 }}</ref>
* [[Ain es Saheb airstrike]]: [[Israel]]i warplanes attack an alleged [[Islamic Jihad Movement in Palestine|Islamic Jihad]] training base deep in [[Syria]] in retaliation for a [[suicide bombing]] at a Haifa restaurant that killed 19 people, the army said Sunday. Israeli media state this is the first Israeli attack on Syrian soil in more than two decades. An emergency session of the UN Security Council is scheduled to debate the action.  France and Germany condemn the attack. The international community calls for restraint by all parties involved.<ref>[http://www.hinduonnet.com/thehindu/holnus/01052200.htm] {{webarchive |url=https://web.archive.org/web/20031028130659/http://www.hinduonnet.com/thehindu/holnus/01052200.htm |date=October 28, 2003 }}</ref>
* [[Pope John Paul II]] canonizes [[Daniele Comboni]] (1831–1881), [[Arnold Janssen]] (1837–1909) and [[Josef Freinademetz]] (1852–1908).
* ''[[Ireland on Sunday]]'' claims that [[Pope John Paul&nbsp;II]] is suffering from terminal [[stomach cancer]] which has spread to his [[Colon (anatomy)|colon]]. The newspaper reports that the Pope has dictated a [[living will]] which gives instructions as to how the Roman Catholic Church is to be administered when the medical treatment he is receiving makes it impossible for him to function as pope. According to the paper, [[Cardinal (Catholicism)|Cardinals]] have been told to be ready at a moment's notice to fly to [[Rome]] for a [[Papal funeral]] and [[Papal conclave]].
