*[[Viacom International Media Networks]] has announced it will no longer pursue a [[Nickelodeon]] underwater theme park and resort in [[Palawan]], [[Philippines]]. The project, announced last January, attracted controversy due to criticisms from environmentalist groups who fear the resort might destroy the area's marine eocsystem, home to [[Puerto Princesa Subterranean River National Park|two UNESCO]] [[Tubbataha Reef|World Heritage sites]].
