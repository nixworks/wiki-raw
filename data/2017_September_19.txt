*[[2017 Atlantic hurricane season]]
**[[Hurricane Maria]]
***Hurricane Maria makes landfall on [[Dominica]] as a [[category 5 hurricane]] with maximum sustained winds of 160 mph (260 km/h). Maria is the second category 5 hurricane to make landfall at that intensity in the 2017 season, making this and [[2007 Atlantic hurricane season|2007]] the only seasons on record to feature this occurence. [http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/text/refresh/MIATCUAT5+shtml/190133.shtml (National Hurricane Center)]
*[[2017 Puebla earthquake]]
**A [[Moment magnitude scale|magnitude]] 7.1 earthquake strikes Mexico, causing the collapse of a building in Mexico City and widespread evacuations, hours after a yearly earthquake drill in commemoration of the [[1985 Mexico earthquake]].[http://www.eluniversal.com.mx/metropoli/cdmx/se-registra-sismo-en-la-cdmx (El Universal)]

*[[Crime in Louisiana]]
**Two [[African-American]] men are killed in separate incidents near [[Baton Rouge, Louisiana]], and a third home is shot at in what local and federal officials call racially-motivated crimes. A local man, Kenneth James Gleason, is arrested on suspicion of the murders. Gleason was out on bail for drug-related charges. [https://www.cbsnews.com/news/white-baton-rouge-man-arrested-deaths-black-men/ (CBS News)] [https://www.usnews.com/news/us/articles/2017-09-19/white-man-arrested-in-killings-of-two-black-men-in-louisiana (U.S. News & World Report)]
