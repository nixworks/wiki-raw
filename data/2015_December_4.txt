*[[Military intervention against ISIL]]
**In an act of solidarity with [[France]] following the [[November 2015 Paris attacks|Paris massacre]], [[Germany|Germany's]] parliament (the [[Bundestag]]) approves measures to move past the [[Bundeswehr|military's]] primary defensive role, to send six of its 29 operational, yet aged, [[Panavia Tornado|Tornado]] [[reconnaissance aircraft]], and as many as 1,200 soldiers to support the coalition missions against [[Islamic State of Iraq and the Levant|Islamic State]] militants in [[Syria]]. The airplanes will be limited to support activities; they will not fly combat missions. [http://www.latimes.com/world/europe/la-fg-germany-islamic-state-20151204-story.html (''The Los Angeles Times'')], [http://in.reuters.com/article/mideast-crisis-germany-idINL8N13S41D20151203 (Reuters)], [http://edition.cnn.com/2015/12/04/europe/isis-germany-syria/index.html (CNN)]
