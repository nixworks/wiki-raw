* U.S. newscast ''[[Nightline (US news program)|Nightline]]'' is taken off the air by several stations owned by the [[Sinclair Broadcast Group]] because of its planned airing of a list of [[Casualties of the Iraq War|U.S. soldiers killed in Iraq]]. Sinclair claims it is a political ploy, while network [[American Broadcasting Company|ABC]] says it is meant as "an expression of respect which simply seeks to honor those who have laid down their lives for this country.

* [[Republic of Macedonia|Macedonian]] officials admit that they staged a bogus gun-battle with "[[Terrorism|terrorists]]" in March 2002 and that they knew the seven men slain had no terrorist connections. * [[Diego Maradona]] leaves hospital after 12 days of treatment for [[heart]] and [[breathing]] problems.
