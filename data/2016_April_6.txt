*[[2015–16 South Pacific cyclone season]]
**[[2015–16 South Pacific cyclone season#Severe Tropical Cyclone Zena|Cyclone Zena]], now a category 3 storm, heads for [[Fiji]], where a tropical depression already dumped up to 12 inches of rain on the western and northern parts of Fiji's largest and most populous island, [[Viti Levu]], triggering major flash flooding that's killed at least two people. The [[archipelago]] is still recovering from category 5 [[Cyclone Winston]] that killed 44 people in February. *[[2016 Ethiopia flood]]
**Deadly floods hit [[Ethiopia]], leaving at least 28 people dead as seasonal rains come early to the country. The majority of these deaths occurred in the city of [[Jijiga]] while elsewhere, heavy downpours of rain were reported with more floods expected in the next few days. [http://www.aljazeera.com/news/2016/04/deadly-floods-hit-ethiopia-160406101102909.html (''Al Jazeera'')]

;Law and crime
*The [[International Criminal Tribunal for the former Yugoslavia]], in a majority vote, ordered an "indefinite" halt in the war crimes trial of [[Serbs of Croatia|Croatian Serb]] rebel leader [[Goran Hadžić]], who is terminally ill with brain cancer and declared "unfit to stand trial." Hadžić was charged with 14 war crimes and crimes against humanity for his role in the [[Croatian War of Independence|1991–95 war]] in [[Croatia]]. [http://www.rferl.org/content/hague-war-crimes-court-halts-trial-ill-serbian-leader-hadzic/27657192.html (Radio Free Europe/Radio Liberty)]
*The [[President of Russia]] [[Vladimir Putin]] announces the creation of a new [[National Guard of Russia|National Guard]], which he said would fight terrorism and organized crime. However, some critics claim the creation of the National Guard is linked to the upcoming [[Russian legislative election, 2016|legislative election]] this September with them saying that Putin fears unrest, something Putin's spokesman [[Dmitry Peskov]] denies. [http://www.bbc.com/news/world-europe-35975840 (BBC)]
