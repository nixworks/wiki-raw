*[[Venezuelan Constitutional Assembly election, 2017]]
**Voters in [[Venezuela]] participate in a controversial election for members of a new Constitutional Assembly, devised by President [[Nicolás Maduro]] to amend [[Constitution of Venezuela|the constitution]] following a [[2017 Venezuelan constitutional crisis|legislative takeover in March]]. [[2017 Venezuelan protests|Protests and strikes]] are expected. [http://www.telesurtv.net/english/news/Venezuelas-Constituent-Assembly-will-Bring-Peace-Rodriguez-20170722-0018.html (teleSUR)]
*[[Senegalese parliamentary election, 2017]]
**Voters in [[Senegal]] elect all 150 members of their [[National Assembly (Senegal)|National Assembly]], with former President [[Abdoulaye Wade]] leading [[Senegalese Democratic Party|a coalition]] against the incumbent [[Macky Sall]]'s [[Alliance for the Republic (Senegal)|United in Hope coalition]]. *[[Republic of the Congo parliamentary election, 2017]]
**Voters in the [[Republic of the Congo]] return to the polls for the [[Two-round system|second round]] of an election for 139 [[National Assembly (Republic of the Congo)|National Assembly]] members, after the governing [[Congolese Party of Labour]] won 70 seats in the first round two weeks earlier.
