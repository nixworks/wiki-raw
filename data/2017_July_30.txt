;Politics and elections
*[[Venezuelan Constitutional Assembly election, 2017]]
**Voters in [[Venezuela]] participate in a controversial election for members of a new Constitutional Assembly, devised by President [[Nicolás Maduro]] to amend [[Constitution of Venezuela|the constitution]] following a [[2017 Venezuelan constitutional crisis|legislative takeover in March]]. Many Venezuelans question the election's legitimacy, resulting in boycotts and [[2017 Venezuelan protests|ongoing protests]]. [http://www.latimes.com/world/mexico-americas/la-fg-venezuela-constitution-20170730-htmlstory.html (''The Los Angeles Times'')]
*[[Senegalese parliamentary election, 2017]]
**Voters in [[Senegal]] elect all 165 members of their [[National Assembly (Senegal)|National Assembly]], with former President [[Abdoulaye Wade]] leading [[Senegalese Democratic Party|a coalition]] against the incumbent [[Macky Sall]]'s [[Alliance for the Republic (Senegal)|United in Hope coalition]]. [http://www.dw.com/en/three-way-rivalry-intensifies-before-elections-in-senegal/a-39886174 (DW)]
*[[Republic of the Congo parliamentary election, 2017]]
**Voters in the [[Republic of the Congo]] return to the polls for the [[Two-round system|second round]] of an election for 139 [[National Assembly (Republic of the Congo)|National Assembly]] members, after the governing [[Congolese Party of Labour]] won 70 seats in the first round two weeks earlier. [http://www.rfi.fr/afrique/20170722-congo-brazzaville-resultats-legislatives-pct-sassou-nguesso (RFI)]
