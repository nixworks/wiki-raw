*[[2017 Atlantic hurricane season]]
**[[Hurricane Harvey]]
***Harvey is now a [[Tropical cyclone#Tropical storm|tropical storm]] with sustained winds of 70 mph (110 kmh). It made landfall on Texas's eastern coast, the eye striking the town of [[Rockport, Texas|Rockport]], as a [[Saffir–Simpson scale#Category 4|Category 4]] hurricane. It's the strongest storm to hit the state since 1961. Harvey is expected to maintain tropical storm strength, with heavy rains and flooding, for at least four days. [http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/text/refresh/MIATCPAT4+shtml/261748.shtml (National Hurricane Center)]  [https://www.nytimes.com/2017/08/26/us/hurricane-harvey-texas.html (''The New York Times'')] [http://www.bbc.com/news/world-us-canada-41062633 (BBC)] [http://www.chron.com/news/houston-weather/hurricaneharvey/article/Harvey-impacts-Texas-Latest-tropical-weather-11953474.php (''Houston Chronicle'')]
***Two people in Texas are confirmed dead with many more reported injured. Authorities are unable to visit the hardest hit area so the toll of dead and injured is expected to rise.[https://www.dallasnews.com/news/weather/2017/08/26/harvey-downgraded-tropical-storm-danger-far (The Dallas Morning News)], [https://www.yahoo.com/news/damaging-hurricane-harvey-settles-over-southeast-texas-082259260.html?soc_trk=gcm&soc_src=90ec6248-c094-11e5-afaa-fa163e2c24a6&.tsrc=notification-brknews (Yahoo! com)]
***At [[Governor of Texas|Governor]] [[Greg Abbott]]'s request,  President Trump issues a disaster proclamation for the state, freeing up federal aid for the worst-affected areas. [https://www.nytimes.com/2017/08/25/us/hurricane-harvey-texas.html (''The New York Times'')]
***[[Tornado]]es are reported in [[Katy, Texas|Katy]] and [[Missouri City, Texas]].  This has not been officially confirmed by the [[National Weather Service]]. [http://www.chron.com/news/houston-weather/hurricaneharvey/article/Harvey-impacts-Texas-Latest-tropical-weather-11953474.php (''Houston Chronicle'')]
***Downtown [[Houston]] has collected about {{convert|3.5|in|cm}} of rain with {{convert|7|in|cm}} recorded in some areas. Nearly {{convert|9|in|cm}} was tallied in [[First Colony]] early this morning. The National Hurricane Center predicts up to {{convert|30|in|cm}} of rain and isolated totals as high as {{convert|40|in|cm}} by the time the storm leaves. [https://www.dallasnews.com/news/weather/2017/08/26/harvey-downgraded-tropical-storm-danger-far (''The Dallas Morning News'')] [http://www.chron.com/news/houston-weather/hurricaneharvey/article/Hurricane-Harvey-rainfall-amount-Houston-area-11959445.php (''Houston Chronicle²'')]
