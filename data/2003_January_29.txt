*A false rumor that [[Thailand|Thai]] actress [[Suvanant Kongying]] had told a reporter that the temple ruins at [[Angkor]] really belong to [[Thailand]] led to a riot in [[Phnom Penh]], [[Cambodia]]. This caused the destruction of the Thai Embassy and dozens of Thai-owned businesses, hotels and factories.
