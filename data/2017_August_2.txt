*[[Impeachment in the Philippines]]
**Two impeachment complaints are filed in the [[House of Representatives of the Philippines|House of Representatives]] against [[Chief Justice of the Supreme Court of the Philippines|Supreme Court Chief Justice]] [[Maria Lourdes Sereno]], citing (among others) culpable violation of the [[Constitution of the Philippines|Constitution]] and betrayal of public trust. Sereno was appointed Chief Justice in 2012, replacing the late [[Renato Corona]] who was [[Impeachment of Renato Corona|impeached as Chief Justice]] the same year on similar charges.
