*[[2014 pro-Russian unrest in Ukraine]]:

**Washington accuses Moscow of fomenting unrest in the east, with Obama threatening the possibility of applying additional sanctions on Russia.[https://www.reuters.com/article/2014/04/24/us-ukraine-crisis-idUSBREA3L11A20140424 (Reuters)]
