* [[Space Shuttle Columbia disaster|Space Shuttle Columbia incident]]: The [[Space Shuttle Columbia]] was reported to have disintegrated over [[Texas]] on its final approach to a landing after its [[STS-107|28th space mission]].  All seven crew members were killed in the disaster.  It was later discovered that a piece of foam insulation had broken off the external fuel tank and punctured a hole in the left wing during launch.
* A crowded [[passenger train]] and a [[freight train]] collided head on and burst into flames in northwestern [[Zimbabwe]], killing 40 people and injuring about 60.  The wreckage was still burning 14 hours after the collision.
* Seven high school students from Calgary, Alberta, Canada, were killed when an avalanche swept over them on a school ski trip. The remaining ten members of the ski trip survived.
