;Law and crime
*[[2011 Helmand Province incident]]
**Sergeant Alexander Blackman, also known as Marine A, a [[Royal Marines|Royal Marine]] who shot dead a wounded [[Taliban]] insurgent in 2011 during [[Operation Herrick]], is released from [[HM Prison Erlestoke]] after serving more than three years of a seven-year sentence. [http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-39740931 (BBC)]
*Richard Vallières, convicted of stealing 3,000 tonnes of [[maple syrup]] from a [[Federation of Quebec Maple Syrup Producers]] warehouse in 2012, is sentenced to eight years in prison and fined $9.4 million, adjustable to fourteen years on non-payment of the fine. [http://www.cbc.ca/news/canada/montreal/richard-vallieres-sentencing-1.4090811 (CBC)]
