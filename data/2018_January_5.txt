;Science and technology
*[[Ozone depletion]]
**[[NASA]] research, published in ''[[Geophysical Research Letters]]'', implies that the [[Ozone layer#Depletion|ozone layer]] is starting to recover due to man's actions. The decline in ozone-depleting chemicals, specifically [[chlorine]] from [[chlorofluorocarbon]] (an effect from the 1980's [[Montreal Protocol|CFC ban]], say the scientists), has resulted in 20 percent less depletion since 2005. [http://www.newsweek.com/nasa-hole-earths-ozone-layer-finally-closing-humans-did-something-771922 (Newsweek)]
