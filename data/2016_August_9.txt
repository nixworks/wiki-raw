**A raging [[Wildfire|wildfire]] destroys dozens of homes and prompts the evacuation of more than 400 people from [[Portugal]]'s [[Madeira]] island as flames spread near the island's capital, [[Funchal]]. [[Presidents of the Regional Government of Madeira|Regional President]] [[Miguel Albuquerque]] says one person had suffered serious burns and scores of others had needed treatment for the effects of smoke.
