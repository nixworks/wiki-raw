Many residents express their concerns over safety and effectiveness; they suggest other preventive methods of [[mosquito control]]. Officials said the measures could cost as much as $1.2 million. [http://www.aljazeera.com/news/americas/2012/08/2012815224318379485.html (Al Jazeera)] [http://www.latimes.com/news/nation/nationnow/la-na-nn-dallas-west-nile-spray-20120816,0,607038.story (''Los Angeles Times'')] [http://www.dallasnews.com/news/community-news/dallas/headlines/20120815-aerial-spraying-for-west-nile-to-start-at-10-tonight-in-dallas-park-cities.ece (Dallas News)]
