* Nearly 40,000 [[manuscript]]s and 700 [[Artifact (archaeology)|artifact]]s belonging to the [[National Museum of Iraq]] in [[Baghdad]] are recovered by U.S. Customs agents working with museum experts in [[Iraq]].
