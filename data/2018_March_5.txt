*[[Politics of Italy]]
**Italy faces a period of political instability after the results of the [[Italian general election, 2018|general election]] are almost complete. The [[Populism|populist]] [[Five Star Movement]] (M5S) is projected to be the largest party in the [[Italian Parliament]] with 32% of the vote. The party has long stated its hostility towards forming a [[coalition government]].
