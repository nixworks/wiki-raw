*[[Executive Order 13780|United States immigration ban]]
**A U.S. federal judge in [[Hawaii]], finding the revised federal immigration/refugee executive order violates the U.S. Constitution's [[Establishment Clause|religious establishment clause]], grants the state of Hawaii's motion for a nationwide temporary restraining order on these rules that were set to become effective at midnight. President [[Donald Trump]] vows to appeal this ruling. Two other federal courts held hearings on similar requests filed by the states of [[Maryland]] and [[Washington (state)|Washington]]; neither of these judges issued a ruling.
