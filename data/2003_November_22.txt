* In [[Tbilisi]], [[Georgia (country)|Georgia]], opponents of President [[Eduard Shevardnadze]] seize the parliament building and demand the president's resignation. Opposition leader [[Mikhail Saakashvili]] vows to "trample" the country's leadership following unrest over disputed election results. Shevardnadze denounces what he calls an attempted [[coup d'état|coup]] and declares a [[state of emergency]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/europe/3227878.stm|title=BBC NEWS - Europe - Georgia 'on verge of revolution'|publisher=|accessdate=22 December 2015}}</ref>
