Bush]], U.K. [[Prime Minister of the United Kingdom|Prime Minister]] [[Tony Blair]], and Spanish [[Politics of Spain|Prime Minister]] [[José María Aznar]] meet in the [[Azores]], Portugal for a summit on the [[Iraq disarmament crisis]].
