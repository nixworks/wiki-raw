* [[Iraqi resistance|Violence in Iraq]]:

* The [[International Maritime Bureau]] says that deaths due to [[piracy]] doubled in the first month of 2004 compared with the same period in 2003, to 30 people. Half of the killings were in [[Nigeria]]n waters. Despite the increased violence, the total number of piracy attacks fell. In the economically critical [[Straits of Malacca]] however, attacks rose by a third. [http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/world/asia-pacific/3925277.stm (BBC)]
