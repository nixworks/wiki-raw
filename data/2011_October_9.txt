;Disasters
*A [[Moment Magnitude Scale|magnitude 4.3]] earthquake hits the southern coast of [[El Hierro]] in the southwestern [[Canary Islands]] of [[Spain]], the strongest such earthquake to occur in an [[earthquake swarm]] on the island. The quake is believed to have been caused by rising [[magma]], which has forced evacuations amid fears of an impending [[volcanic eruption]], which last occurred on El Hierro in 1793.
