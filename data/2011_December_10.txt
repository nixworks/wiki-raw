*[[New Zealand general election, 2011|New Zealand general election]] and [[New Zealand voting system referendum, 2011|voting system referendum]]:
**Official results for the 26 November general election confirm [[John Key]]'s [[New Zealand National Party|National Party]] holding a plurality of the votes and seats. With [[confidence and supply]] agreements with [[ACT New Zealand]] and [[United Future New Zealand|United Future]] confirmed, National forms a [[minority government]] with a one-seat majority, returning the [[Fifth National Government of New Zealand|Fifth National Government]] to a second term in office.
