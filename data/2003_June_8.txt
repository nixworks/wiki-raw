* The presence of the [[monkeypox]] [[Virus (biology)|virus]] in the United States is confirmed with four cases in [[Wisconsin]], sparking the first discovery of the virus in the Western Hemisphere. Dozens of suspected cases have appeared across three [[Midwest]] states, where pet enthusiasts came into contact with infected domestic [[prairie dog]]s, which caught the disease from the [[The Gambia|Gambian]] giant [[rat]].
