* A gunman opens fire at the [[Turkey|Turkish]] [[Turkish Council of State|Council of State]], the top court in [[Ankara]], while the court is in session, injuring four [[judge]]s, and killing one - [[Mustafa Yücel Özbilgin]]. [http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/europe/4989034.stm (BBC)]
