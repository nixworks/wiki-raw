*Aftermath of the [[2017 cyberattacks on Ukraine]]
**The [[United Kingdom]] [[Cabinet of the United Kingdom|government]] and the [[United States]] [[White House]] accuse the [[Military of Russia|Russian military]] of being responsible for the launch of the ''[[Petya (malware)|NotPetya]]'' malware in June 2017. Russia denies responsibility and dismisses the accusation as "groundless", lacking evidence, and "Russophobic".
