*[[Convention on preventing and combating violence against women and domestic violence]] (Istanbul Convention)
**[[Prime Minister of Bulgaria]] [[Boyko Borissov]] says that [[GERB]], his party, will not ratify the convention due to lack of support from political parties. The treaty was designed by the [[Council of Europe]] to combat [[domestic violence]] and [[violence against women]] but critics say its language is vague, it could encourage youth to identify as [[transgender]] or [[Third gender|third sex]] and it could lead to [[same-sex marriage in Bulgaria]].  [https://www.reuters.com/article/us-bulgaria-treaty/bulgarias-ruling-party-shuns-treaty-opposing-violence-against-women-due-to-lack-of-support-idUSKCN1FZ0FJ (Reuters)]
