* After 60&nbsp;mm (2.4&nbsp;in) of rain in two hours, severe [[flash flooding]] at [[Boscastle]] in [[Cornwall]], UK, results in buildings, roads, and over 50 cars swept away. Flood waters race through town at speeds up to 65&nbsp;km/h (40&nbsp;mph). Many have to leave their homes; helicopters airlift 150 people to safety. [http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/uk_news/england/cornwall/3571844.stm (BBC)] [http://www.reuters.co.uk/newsPackageArticle.jhtml?type=topNews&storyID=566587&section=news (Reuters)]
* [[NASA]]/[[ESA]] [[Spacecraft]] [[Cassini–Huygens]] discovers two new [[natural satellite]]s of [[Saturn]]. They are provisionally named "S/2004 S 1" (later named "[[Methone (moon)|Methone]]") and "S/2004 S 2" (later named "[[Pallene (moon)|Pallene]]"). [http://saturn.jpl.nasa.gov/news/press-releases-04/20040816-pr-a.cfm (NASA)]
