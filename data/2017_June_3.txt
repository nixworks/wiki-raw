*[[June 2017 London attacks]]
**A van travelling at approximately 50&nbsp;mph ploughs into pedestrians on [[London Bridge]]; the three occupants then abandon the van and embark on a stabbing spree in [[Borough Market]] before being shot dead by officers of the [[Metropolitan Police Service|Metropolitan Police]]. At least six civilians are killed. [http://www.bbc.com/news/uk-40146916 (BBC)] [http://www.cbc.ca/news/world/london-bridge-police-respond-incident-1.4145144 (CBC)]

*[[Lesotho general election, 2017]]
**Voters in [[Lesotho]] go to the polls for an election, with a tight race predicted between the [[Democratic Congress]], led by current [[List of Prime Ministers of Lesotho|Prime Minister]] [[Pakalitha Mosisili]], and the [[All Basotho Convention]], led by former prime minister [[Tom Thabane]]. [http://www.aljazeera.com/news/2017/06/lesotho-voting-election-2012-170603060926252.html (Al Jazeera)]
*[[Maltese general election, 2017]]
**Voters in [[Malta]] go to the polls for a snap election, with current [[Prime Minister of Malta|Prime Minister]] [[Joseph Muscat]] and his [[Labour Party (Malta)|Labour Party]] facing off against [[Forza Nazzjonali]], led by [[Simon Busuttil]]. The election comes amid corruption allegations leveled against Muscat's associates. [https://www.reuters.com/article/us-malta-election-idUSKBN18U0CW (Reuters)]
