*[[Nola (rhinoceros)|Nola]], a 41-year-old female [[Northern white rhinoceros|northern white rhino]], dies at the [[San Diego Zoo Safari Park]]. She was euthanized after her health took a turn for the worse. Nola was rescued from [[poaching#In Sub-Saharan Africa|violent poaching in the southern savanna woodlands]] of [[Sudan]] and came to the park in 1989. Only three animals of this [[endangered species]] are known left in the world.
