*[[Macedonia naming dispute]]
**[[Prime Minister of Macedonia]] [[Zoran Zaev]] states that his country is ready to add a geographical qualifier to its name in order to end the dispute. He also says that his government renamed the country's [[International Airport Skopje|main airport]] and a [[M-1 motorway (Republic of Macedonia)|key highway]].
