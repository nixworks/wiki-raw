* [[Cameroon]]ian [[soccer]] player [[Marc-Vivien Foé]] collapsed during a [[Confederations Cup]] semi-final between [[Cameroon]] and [[Colombia]] in [[Lyon]], France. He subsequently died after doctors failed to resuscitate him. He played for [[Olympique Lyonnais]] and was loaned to [[Manchester City F.C.]] in the 2002/03 season.<ref>[http://fifa.com/en/display/mrel,70423.html ]{{dead link|date=December 2015}}</ref>
* The [[Supreme Court of the United States|U.S. Supreme Court]] issues opinions in ''[[Lawrence v. Texas]]'', ruling 6–3 that a ban on [[homosexuality|gay]] sex is an unconstitutional violation of the [[Fourteenth Amendment to the United States Constitution|Fourteenth Amendment]].  The decision reverses the court's ruling 17 years ago which upheld the [[sodomy law|anti-sodomy laws]].<ref>[https://www.washingtonpost.com/wp-dyn/articles/A35196-2003Jun26.html ]{{dead link|date=December 2015}}</ref>
* Under pressure from members of Congress and human rights activists, officials in the administration of United States President [[George W. Bush]] publicly pledge for the first time that the United States will not torture terrorism suspects.<ref>[https://www.washingtonpost.com/wp-dyn/articles/A37460-2003Jun26.html ]{{dead link|date=December 2015}}</ref>
