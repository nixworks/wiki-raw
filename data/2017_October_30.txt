**[[Paul Manafort]] and [[Rick Gates (political consultant)|Rick Gates]] surrender to the [[Federal Bureau of Investigation|F.B.I.]] after both are indicted on multiple charges stemming from the investigation into the [[Donald Trump presidential campaign, 2016|Donald Trump presidential campaign]]'s possible associations with the Russian government.
