[http://www.reuters.com/article/us-mexico-quake-idUSKBN0U02T620151217 (Reuters)] [http://earthquake.usgs.gov/earthquakes/eventpage/us100047zq#general_summary (USGS)]

Thirty-three miners escaped. [http://www.mining.com/hope-fades-for-19-trapped-coal-miners-in-china/ (MINING.com)]

The [[State Administration of Work Safety]] reports 13 miners died at the site while four died at the hospital. Another ten miners are hospitalized. [http://news.xinhuanet.com/english/2015-12/18/c_134929584.htm (Xinhua)] [https://www.washingtonpost.com/world/asia_pacific/17-die-in-chinese-mine-accident/2015/12/17/1e98a6d2-a52f-11e5-8318-bd8caed8c588_story.html (AP via ''The Washington Post'')]

;International relations
* [[China]] summons Kaye Lee, the [[Embassy of the United States, Beijing|U.S. deputy chief of mission]] in [[Beijing]], to protest [[Federal government of the United States|Washington]]'s announcement that it is selling two warships to [[Taiwan]] as part of a $1.83 billion arms deal. U.S. state department spokesman [[John Kirby (admiral)|John Kirby]] says the sale is consistent with the [[Taiwan Relations Act|1979 Taiwan Relations Act]] while Chinese [[Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China|Vice-Foreign Minister Zheng Zequang]] threatens to impose sanctions on U.S. firms because the proposed sale "severely damaged China's sovereignty and security interests." [http://www.nbcnews.com/news/china/china-summons-u-s-envoy-over-1-83b-arms-sale-n481646 (NBC News)] [http://www.bbc.com/news/world-us-canada-35115507 (BBC)]

[http://www.reuters.com/article/us-mideast-crisis-islamic-state-un-idUSKBN0U030P20151217 (Reuters)] [http://news.yahoo.com/un-unanimously-adopts-resolution-targeting-finances-203644810.html (AFP via Yahoo News)]

Afghanistan becomes the 164th WTO member and is the 36th [[least developed country]] (LDC) in the global trade body. [http://www.globalpost.com/article/6707307/2015/12/17/afghanistans-wto-membership-approved-after-11-years-talks (Xinhua News Agency via Global Post)]  [http://www.channelnewsasia.com/news/business/afghanistan-joins-world/2357102.html (Channel News Asia)]
* The [[United Nations General Assembly]] passes a resolution condemning [[North Korea|North Korea's]] human rights abuses and calls on the [[United Nations Security Council|Security Council]] to refer violations to the [[International Criminal Court]]. [http://www.upi.com/Top_News/World-News/2015/12/18/UN-adopts-resolution-condemning-North-Korea-human-rights-violations/4051450461987/?spt=hs&or=tn_int (UPI)] [http://www.japantimes.co.jp/news/2015/12/18/asia-pacific/u-n-adopts-motion-north-korea-right-abuses-urging-criminal-court/#.VnQ0EcArI6V (''The Japan Times'')]
