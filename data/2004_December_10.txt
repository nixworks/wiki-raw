* [[Israel]]i troops kill Rania Siam, an 8-year-old [[Palestinian people|Palestinian]] girl, as she eats lunch in the kitchen of her home in [[Khan Yunis]], [[Gaza Strip]]. Earlier, three mortar shells are fired into the nearby [[Israel]]i settlement of Neve Dekalim injuring four people, one of them a child. Hamas claims responsibility. Israeli troops fire in the general direction the source of [[mortar (weapon)|mortar fire]]. The Israeli army says it will investigate Rania Siam's death.
