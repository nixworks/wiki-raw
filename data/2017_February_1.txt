*According to a report released by [[Malegapuru William Makgoba]], 94 mentally ill patients in [[South Africa]] died from starvation, dehydration and diarrhea between March and December 2016 because of a "reckless" [[Government of South Africa|government]] attempt to save money by transferring the patients from a specialized institution to care centres with "invalid licenses".
