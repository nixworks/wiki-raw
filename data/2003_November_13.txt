* A British court rejects a request by the Russian government for [[extradition]] of [[Akhmed Zakayev]], an envoy of the [[Chechnya|Chechen]] rebels, who is accused of being a [[terrorism|terrorist]] and having committed a number of crimes including kidnapping, murdering Russian soldiers, and levying war. The request was denied on the grounds that Mr. Zakayev was considered likely to be tortured if he was extradited, which would make such deportation illegal under article 3 of the [[European Convention on Human Rights]]. The prosecution's evidence was described as a "farce" by one BBC reporter, and reminiscent of [[Soviet Union|Soviet]]-era show trials.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/uk/3266325.stm|title=BBC NEWS - UK - Court rejects Chechen extradition|publisher=|accessdate=22 December 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/uk/3269385.stm|title=BBC NEWS - UK - UK accused of hypocrisy on terror|publisher=|accessdate=22 December 2015}}</ref><ref>http://newsfromrussia.com/main/2003/11/13/51210.html</ref>
