*[[North Korea and weapons of mass destruction]]
**American and South Korean officials confirm that North Korea has fired a test missile from the country's west coast. The projectile flew for 30 minutes and 435 miles/700 kilometers before landing in the sea of Japan. The firing of the test missile comes a couple of days after [[Moon Jae-in]] was [[South Korean presidential election, 2017|elected]] president of South Korea.
