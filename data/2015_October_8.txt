*[[Fiat Chrysler Automobiles]] (FCA) and the [[United Automobile Workers|United Auto Workers]] (UAW) reach a new tentative four-year labor agreement, averting a threatened strike of the automaker’s [[United States|U.S.]] operations. If ratified, the UAW intends to use the pact as a template for negotiations with [[General Motors|General Motors Co.]] and [[Ford Motor Company]].
