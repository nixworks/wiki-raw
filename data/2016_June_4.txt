;Science and technology
*Scientists report, in the [[American Heart Association|AHA]] journal [[Stroke (journal)|''Stroke'']], that a small trial of [[stroke]] victims  showed significant improvement following injection of [[stem cell]]s  directly into their brains. The study had been designed just to test whether the highly experimental therapy was safe. Such treatments were [[Organ transplantation in China|available in China]] for many years but treatment results were very inconclusive. [http://www.techinsider.io/using-stem-cells-to-treat-stroke-symptoms-2016-6 (Tech Insider)] [http://stroke.ahajournals.org/content/early/2016/06/02/STROKEAHA.116.012995.abstract (''Stroke'')]
