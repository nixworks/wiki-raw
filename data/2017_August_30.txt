*[[Fast radio burst]]s
**Researchers at the [[Breakthrough Listen]] initiative announce they have detected 15 new powerful [[fast radio burst]]s (FRB) from [[FRB 121102]], located in a [[dwarf galaxy]] about three billion light-years from [[Earth]]. FRB 121102 was first detected in 2012, and gained further attention after FRBs were detected from there again in 2015.
