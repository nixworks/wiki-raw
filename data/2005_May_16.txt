* [[May 2005 unrest in Uzbekistan|Unrest in Uzbekistan]]: [[Uzbekistan|Uzbek]] soldiers seal off the town of [[Qorasuv]] after locals take over government buildings. The government denies giving an order to fire at protesters but they do not let journalists or the [[Red Cross]] visit the affected areas to evaluate the situation. Phone and internet access has been cut. There are varying reports of at least 700 people dead and continuing firefights in [[Andijan]] and other towns like [[Teshiktosh]]. [[Saidjahon Zaynabitdinov]], head of the local human rights advocacy group ''Appeal'', says that government troops had killed 200 in [[Pakhtabad]] and expects mass arrests. Opposition supporters and human rights campaigners rally in the capital [[Tashkent]]. Hundreds of [[refugee]]s have fled over the border of [[Kyrgyzstan]]; they believe that the death toll may be in thousands.
