*[[2018 Inter-Continental Hotel Kabul attack]]
**Three gunmen kill at least 18 people, 4 [[Afghanistan|Afghans]] and 14 foreigners, in the 13-hour gun battle. The [[Ministry of Interior Affairs (Afghanistan)|Afghan Interior Ministry]] says that the siege ended when the last gunman was shot dead. [[NATO]] reports that "no foreign troops" died. The [[Taliban]] claim that they deployed 5 suicide bombers armed with hand grenades and AK-47 assault rifles. The [[Government of Afghanistan|Afghan government]] blames the attack on the [[Haqqani network]].
