*[[2017 Atlantic hurricane season]]
**[[Hurricane Maria]]
***[[President of the United States|U.S. President]] [[Donald Trump]] waives the [[Merchant Marine Act of 1920|Jones Act]] for [[Puerto Rico]] after he first disallowed additional aid, citing shipping industry business interests. Senator [[John McCain]] says the Jones Act makes prices for food, water, and other emergency supplies too high in Puerto Rico. [http://www.newsweek.com/puerto-rico-hurricane-maria-donald-trump-jones-act-relief-aid-672778 (''Newsweek'')]
*[[Ring of Fire]]
**More than 134,000 people on the island of [[Bali]], [[Indonesia]], have been taken to shelters as the [[Mount Agung]] [[volcano]]  continues to release smoke. The alert level for an [[volcanic eruption|eruption]] has been raised to the second highest level, 4. In addition, the entire population of [[Vanuatu]]'s [[Aoba Island]], also at alert level 4, is being moved from the path of the increasingly active [[Lombenben]] volcano. [http://www.news.com.au/travel/travel-updates/incidents/a-massive-volcano-threatens-to-erupt-at-any-moment-but-aussie-tourists-in-bali-arent-worried/news-story/1eea13358b91530c7a8f1b8e1253c37a (news.com.au)] [http://www.cnn.com/2017/09/28/asia/volcano-evacuations-bali-vanuatu/index.html (CNN)]
