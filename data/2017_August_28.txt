*[[Crime in Germany]]
**Investigations concerning [[Niels Högel]], a German nurse already convicted for murdering two of his patients, revealed that the number of people killed while in his care is significantly higher, reaching at least 84, with some estimates being even higher. This makes Högel the [[serial killer]] with the highest known victim count in modern German history. [https://www.nytimes.com/2017/08/28/world/europe/niels-hogel-german-nurse-killed-at-least-86-patients-officials-say.html (''New York Times'')]
