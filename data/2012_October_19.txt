*[[2011–2012 conflict in Lebanon]]:
**A [[car bomb]] explodes at [[Sassine Square]] in the [[Lebanon|Lebanese]] capital of [[Beirut]], killing at least eight people and wounding up to 78 others. [http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-middle-east-20006389 (BBC)]
**[[Wissam al-Hassan]], head of [[Saad Hariri|Saad al-Hariri]]'s security team, [[Assassination of Wissam al-Hassan| is killed]] in the blast. Al Hassan was known for being the prime investigator in the Michel Samaha case, where [[Michel Samaha]] was caught smuggling bombs into Lebanon upon orders from the Syrian government to kill Lebanese officials between 2005 and 2008, starting with the [[Assassination of Rafic Hariri|murder]] of [[Rafic Hariri|Rafik al-Hariri]].[http://www.aljazeera.com/news/middleeast/2012/10/20121019154940460986.html (Al Jazeera)]
**The [[March 14th Alliance]] blames Syrian President [[Bashar al-Assad]] and the Syrian government for the attack. [http://english.alarabiya.net/articles/2012/10/19/244748.html (''Al Arabiya'')]

;Disasters and accidents
*A [[tour bus]] went off the highway and crashed in northwest Arizona late Friday at around 8:00 PM PDT, killing the [[bus driver]] (who was believed to have suffered a medical incident), and leaving at least four of the passengers with serious injuries. About 45 other passengers were hurt less seriously, and some were not hospitalized. The bus was northbound on Highway 93 near [[Willow Beach, Arizona]] and the Nevada state line, southeast of [[Las Vegas]]. [http://www.msnbc.msn.com/id/49485846/ns/us_news-life/ (NBC)]
