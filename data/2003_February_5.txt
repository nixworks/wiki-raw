* At the [[United Nations]] [[United States Secretary of State|US Secretary of State]] [[Colin Powell]] presents the [[Government of the United States|US government]]'s case against the [[Saddam Hussein]] government of [[Iraq]], as part of the diplomatic side of the [[2003 invasion of Iraq|U.S. plan to invade Iraq]]. The presentation includes tape recordings, [[satellite photography|satellite photograph]]s and other [[military intelligence|intelligence]] data, and aims to prove [[weapon of mass destruction|WMD]] production, evasion of [[disarmament of Iraq|weapons inspections]] and a link to [[Al-Qaida]].
* The [[International Court of Justice]] orders the United States to take "all measures necessary" to prevent the execution of three [[Mexican people|Mexican]] nationals, pending its final judgment<ref>[http://www.icj-cij.org/icjwww/ipresscom/ipress2003/ipresscom2003-09_mus_20030205.htm ]{{dead link|date=October 2015}}</ref>
* [[United States House of Representatives|Congressman]] [[Howard Coble]], of [[North Carolina]], chairman of the House committee overseeing [[homeland security]], said that the [[internment]] of [[Japanese Americans]] during [[World War&nbsp;II]] was justified, and that it had been done for "their own safety."  He was roundly criticized by [[Asian American]] members of Congress and spokespeople for Asian American organizations.
