*A total [[solar eclipse]] [[Solar eclipse of November 13, 2012|occurred]] in parts of [[Australia]] and the South [[Pacific]]. The most populous city to experience totality was [[Cairns]]. [http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/science-environment-20320667 (BBC)]
*A pinch of fine sand and dust became the first solid [[Mars|Martian]] sample deposited into the [[Sample Analysis at Mars]], the biggest instrument on [[NASA]]'s Mars rover [[Curiosity rover|Curiosity]]. The sample came from the patch of windblown material called [[Rocknest (Mars)|Rocknest]], which had provided a sample previously for mineralogical analysis by [[CheMin]] instrument. [http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2012-356 (JPL-NASA)]
