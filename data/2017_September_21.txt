* [[Protests against Rodrigo Duterte]]
**Coinciding the 45th anniversary of declaration of [[Proclamation No. 1081|1972 Martial Law]] by late [[President of the Philippines|Philippine President]] [[Ferdinand Marcos]], a nationwide protests are conducted by various groups against the government's implementation of [[Philippine Drug War|war on drugs]] and [[Proclamation No. 216|ongoing martial law]] in the whole [[Mindanao]] under the administration of President [[Rodrigo Duterte]].
