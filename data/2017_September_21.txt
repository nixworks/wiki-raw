* [[Protests against Rodrigo Duterte]]
**Coinciding with the 45th anniversary of the declaration of [[Proclamation No. 1081|martial law]] in [[1972]] by the late [[President of the Philippines|Philippine President]] [[Ferdinand Marcos]], nationwide protests are conducted by various groups against the government's implementation of the [[Philippine Drug War|war on drugs]] and the [[Proclamation No. 216|ongoing martial law]] in the whole of [[Mindanao]] under the administration of President [[Rodrigo Duterte]].
