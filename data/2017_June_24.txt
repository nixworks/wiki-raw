*[[Papua New Guinean general election, 2017]]
**Polling places open in [[Papua New Guinea]], and will remain open for two weeks, to elect all 111 members of the [[National Parliament of Papua New Guinea|National Parliament]]. The [[People's National Congress (Papua New Guinea)|People's National Congress]] led by [[Prime Minister of Papua New Guinea|Prime Minister]] [[Peter O'Neill]] is considered likely to maintain a [[Coalition government|governing coalition]], despite uncertainty due to lack of [[opinion poll]]ing.
