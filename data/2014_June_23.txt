*[[War in North-West Pakistan]]:
**[[Pakistan|Pakistani]] forces conduct airstrikes on terrorist hideouts in [[North Waziristan]], killing 25 militants. [http://www.business-standard.com/article/news-ians/25-militants-killed-in-pakistan-airstrike-114062301275_1.html (''Business Standard'')]

*[[Syrian Civil War]]:
**[[Syria]] hands over its remaining 100 tonnes of its declared [[chemical weapons]] to the [[United Nations]] [[Organisation for the Prohibition of Chemical Weapons]] but the organisation cannot declare that the country no longer has any such weapons. [https://www.reuters.com/article/2014/06/23/us-syria-crisis-chemicalweapons-idUSKBN0EY18T20140623 (Reuters)]
*[[Islamist insurgency in Nigeria]]:

*[[War in Somalia (2009–present)]]:
**[[Kenyan Air Force]] planes attack the [[al-Qaeda]]-linked [[Al-Shabaab (militant group)|al-Shabab]] killing 80 of its fighters according to the [[African Union]] force in [[Somalia]]. [http://www.bbc.com/news/world-africa-27976201#TWEET1165191 (BBC)]
*[[2014 pro-Russian conflict in Ukraine]]
**Pro-Russian insurgents agree to a ceasefire proposed by the [[Government of Ukraine]]. [http://www.sfgate.com/news/world/article/Peace-talks-begin-in-east-Ukraine-after-cease-fire-5572393.php (AP via ''San Francisco Chronicle'')]

[http://whc.unesco.org/en/news/1160 (UNESCO)]
*A painting in [[Claude Monet]]'s [[Water Lilies]] series is sold at auction in London for £31.7 million or $54 million US, the second highest price for a Monet on record. [http://www.bangkokpost.com/news/world/417025/water-lilies-painting-sells-for-54m (AFP via ''Bangkok Post'')]
