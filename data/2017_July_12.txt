*[[Human rights in China]]
**Nobel Peace Laureate [[Liu Xiaobo]], who has been incarcerated in [[China]] for organizing the pro-democracy manifesto "Charter 08," is suffering from late-stage liver cancer which is worsening. The Chinese hospital treating him and foreign doctors, who examined Liu the weekend, disagree on future treatment. [[Germany]], the [[United States]], [[United Kingdom|UK]], and other countries have appealed that Liu be allowed to go to the hospital of his choice.
