* American celebrity [[Martha Stewart]] is sentenced to five months in a federal [[prison]], five months of [[house arrest]], two years [[probation]], and ordered to pay a $30,000 fine, for attempting to cover up illegal stock trading.  The sentence is stayed pending appeal. [https://www.reuters.com/financeNewsArticle.jhtml?type=businessNews&storyID=5693645 (Reuters)]

The [[Jenin Martyrs' Brigade]] claims responsibility.  Hours later the police chief is released and another official of the Palestinian Authority kidnapped.
