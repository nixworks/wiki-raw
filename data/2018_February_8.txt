*[[LGBT rights in Bermuda]]
**[[Bermuda]] repeals [[Same-sex marriage in Bermuda|same-sex marriage]], becoming the world's first jurisdiction to legalise, then repeal same-sex marriage. However, about half a dozen same-sex marriages that took place in Bermuda between the Supreme Court ruling in May 2017 and the repeal will continue to be recognised. [https://www.theguardian.com/world/2018/feb/08/bermuda-repeal-same-sex-marriage (''The Guardian'')]
