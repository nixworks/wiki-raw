At the Pentagon, [[Harry B. Harris Jr.|Harry Harris]], the commander of U.S. forces in the Pacific, said that if China wanted to prevent consideration of the THAAD system's deployment, it should lean on Pyongyang saying, "If China wanted to exert a lot of influence on somebody to prevent THAAD from being considered going into Korea, then they should exert that influence on North Korea." *[[Austria–Greece relations]]
**[[Greece]] recalls its ambassador to [[Austria]] in response to Austria's hosting of a meeting with [[Balkans|Balkan states]], to which Greece was not invited, about [[European migrant crisis]] policies that would make it harder for [[Human migration|migrants]] to head north across [[Europe]]. [[Ministry of Foreign Affairs (Greece)|Greece's foreign ministry]] called the move an "unfriendly act." More than 100,000 migrants have entered the [[European Union|EU]] illegally so far in 2016, nearly all of them arriving in Greece. [http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-europe-35658776 (BBC)] [https://www.reuters.com/article/us-europe-migrants-greece-austria-idUSKCN0VY1OP (Reuters)]
