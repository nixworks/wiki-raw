;Health
*[[Biogen Idec]] Inc. reports that a drug in a [[phase II clinical research trial]] has achieved the first experimental demonstration of a statistically significant reduction in amyloid plaque as well as a slowing of clinical impairment in patients with [[Alzheimer's disease]]. The [[antibody]] drug, [[aducanumab]], blocked [[beta amyloid]] formation, a protein that forms toxic brain-destroying plaques, and significantly slowed cognitive decline. [http://www.reuters.com/article/2015/03/20/us-biogen-alzheimers-idUSKBN0MG1OU20150320 (Reuters)]
