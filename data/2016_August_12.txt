;Science and technology
*Scientists say [[Greenland shark|Greenland sharks]] are now known to be the longest-living [[Vertebrate|vertebrates]] on [[Earth]], after researchers at the [[University of Copenhagen]], using [[Radiocarbon dating|radiocarbon dating]], determined the ages of 28 of the animals, and estimated that one female was about 400 years old. The former vertebrate record-holder was a [[Bowhead whale]] estimated to be 211 years old. [http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/science-environment-37047168 (BBC)]
