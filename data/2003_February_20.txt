* [[The Station nightclub fire]]: A fire started by [[pyrotechnics]] set-off by [[Great White]], a [[rock band]] playing a [[nightclub]] in [[West Warwick, Rhode Island|West Warwick]], [[Kent County, Rhode Island]], kills at least 96 and injures nearly 200, with 35 in critical condition. Fatalities from burn injuries are expected to increase.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.foxnews.com/story/0,2933,79161,00.html|title=R.I. Nightclub Blaze Kills at Least 96|work=Fox News}}</ref>
* [[University of South Florida]] [[computer engineering]] professor Dr. [[Sami Al-Arian]] was arrested by the [[FBI]] after he and seven others were indicted on 50 [[terrorism]]-related charges. [[United States Attorney General]] [[John Ashcroft]] alleged at a news conference that Al-Arian is the North American leader of the [[Palestinian Islamic Jihad]].
