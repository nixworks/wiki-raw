"... **The [[Centers for Disease Control and Prevention|U.S. Centers for Disease Control and Prevention]] advises women exposed to Zika, which means traveling to [[South America]]n (or other) countries where the disease is rampant, have [[Safe sex|protected sex]] for at least two months before attempting to get [[Pregnancy|pregnant]]. Men exposed should not have unprotected sex for at least six months.
