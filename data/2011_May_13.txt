**A video shows 11 dead [[imam]]s and 45 wounded Muslim holy men, 5 of whom are in a [[coma]], alleged to have been caused by a [[NATO]] airstrike. *[[2011 Egyptian revolution]]:

**[[Suzanne Mubarak]], the wife, is detained for 15 days on allegations of corruption and has a heart attack. [http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-africa-13392099 (BBC)]
**Tens of thousands of people gather in [[Tahrir Square]] to display unity against sectarian tension and solidarity with the plight of the [[Palestinian people]] and the other popular uprisings against regimes in the region. Cheers erupt as Suzanne Mubarak is incarcerated. [http://www.guardian.co.uk/world/2011/may/13/tahrir-square-egypt-mubarak-wife (''The Guardian'')]
