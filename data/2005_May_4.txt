* [[Israeli-Palestinian Conflict]]: 
** [[Israel]] freezes the handover of [[West Bank]] [[Palestinian people|Palestinian]] towns to the [[Palestinian Authority]]. [[Israel]] claims they will resume the handover process when militant groups are disarmed. The [[Prime Minister of Israel]], [[Ariel Sharon]], had agreed to hand over five West Bank towns to Palestinian control, but thus far has only handed over [[Tulkarm]] and [[Jericho]]. [http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/middle_east/4513851.stm (BBC)]
** [[Israel]]i troops shoot dead two teenage boys.  The cousins were protesting against the building of the [[Israeli West Bank barrier]] among a group of people, many of whom were throwing stones at the soldiers. According to the [[Stop the Wall]] group, protestors were chased by Israeli soldiers using live ammunition. [http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/world/middle_east/4514783.stm (BBC)]
