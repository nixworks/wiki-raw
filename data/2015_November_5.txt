*[[Belizean general election, 2015]]
**[[List of Prime Ministers of Belize|Belizean Prime Minister]] [[Dean Barrow]] wins a record third term in Wednesday's [[Snap election|snap general election]] in the  [[Central America]]n nation as his [[United Democratic Party (Belize)|United Democratic Party]] (UDP) gains a clear majority in parliament. The UDP won 19 [[Constituencies of Belize|constituencies]] and [[Francis Fonseca|Francis Fonseca's]] [[People's United Party]] (PUP) took 10, with two constituencies yet to report. Belize is a [[Parliamentary system|parliamentary]] [[constitutional monarchy]] with Queen Elizabeth II, the symbolic head of state.  [http://www.reuters.com/article/2015/11/05/us-belize-election-idUSKCN0ST1VE20151105#hLWpFMhRXc31M0xR.97 (Reuters)] [http://europe.newsweek.com/belize-pm-dean-barrow-wins-record-third-consecutive-term-336042 (Newsweek)]
*The [[President of the Maldives]], [[Abdulla Yameen]], declares a [[state of emergency]] suspending civil rights and giving sweeping powers to security forces. [http://www.theguardian.com/world/2015/nov/04/maldives-declares-state-of-emergency (''The Guardian'')]
