*[[Regulation of chemicals]]
**Reacting to a [[European Citizens' Initiative|petition]] from more than a million [[Citizenship of the European Union|EU citizen]]s, the [[European Commission]] releases a 16-page report on [[glyphosate]]. It adopts a renewal of the approval of glyphosate for a 5-year period and plans to improve the science reviewing process. Glyphosate is a [[Herbicide#Classification|systemic herbicide]] and [[Crop desiccation|crop desiccant]] commonly sold under the [[trademark]] ''Roundup''. The petitioners fear that it would [[Causes of cancer|cause cancers]]. [http://www.lefigaro.fr/flash-eco/2017/12/12/97002-20171212FILWWW00205-la-commission-officialise-le-renouvellement-du-glyphosate.php (''Le Figaro'' and Reuters)]
