;Armed conflicts and attacks
* The [[Israel Defense Forces]] report the complete destruction of an [[Gaza Strip smuggling tunnels|underground tunnel]] dug by [[Hamas]] under the  [[Kerem Shalom crossing]]. Israel shut down the crossing before its jets bombed the tunnel opening in Gaza Saturday night. The crossing remains closed. [https://www.ynetnews.com/articles/0,7340,L-5070606,00.html (Ynetnews)],  [http://edition.cnn.com/2018/01/14/middleeast/israel-hamas-tunnel/index.html (CNN)]
*[[American-led intervention in the Syrian Civil War]]
**The [[Combined Joint Task Force – Operation Inherent Resolve|U.S.-led coalition]] confirms reports that it will form a new 30,000-strong Syrian Border Security Force (BSF). Half of the forces will be [[Syrian Democratic Forces]] veterans. Another 15,000 will be recruited and trained in the near future. [https://www.dailysabah.com/war-on-terror/2018/01/14/us-led-coalition-to-form-30000-strong-sdf-border-army (''Daily Sabah'')]
