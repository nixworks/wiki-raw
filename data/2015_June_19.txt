*The [[European Central Bank]] increased the cap on cash available to Greek banks through [[Lender of last resort|emergency liquidity assistance]], as those banks continue to experience steady withdrawals. Greek citizens have withdrawn over 3 billion Euros in the last month. [http://www.reuters.com/article/2015/06/19/us-eurozone-greece-pm-idUSKBN0OZ0DP20150619 (Reuters)]

*The centre-right opposition bloc led by ''[[Venstre (Denmark)|Venstre]]'' wins the [[Danish general election, 2015|2015 Danish general election]], even though the [[Social Democrats (Denmark)|Social Democrat]] party remains Denmark's largest. The anti-immigration [[Danish People's Party]] will be the second-largest in parliament. [http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-europe-33171549 (BBC)] [http://www.usnews.com/news/world/articles/2015/06/18/left-right-are-neck-and-neck-as-danes-vote-in-elections (U.S. News & World Report)]
*[[Hawaii]] becomes the first U.S. state to raise the [[smoking age]] from 18 to 21, effective January 1, 2016, and will also outlaw sales, purchases, or uses of [[electronic cigarette]]s for those under 21.
