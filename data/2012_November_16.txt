*[[England and Wales Police and Crime Commissioner elections, 2012|Elections]] to choose [[Police and Crime Commissioner]]s in England and Wales are marked by voter apathy with a turnout of just 14.9%. *[[Labour Party (UK)|Labour]] wins all three by-elections contested in the UK on Thursday, including gaining [[Corby by-election, 2012|Corby]] from the [[Conservative Party (UK)|Conservatives]]. [http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-politics-20345196 (BBC)]
