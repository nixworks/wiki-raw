*[[Economy of the United States]]
**The U.S. economy added over 200,000 jobs last month, dropping that country's [[unemployment]] rate to match a previous 16-year low at 4.3%.  Wage growth remained below the [[Federal Reserve Board of Governors|Federal Reserve Board's]] target of 3.5%. *[[Rwandan presidential election, 2017]]
**Voters in [[Rwanda]] go to the polls to elect their [[List of Presidents of Rwanda|president]].
