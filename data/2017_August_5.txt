*[[Mauritanian constitutional referendum, 2017]]
**Voters in [[Mauritania]] participate in a referendum on whether to amend the [[Constitution of Mauritania]], with the abolition of the [[Senate (Mauritania)|Senate]] and addition of red bands to the [[flag of Mauritania]] among the most significant proposed changes. The [[Office of the United Nations High Commissioner for Human Rights|OCHR]] has criticised [[Mohamed Ould Abdel Aziz]]'s government for apparent suppression of opposition protests.
