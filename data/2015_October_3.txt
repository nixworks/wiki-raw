*[[Hurricane Joaquin]]
**As Hurricane Joaquin, still a [[Saffir–Simpson hurricane wind scale#Category 3|Category 3 storm]], heads northeast,  [[Tropical cyclone warnings and watches#Tropical cyclone warnings and watches|a Tropical Storm Warning and a Hurricane Watch]] are in effect for [[Bermuda]]. Winds will begin to strengthen early Saturday with thunderstorms and more significant showers around midnight. Joaquin should turn toward the north-northeast Sunday and pass around {{Convert|125|nmi|abbr=off}} west of Bermuda that evening. (NHC)], [http://www.royalgazette.com/article/20151002/NEWS/151009905 (''Bermuda Royal Gazette'')]
