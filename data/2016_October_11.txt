Authorities charged her with first-degree manslaughter. [http://www.foxnews.com/us/2016/10/11/autopsy-oklahoma-police-victim-had-pcp-in-system-when-died.html (AP via Fox News)]

*Astronomers announce that [[2014 UZ224]] is the third most distant [[Solar System]] object known from our [[Sun]], currently over 90 times the Earth's distance from the Sun (90.8 [[Astronomical unit|AU]] or 8.44 billion miles), after [[Eris (dwarf planet)|Eris]] (96.2 AU) and [[V774104]] (~103 AU).
