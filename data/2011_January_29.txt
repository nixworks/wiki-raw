*[[2011 Egyptian protests]]:
**The protests continue as [[Egyptian Army]] tanks surround [[Cairo]]'s [[Tahrir Square]]. Medical aid is given at the doors of [[mosque]]s. [http://edition.cnn.com/2011/WORLD/africa/01/29/egypt.interior.ministry.scene/ (CNN)]

**A [[curfew]] is extended from 16:00 until 8:00 Egyptian time according to state television. [http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/world/middle-east/Egypt-curfew-hours-extended-State-TV/articleshow/7385124.cms (''The Times of India'')]
**[[Suzanne Mubarak]], the wife of [[President of Egypt|President]] [[Hosni Mubarak]] and [[First Lady]] of Egypt, is reported to have fled to [[London]]. [http://www.ynetnews.com/articles/0,7340,L-4020764,00.html (Ynetnews)]
