* [[Vanuatu]] [[Prime Minister of Vanuatu|Prime Minister]] [[Serge Vohor]] is ousted in a vote of no-confidence and replaced by [[Ham Lini]]. Vohor had fallen out of favor with his cabinet after he unilaterally travelled to [[Taipei]] and established [[diplomatic relations]] with the [[Republic of China|Taiwan]]. The Vanuatu Council of Ministers voted to void the decision and continue relations with the People's Republic of China.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.chinadaily.com.cn/english/doc/2004-12/11/content_399473.htm|title=Vanuatu gov. reshuffled after Taiwan controversy|work=chinadaily.com.cn|accessdate=4 October 2015}}</ref>
* The opposition [[pan-blue alliance]] achieves a majority in the [[ROC legislative election, 2004|Taiwanese legislative election]], garnering at least 114 of the 225 seats. The governing [[Democratic Progressive Party]] and its [[pan-green alliance|pan-green allies]] win 101 seats.<ref>{{cite news|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/world/asia-pacific/4085503.stm|title=BBC NEWS - Asia-Pacific - Opposition wins Taiwan elections|work=bbc.co.uk|accessdate=4 October 2015}}</ref><ref>[http://www.guardian.co.uk/print/0,3858,5083883-103681,00.html Victory for Taiwan opposition]</ref><ref>[http://www.chinapost.com.tw/detail.asp?ID=55500&GRP=A (''China Post'')]</ref>
* Doctors in Austria confirm that [[Ukraine|Ukrainian]] [[Ukraine presidential election, 2004|presidential candidate]] [[Viktor Yushchenko]]'s illness was caused by [[Polychlorinated dibenzodioxins|dioxin]] poisoning. It is alleged that he first became ill after dining with the chairman of the [[Ukrainian Security Service]] in early September 2004.<ref>{{cite news|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/health/4041321.stm|title=BBC NEWS - Health - Yushchenko and the poison theory|work=bbc.co.uk|accessdate=4 October 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://story.news.yahoo.com/news?tmpl=story&e=1&u=/ap/20041211/ap_on_re_eu/yushchenko_health|title=Yahoo News - Latest News & Headlines|work=Yahoo News|accessdate=4 October 2015}}</ref> (Link dead as of 21:43, {{Nowrap|14 January}} 2007 (UTC))
* [[Bernard Kerik]] withdraws his nomination for the post of [[Secretary of Homeland Security]], fearing a scandal over the immigration status of his [[nanny]].<ref>[http://www.nydailynews.com/front/story/261052p-223581c.html (Newsday)]</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.bloomberg.com/apps/news?pid=10000087&sid=aZt2vIKQGcA0&refer=top_world_news|title=Bernard Kerik, Bush Nominee for Homeland Security, Withdraws|work=bloomberg.com|accessdate=4 October 2015}}</ref>
* [[Jean-Christophe Lafaille]], a French climber, climbs [[Shishapangma]], the first solo ascension of an [[eight-thousander]] in winter from a new route in the South face.
