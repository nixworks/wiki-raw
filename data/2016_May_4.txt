*[[2016 Fort McMurray wildfire]]
**[[Premier of Alberta|Alberta Premier]] [[Rachel Notley]] says the wildfire has destroyed about  1,600 structures in Fort McMurray,  which is  in Canada's [[oil sands]] region. No major oil sands facilities are in the fire's path, though two [[Royal Dutch Shell]] mines are closing, and a [[Suncor Energy]] facility is reducing crude production. The fire is  threatening the [[Alberta Highway 63]] bridge across the [[Athabasca River]], the  sole route south. **The [[European Union]] proposes [[Visa policy of the Schengen Area|visa-free travel]] for [[Turkey|Turkish]] citizens within Europe's [[Schengen Area]] and invites member states and EU lawmakers to endorse the move by June 30.
