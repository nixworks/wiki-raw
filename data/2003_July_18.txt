* U.S. [[Basketball]]:  [[Eagle County, Colorado]] District Attorney Mark Hurlbert announces that [[Los Angeles Lakers]] star [[Kobe Bryant]] has been charged with one count of felony sexual assault, stemming from a June 30 incident at a gated resort involving a 19-year-old woman.

Kelly was a British government advisor involved in the ''[[September Dossier]]'' investigation relating to the [[2003 invasion of Iraq|2003 war on Iraq]]. Former Labour Junior Minister [[Glenda Jackson]] calls for [[Tony Blair|Prime Minister Blair]]'s resignation and a [[The Mail on Sunday|Mail on Sunday]] reporter asks, "Do you have blood on your hands. Prime Minister?"
