*1,427 gold coins from the mid-19th century that were buried in eight cans are discovered by an anonymous couple while walking their dog in [[Gold Country]], California. The find dubbed the [[Saddle Ridge Hoard]] is expected to be worth over $10 million dollars (USD) and is the largest known [[hoard]] of gold coins ever found in the United States.[https://www.usatoday.com/story/news/nation/2014/02/25/california-gold-coins-buried-treasure/5817179/ (USA Today)]
