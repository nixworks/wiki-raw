*[[2018 Alaska earthquake]]
**A 7.9 [[Moment magnitude scale|magnitude]] earthquake occurs in the [[Gulf of Alaska]]. A [[Tsunami warning system|tsunami warning]] is issued for coastal [[Alaska]] and [[British Columbia]], and the entire [[West Coast of the United States|U.S. West Coast]] is placed under a tsunami watch. Areas of Alaska remain under a tsunami advisory. It is tied as the [[List of earthquakes in the United States|sixth-largest earthquake]] ever recorded in the [[United States]], but there are no reports of significant damage or fatalities. [http://news.sky.com/story/tsunami-warning-after-huge-alaska-quake-11219220 (Sky News UK)] [https://www.cbsnews.com/news/tsunami-warning-for-much-alaska-coast-follows-earthquake-live-updates/ (CBS News)] [https://earthquake.usgs.gov/earthquakes/eventpage/us2000cmy3#executive (USGS)]
