* A case of [[Bovine Spongiform Encephalopathy|BSE]] ("mad cow disease") in a single [[cow]] in [[Alberta]] is confirmed by Canadian federal and provincial officials. The animal had been destroyed and declared unfit for consumption prior to being diagnosed. The US issues a temporary ban on all Canadian beef. This is the first North American case of BSE since one in 1993 involving an animal born in Britain.
* [[DARPA]]'s Congressional report announces that the controversial [[Information Awareness Office|Total Information Awareness]] program will be known as the [[Terrorist Information Awareness]] program from now on, to emphasize that its purpose is to compile data on terrorists, and not to compile dossiers on US citizens.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://w2.eff.org/Privacy/TIA/20030523_tia_report_review.php |title=EFF Review of May 20 Report on Total Information Awareness |postscript=<!--None-->}}</ref>
