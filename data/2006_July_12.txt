* [[Israeli-Palestinian conflict]]
** An [[Israel]]i air strike destroys the Palestinian Foreign Ministry Building in [[Gaza City]]. [http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/middle_east/5175002.stm (BBC)], [http://www.forbes.com/technology/feeds/ap/2006/07/12/ap2875344.html (AP)]
** An [[Israel]]i [[brigade]] enters the central [[Gaza Strip]] via [[Israeli Gaza Strip barrier#Overview|Kissufim crossing]], aiming at temporarily bisecting it. Simultaneously, the [[Israel Air Force]] targets a meeting of [[Hamas]] [[Izz ad-Din al-Qassam Brigades|operational wing]] commanders in an apartment building in [[Gaza Strip|Gaza]] city. One Hamas leader, seven members of his family and one neighbour are killed. Top Hamas leaders [[Mohammed Deif]] and Abu Anas al-Ghandour, who Israeli officials claim were heavily involved in the kidnapping of Cpl. [[Gilad Shalit]], are moderately wounded. Fourteen additional [[Hamas|Palestinian militants]] are killed in other incidents in the [[Gaza Strip]]. [http://www.haaretz.com/hasen/spages/737324.html (Haaretz)], [http://www.ynetnews.com/articles/0,7340,L-3274212,00.html (Ynet)], [http://today.reuters.com/news/newsArticle.aspx?type=worldNews&storyID=2006-07-12T122502Z_01_L12236669_RTRUKOC_0_US-MIDEAST-GAZA.xml (Reuters)]
** [[2006 Israel-Lebanon crisis]]:
*** [[Hezbollah]] militants kidnap two [[Israel]]i soldiers patrolling along the northern Israel border. Three soldiers are killed in the incident, and five soldiers are killed in subsequent confrontations inside [[Lebanon]]. Hezbollah demands the release of thousands of [[Palestinian people|Palestinian]] prisoners and one [[Lebanon|Lebanese]] prisoner, the killer of two small girls and their father. [http://www.haaretz.com/hasen/spages/737634.html (Haaretz)], [http://www.ynetnews.com/articles/0,7340,L-3274258,00.html (Ynet)], [http://iafrica.com/news/specialreport/middleeast/677341.htm (iAfrica)] It is later reported that the two Israeli soldiers "had trespassed into Lebanon's side of the border with Israel". [http://www.atimes.com/atimes/Middle_East/HG15Ak02.html (Asia Times)] [http://english.bna.bh/?ID=47348 (Bahrain News)] [http://www.whatreallyhappened.com/israeli_solders.html (What Really Happened.com)]
*** Simultaneously, [[Hezbollah]] militants launch [[Katyusha rocket launcher|Katyusha]] rockets and [[Mortar (weapon)|mortar]] shells at Israeli towns along the border, wounding six Israeli civilians and five soldiers. [http://www.haaretz.com/hasen/spages/737634.html (Haaretz)], [http://www.ynetnews.com/articles/0,7340,L-3274258,00.html (Ynet)], [http://iafrica.com/news/specialreport/middleeast/677341.htm (iAfrica)]
*** Israeli forces attack installations and [[Hezbollah]] positions in [[Lebanon]], in failed attempt to thwart the transportation of the kidnapped soldiers from the area. [http://www.haaretz.com/hasen/spages/737634.html (Haaretz)], [http://www.ynetnews.com/articles/0,7340,L-3274258,00.html (Ynet)], [http://iafrica.com/news/specialreport/middleeast/677341.htm (iAfrica)]

The [[Politics of Syria|Syrian government]] and [[Hamas]] praise the attack by Hezbollah.
