;Armed conflicts and attacks
*[[Syrian civil war]]:
**The [[Syrian Army]] is reportedly preparing for a large-scale offensive in [[Aleppo]], with the [[US State Department]] citing credible reports of tank columns moving into the city, backed by special forces, helicopter gunships and fixed-wing aircraft. [http://www.aljazeera.com/news/middleeast/2012/07/20127261861988152.html (Al-Jazeera)]
***The pro-government newspaper ''[[Al-Watan (Syria)|Al-Watan]]'' calls the incoming operation 'the mother of all battles'. [http://www.aljazeera.com/news/middleeast/2012/07/20127261861988152.html (Al-Jazeera)]
**At least 200 people are killed in violence throughout the country according to the [[Local Coordination Committees of Syria]]. The toll is highest in Aleppo and the capital [[Damascus]], where a total of 94 are killed. Dozens of casualties are reported from [[Idlib]], [[Daraa]] and [[Homs]] as well. [http://edition.cnn.com/2012/07/26/world/meast/syria-unrest/index.html?hpt=hp_t1 (CNN)]
**Ikhlas al Badawi, [[Aleppo Governorate|Aleppo]] [[People's Council of Syria|MP]], a [[Arab Socialist Ba'ath Party – Syria Region|Baathists]] and an Assad loyalist, [[List of Syrian defectors|defects]] to [[Turkey]] with her six children. [http://www.albawaba.com/news/clashes-continue-syrian-mp-defects-435755 (Albawaba)] [http://www.reuters.com/article/2012/07/27/us-syria-crisis-defection-idUSBRE86Q04K20120727 (Reuters)]

*A bomb explosion in a crowded market in [[northwestern Pakistan]] close to the Afghan border kills at least seven people. [http://www.usatoday.com/news/world/story/2012-07-26/Pakistan-bomb/56493862/1 (USA Today)]
*Insurgents attack an [[Iraqi Army]] checkpoint north of [[Baqubah]], downing a helicopter and sparking an intense firefight. At least 19 people are reported killed, including 11 policemen and a soldier. [http://www.sfgate.com/news/article/19-killed-as-Iraqi-forces-clash-with-militants-3736302.php (San Francisco Chronicle)]
