* One of the holiest sites in [[Jerusalem]], known to Jews as the [[Temple Mount]] and to Muslims as the [[Haram al-Sharif]] or the ''Noble Sanctuary'', is re-opened to controversy. Jerusalem's police chief, Mickey Levey says the decision was taken before the most recent suicide bombing. However the decision is condemned by the [[Grand Mufti of Jerusalem]], Sheikh Ekrima Sabri, who says the re-opening was done without the agreement of the [[Waqf]], the Muslim authority that oversees the site. Palestinians from outside Jerusalem who are under the age of 40 are currently barred from entering.
