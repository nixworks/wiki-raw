;International relations
*[[Zapad 2017 exercise]], [[Belarus–Russia relations]]
**[[Russia]] and [[Belarus]] begin a six-day joint strategic [[military exercise]] inside Belarus and Russia's [[Kaliningrad Oblast]]. The military exercise simulates war against the fictional state of [[Veyshnoria]]. According to the [[Defence Ministry of Belarus]], fewer than 13,000 personnel will be taking part in the exercise; however, Western analysts believe the total number will range from 60,000 to 100,000. [https://www.reuters.com/article/us-russia-nato/russias-zapad-war-games-unnerve-the-west-idUSKCN1BO1OG (Reuters)]
