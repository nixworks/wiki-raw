*[[USS Fitzgerald and MV ACX Crystal collision|USS ''Fitzgerald'' and MV ''ACX Crystal'' collision]]
**The [[USS Fitzgerald|USS ''Fitzgerald'']], a [[United States Navy]] destroyer, [[USS Fitzgerald#Accidents and incidents|collides]] with a merchant ship [[MV ACX Crystal|MV ''ACX Crystal'']] off the coast of [[Japan]] at around 2:30 a.m. local time, with seven American sailors reported missing. The ship was taking on water and able to operate at limited propulsion. [http://edition.cnn.com/2017/06/16/politics/us-navy-destroyer-collides-with-merchant-ship-japan/index.html (CNN)]
*[[Climate of Chile]]
**Four people are killed as intense rains and snowstorms hit central and southern [[Chile]]. About 3,000 people are homeless, and hundreds of thousands have lost power. [http://www.reuters.com/article/us-chile-storm-idUSKBN1980UP (Reuters)]
*[[2017 Portugal wildfires]]
**Wildfires in central [[Portugal]] kill at least 24 people and several [[firefighter]]s are among 20 injured. The death toll so far is the largest due to a wildfire in Portugal since 1966. [http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-europe-40316934 (BBC)]
