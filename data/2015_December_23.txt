;Health
*[[2015 Middle East respiratory syndrome outbreak in South Korea|South Korean MERS outbreak]]
**[[South Korea]] announces an end to the [[Middle East respiratory syndrome]] (MERS) outbreak that has killed 36 people since May 2015. The virus infected 186 people, with nearly 17,000 people confined to their homes and thousands of schools temporarily closing at the peak of the largest MERS outbreak outside [[Saudi Arabia]], where it first appeared in 2012. [http://www.france24.com/en/20151223-south-korea-announces-official-end-mers-outbreak?ns_campaign=reseaux_sociaux&ns_source=twitter&ns_mchannel=social&ns_linkname=editorial&aef_campaign_ref=partage_aef&aef_campaign_date=2015-12-23&dlvrit=65413 (AFP via FRANCE 24)]
