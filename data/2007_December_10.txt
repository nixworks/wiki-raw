* Subtropical Storm Olga forms north of [[Puerto Rico]], ten days after the official end of the [[2007 Atlantic hurricane season]]. The [[National Hurricane Center]] forecasts landfall in the [[Dominican Republic]] within 24 hours, and Tropical Storm Warnings and Watches have been posted for the northern shore of the nation. [http://www.guardian.co.uk/worldlatest/story/0,,-7142371,00.html (''The Guardian'')]
* The [[United States]] [[Supreme Court of the United States|Supreme Court]] decides that federal district judges have broad discretion to impose what they think are reasonable sentences, even if federal guidelines call for different sentences. The case that they ruled on involved [[crack cocaine]]. [https://www.nytimes.com/2007/12/11/washington/11scotus.html (''The New York Times'')]
* Former [[Atlanta Falcons]] quarterback [[Michael Vick]] receives a 23-month jail sentence for [[Bad Newz Kennels dog fighting investigation|his involvement in a dogfighting ring]]. [https://www.tsn.ca/nfl/news_story/?ID=224839&hubname= (Canadian Press via TSN)] 
* Former media tycoon [[Conrad Black]] receives a 78-month jail sentence in the [[United States]] for [[mail fraud]] and [[obstruction of justice]]. [http://edition.cnn.com/2007/US/law/12/10/black.sentence/index.html (CNN)]
* [[Russia]]n [[President of Russia|President]] [[Vladimir Putin]] announces at a party meeting that he will support [[Dmitry Medvedev]] as his successor in the [[Russian presidential election, 2008|March 2008 presidential election]]. [http://news.smh.com.au/russias-putin-anoints-deputy-premier-as-presidential-frontrunner/20071210-1g8m.html (''The Sydney Morning Herald'')]
* Hard rock band [[Led Zeppelin]] reunites in London's [[The O2 Arena|O2 Arena]].
