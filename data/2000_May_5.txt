* A rare [[Conjunction (astronomy and astrology)|conjunction]] occurs on the [[New Moon]] including all seven of the traditional [[celestial bodies]] known from [[ancient]] times up until 1781 with the discovery of [[Uranus]]. The conjunction consists of the [[Sun]] and [[Moon]], [[Mercury (planet)|Mercury]], [[Venus]], [[Mars]], [[Jupiter]], and [[Saturn]].([http://nssdc.gsfc.nasa.gov/planetary/alignment.html NASA])
