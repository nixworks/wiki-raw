*[[2017 French Open]]
**In [[tennis]], [[Jeļena Ostapenko]] of [[Latvia]] defeats [[Simona Halep]] of [[Romania]] 4–6, 6–4, 6–3 in the [[2017 French Open – Women's Singles|Women's Singles]] of the [[French Open]]. It is Ostapenko's first [[Women's Tennis Association|WTA Tour]] title, making her the first woman to earn her first tour victory at a [[Grand Slam (tennis)|Grand Slam]] event since [[Barbara Jordan (tennis)|Barbara Jordan]] at the [[1979 Australian Open – Women's Singles|1979 Australian Open]]. She also becomes the first [[Latvians|Latvian]] of either sex to win a Grand Slam event and the first unseeded woman to win the French Open since [[1933 French Championships – Women's Singles|1933]]. [https://www.washingtonpost.com/news/early-lead/wp/2017/06/10/jelena-ostapenko-storms-back-to-stun-simona-halep-for-improbable-french-open-title/?wpisrc=al_alert-sports&wpmk=1 (''The Washington Post'')] [http://www.espn.com/tennis/story/_/id/19597442/jelena-ostapenko-wins-french-open-first-grand-slam-title (ESPN)]
