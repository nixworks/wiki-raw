* A report submitted today to the State of [[Maryland]] states that the [[electronic voting]] [[voting machine|machine]]s made by [[Diebold Election Systems]] "have such poor computer security and physical security that an election could be disrupted or even stolen by corrupt insiders or determined outsiders". The machines have been purchased by a number of states in the United States.<ref>http://www.nytimes.com/2004/01/29/technology/29CND-SECU.html</ref>  This is the third report to state that the machines do not meet the security requirements of an election. Previous reports are available online.<ref>[http://www.dbm.maryland.gov/dbm_search/technology/toc_voting_system_report/votingsystemreportfinal.pdf ]{{dead link|date=December 2015}}</ref><ref>[http://avirubin.com/vote/ ]{{dead link|date=December 2015}}</ref>
* [[Hutton Inquiry]]: The [[BBC]] Director-General, [[Greg Dyke]], resigns in the continuing fall-out from the report. Mr Dyke is the second high-ranking BBC official to resign. [[Mark Byford]] is appointed Acting Director-General.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/uk_politics/3441181.stm|title=BBC NEWS - UK - Politics - BBC apologises as Dyke quits|publisher=|accessdate=22 December 2015}}</ref> The UK media in general condemns the report as a whitewash.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://media.guardian.co.uk/huttoninquiry/story/0,13812,1134025,00.html|title=Press rounds on Hutton|work=the Guardian|accessdate=22 December 2015}}</ref>
* The [[Al-Aqsa Martyrs' Brigades]] of [[Fatah]] claim responsibility for a [[suicide bombing]] aboard a city bus, in which [[Ali Yusuf Jaara]], a member of the [[Palestinian National Authority|Palestinian]] police force, kills 10 [[Israelis]] and wounds more than 50, outside the Prime Minister's residence in [[Jerusalem]]. Simultaneously with the bombing, [[Shaul Mofaz]], Israeli Defense Minister, is meeting with American envoys Wolfe and Sauterfield, who have requested an easing-up of conditions for the Palestinians. The explosion also coincides with a German-brokered prisoner swap between Israel and the Lebanese [[guerrilla warfare|guerrilla]] group [[Hezbollah]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.haaretz.com/hasen/spages/388486.html|title=IDF Conducting Search and Arrest Operation in Bethlehem|work=Haaretz.com|accessdate=22 December 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.israelnn.com/news.php3?id=56993|title=Israel News - Israel's #1 News Site - Arutz Sheva|work=Arutz Sheva|accessdate=22 December 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.dunnconnect.com/articles/2004/01/28/ap/Headlines/d80chhb00.txt|title=Dunn County News|author=Chippewa Herald|publisher=|accessdate=22 December 2015}}</ref>
