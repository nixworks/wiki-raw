*[[2014 Israel-Gaza conflict]]
**Four Arab-Israeli taxi drivers are injured, two seriously, after they came under mortar and rocket attack at the [[Erez]] border crossing while waiting to transport critically injured [[Palestinians]] for treatment at [[Israel]]i hospitals. Due to warnings by the [[Israel Defense Forces|IDF]] to evacuate the building, there were no fatalities in the strike. [http://www.timesofisrael.com/day-49-barrage-of-rockets-before-midnight-hamas-head-says-no-return-to-talks/ (Times of Israel)]
