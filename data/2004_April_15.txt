[[diplomat]]s state that [[Nuclear weapon|nuclear]]-related equipment, some [[contamination|contaminated]], and a number of [[missile]] engines have been [[smuggle]]d out of [[Iraq]] for [[recycle|recycl]]ing in [[Jewometaal Stainless Processing B.V.]] [[scrap yard]]s.
