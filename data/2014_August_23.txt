;Armed conflicts and attacks
*[[2014 Israel-Gaza conflict]]
**Four additional Palestinians charged with collaborating with Israel are publicly executed outside of the [[Jabalia]] [[Jabalia Camp|refugee camp]] by [[Hamas]]; the killings are denounced by the secretary-general of the [[Palestinian National Authority|PA]], Tayeb Abdel Rahim, who compared them to [[Islamic State of Iraq and the Levant|ISIS]]. [http://www.timesofisrael.com/pa-official-says-hamas-executions-like-isis/ (Times of Israel)]
**The [[Israel Defense Forces|IDF]] retracts claims that the mortar shell that killed an Israeli child was fired near or from a school run by [[UNRWA]], saying that the shell was fired from a school used as a shelter run by Hamas. [http://www.theguardian.com/world/2014/aug/23/israel-admits-mortar-bomb-not-fired-un-school (The Guardian)]
*[[2014 Libyan conflict]]

**[[Tripoli International Airport]] is captured by Islamist militias following days of clashes with nationalist militias. [http://news.yahoo.com/libya-islamist-militias-taken-tripoli-airport-165106137.html;_ylt=AwrBEiLc3_hTABEAck7QtDMD (AFP via Yahoo! News)]
*[[Iraqi insurgency (2011–present)]]:
**The [[United Nations|UN]] calls for action to prevent what it suspects may become a massacre in the northern Iraqi town of [[Amirli]], which has been besieged by [[Islamic State of Iraq and the Levant|IS]] forces since June. [http://www.bbc.com/news/world-middle-east-28910674 (BBC)]
**At least 30 people are killed in bombings in [[Baghdad]] and [[Kirkuk]]. [http://news.sky.com/story/1323435/blasts-in-iraq-as-un-warns-of-town-massacre (Sky News)]
