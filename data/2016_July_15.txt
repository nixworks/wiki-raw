*[[2016 Nice attack]]
**The [[President of France]] [[François Hollande]] extends a [[state of emergency]] against terrorism for three months following the attack which has now claimed over 80 lives. He had previously announced that it would be lifted on 26 July. [http://sputniknews.com/europe/20160715/1043001482/hollande-emergency.html (Sputnik News)], [http://www.bbc.com/news/world-europe-36800730 (BBC News)]
**Authorities identify the attacker as a 31-year-old [[Tunisia]]n resident in France, who was killed during the exchange of gunfire with police. The man was known to police in connection with petty crime, but was not on the  [[Directorate-General for External Security|French intelligence service]] watch list. [https://www.reuters.com/article/us-france-crash-idUSKCN0ZU2K7 (Reuters)] [http://www.bbc.com/news/world-europe-36801763 (BBC)]
*[[2016 Turkish coup d'état attempt]]
**The [[Prime Minister of Turkey]] [[Binali Yıldırım]] calls for "calm" over what he states was an "illegal attempt" by sections of the [[Turkish military]] to claim power. [http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/2016/07/15/turkey-low-flying-jets-and-gunfire-heard-in-ankara1/ (''The Telegraph'')]
**The Turkish military claims to have gained control of the government with gunfire reported on the streets of [[Ankara]]. [https://www.cnbc.com/2016/07/15/gunfire-heard-jets-seen-flying-in-turkish-capital-of-ankara.html (CNBC)]
**The [[President of Turkey]] [[Recep Tayyip Erdoğan]] urges supporters to oppose the [[coup d'etat]] by taking to the streets. [https://www.washingtonpost.com/news/world/wp/2016/07/15/turkish-president-rejects-coup-attempt-urges-supporters-to-take-to-the-streets/?wpisrc=al_alert-COMBO-world%252Bnation (''Washington Post'')]
**The death toll from the coup is reported to be at least 42 in [[Ankara]] and 60 across Turkey. 130 soldiers have been taken into custody and one [[Turkish Army]] general has been killed. [http://news.trust.org/item/20160716024151-60u31 (Reuters via Trust)], [http://www.nbcnews.com/storyline/turkey-military-coup/attempt-coup-turkey-prime-minister-says-n610531?cid=eml_nbn_20160715 (NBC News)]
