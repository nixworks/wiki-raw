**The [[United States Environmental Protection Agency|U.S. Environmental Protection Agency]] orders [[Volkswagen]] to fix nearly 500,000 VW and [[Audi]] [[Diesel engine|diesel]] cars from model years 2009-2015 that include software that circumvents EPA [[Emission standard|emissions standards]].
