* In the [[United Kingdom|UK]], flash flooding severely affected several [[North Yorkshire]] villages and towns, including [[Thirsk]], [[Helmsley]] and [[Hawnby]], when the rivers [[River Swale|Swale]] and [[River Rye, Yorkshire|Rye]] burst their banks.
* In [[Formula One]] the [[2005 United States Grand Prix|United States Grand Prix]] sparked controversy, when only three teams on [[Bridgestone]] tyres competed because of [[Michelin]] not being able to supply safe tyres to the remaining seven teams running on the French company's rubber.
