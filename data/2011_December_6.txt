*Afghanistan terror attacks
**A [[2011 Afghanistan terror attacks|suicide attack]] attacks a [[Shiite]] shrine in the [[Afghanistan|Afghan]] capital [[Kabul]] during the festival of [[Day of Ashura|Ashura]], resulting in at least 50 people killed and hundreds injured. Hours later another attack takes place at [[Mazar-i-Sharif]], killing at least 4. [http://www.reuters.com/article/2011/12/06/us-kabul-blast-idUSTRE7B50C320111206?feedType=RSS&feedName=worldNews (Reuters)], [http://www.guardian.co.uk/world/2011/dec/06/kabul-shrine-blast-kills-worshippers (''The Guardian'')], [http://news.ninemsn.com.au/world/8385613/blast-rocks-kabul-shrine-many-feared-dead (AP via NineMSN)]
**The [[President of Afghanistan]] [[Hamid Karzai]] cancels a trip to the [[United Kingdom]] following the attacks. [http://www.theaustralian.com.au/news/world/karzai-returns-to-afghanistan-as-suicide-bomb-blast-toll-climbs-to-59/story-e6frg6so-1226215707400 (AP via ''The Australian'')]
