* British forces step up their presence in the southern [[Iraq]] city of [[Basra]]. According to embedded journalists, the citizens of Basra braved gunfire to dance in the streets and cheer for the British troops. [[UPI]]'s Chief International Correspondent Martin Walker claimed that he had witnessed at least one Basra citizen kiss a British tank.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.upi.com/view.cfm?StoryID=20030407-025947-4642r |title=The Anglo-American alliance wins again |publisher=UPI.com |date=2003-04-07 |accessdate=2015-09-26}}</ref>
* In a [[friendly fire]] incident, U.S. warplanes struck a convoy of allied [[Kurds|Kurdish]] fighters and U.S. Special Forces during a battle in northern [[Afghanistan]]. At least 18 people are killed and more than 45 wounded, including senior Kurdish commanders.
