*[[Mauritanian constitutional referendum, 2017]]
**Results from Saturday's constitutional referendum show [[Mauritania|Mauritanian]]s have voted to abolish the [[Senate of Mauritania]]. Turnout was 53.73%, with 85% of voters supporting the change, according to officials. [http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-africa-40847092 (BBC)]
