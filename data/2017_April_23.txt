*[[French presidential election, 2017]]
**Voters in France go to the polls to elect a new [[President of France|President]]. Opinion polls predict that the two [[Major party|major parties]]' candidates will receive fewer votes than [[Emmanuel Macron]] of the centrist [[En Marche!]] and [[Marine Le Pen]] of the far-right [[National Front (France)|National Front]], and that a [[Two-round system|second round]] will be needed, to be held on 7 May.
