* [[2008 South Ossetia war]]
** Russian President [[Dmitry Medvedev]] ordered the Defense Ministry, law enforcement agencies and the Foreign Ministry to investigate a bombing in the South Ossetian capital, [[Tskhinvali]], on 3 October, 2008. A car, carrying weapons, was detained by Russian peacekeepers in Georian village and transported to Tskhinvali, where it exploded. [[European Union|EU]] and [[Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development]] earlier condemned this 'act of terrorism'. Georgian authorities claimed 'Russian special services' 'were behind' the blast that left 7 servicemen of Russian peacekeeping forces dead.
