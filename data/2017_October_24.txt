*The [[European Commission]] scraps the draft legislation that would have permitted the [[European Banking Authority|EBA regulator]] to order "[[too big to fail]]" banks to split off their [[Trader (finance)|trading activities]], citing "no foreseeable agreement" in sight on criteria. The draft was supposed to be the [[European Union|EU]]'s answer to the [[United States]]' [[Volcker Rule]].
