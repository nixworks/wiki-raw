;Business and economy
*Citing "no foreseeable agreement" in sight on criteria, the [[European Commission]] scraps the draft legislation that would have permitted the [[European Banking Authority|EBA regulator]] to order "[[too big to fail]]" banks to split off their [[Trader (finance)|trading activities]]. The draft was supposed to be the [[European Union|EU]]'s answer to the [[United States]]' [[Volcker Rule]]. [http://www.reuters.com/article/us-eu-banks-regulations/eu-scraps-its-answer-to-u-s-volcker-rule-for-banks-idUSKBN1CT285?il=0 (Reuters)]
