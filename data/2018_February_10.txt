*[[February 2018 Israel–Syria incident]]
**The [[Israel Defense Forces|Israeli Army]] says that it shot down a Syrian [[UAV]] manufactured in [[Iran]] and, in response, it bombed the [[Tiyas Military Airbase]] in [[Homs Governorate]], Syria, where it was launched from.  [https://www.reuters.com/article/us-israel-iran/israel-shoots-down-iranian-drone-strikes-iranian-targets-in-syria-army-idUSKBN1FU07L (Reuters)]
**The [[Syrian Air Defense Force]] shoots down an [[Israel|Israeli]] [[General Dynamics F-16 Fighting Falcon|F-16]] over the [[Golan Heights]] after it fired on alleged [[Iran|Iranian]] targets inside [[Syria]]. Both pilots parachute to safety. [http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-middle-east-43014081 (BBC)] [https://www.rferl.org/a/israel-iran-syria-fighter-jet-shot-down/29031815.html (RFERL)]
**Syrian state media cite a military source saying that the Syrian air defences hit "more than one plane" at dawn. [https://www.reuters.com/article/us-israel-iran-jet-syria/syrian-state-tv-says-syrian-air-defences-hit-israeli-jets-idUSKBN1FU08M (Reuters)]
*[[Turkish military operation in Afrin]]
**The [[Kurds in Syria|Kurdish]] [[People's Protection Units|YPG]] shoots down a [[Turkish Air Force]] [[TAI/AgustaWestland T129 ATAK|T129 ATAK]] helicopter over Syria's [[Afrin District]], killing both pilots. [http://www.aljazeera.com/news/2018/02/turkish-troops-killed-helicopter-downed-180210131736070.html (Al Jazeera)]
