**Three explosions occur near the [[Borussia Dortmund]] football team bus as it slowed down to turn a corner. Spanish player [[Marc Bartra]] was injured and has been taken to hospital. Dortmund's [[2016–17 UEFA Champions League|UEFA Champions League]] fixture against [[AS Monaco FC]] is subsequently postponed.
