*[[Operation Car Wash]], [[Corruption in Ecuador]]
**Suspended [[Vice President of Ecuador|Ecuadorian Vice President]] [[Jorge Glas]] is sentenced to six years imprisonment for the delict of illicit association and taking $13.5m (£10.2m) in [[Graft (politics)|bribes]] from Brazilian construction company [[Odebrecht]]. Odebrecht paid $33.5m in bribes to secure infrastructure contracts in the country. The uncle of Glas, involved in the same plot, received the same sentence. However, the 48-year-old engineer, imprisoned preventively since October, will not be able to be totally stripped of his functions until all judicial remedies have been exhausted. Former President Rafael Correa has previously said that he believes the case is politically motivated.
