*[[Operation Car Wash]], [[Corruption in Ecuador]]
**[[Vice President of Ecuador|Ecuadorian Suspended Vice President]] [[Jorge Glas]] is sentenced to six years imprisonment for the delict of illicit association and taking $13.5m (£10.2m) in [[Graft (politics)|bribes]] from Brazilian construction company [[Odebrecht]], which paid $33.5m in bribes to secure infrastructure contracts in the country. Former President [[Rafael Correa]], who also had Glas in the same office, has defended him expressly and considers that the case is politically motivated. The uncle of Glas, involved in the same plot, received the same sentence.
