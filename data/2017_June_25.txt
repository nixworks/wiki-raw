;Disasters and accidents
*More than 120 people have died and 130 were injured when an oil tanker truck exploded in [[Bahawalpur]] city of [[Pakistan]]. The accident occurred when the truck fell off the road it was traveling on Sunday morning and as per the sources the driver ran away. People from surrounding villages gathered around the truck to fill their bottles and buckets with the spilling oil. The oil caught fire due to which many people caught fire and then the oil tanker exploded causing many casualties and third degree burn.
