;Business and economy
*[[Economy of the United States]]
**[[New York State]]-based [[GateHouse Media]] announces an agreement to buy 11 U.S. newspapers from [[Georgia (U.S. state)|Georgia]]-based [[Morris Communications]]. Included in the deal is the ''[[Log Cabin Democrat]]'', founded in 1879, and three Alaskan newspapers: the ''[[Juneau Empire]]'', the ''[[Peninsula Clarion]]'' and ''[[Homer News]]''. The deal is expected to close on October 2. [http://www.adweek.com/digital/gatehouse-media-morris-communications/ (AdWeek)]
**[[DineEquity]] announces it will close 160 [[Applebee's]] and [[International House Of Pancakes|IHOP]] restaurants. [https://www.usatoday.com/story/money/2017/08/10/many-160-applebees-and-ihop-locations-close/556343001/ ''(USA Today)''] 

;Disasters and accidents
*The United Nations [[International Organization for Migration]] reports, over the last two days in two incidents, smugglers have thrown 280 migrants from [[Ethiopia]] and other [[Horn of Africa]] countries into the sea off the coast of [[Yemen]]. More than 50 refugees have drowned with over 30 people still missing. [http://www.cbsnews.com/news/smugglers-throw-280-migrants-into-the-sea-off-yemen-u-n-says/ (AP via CBS News)] [http://www.aljazeera.com/news/2017/08/200-refugees-thrown-yemen-sea-50-missing-170810104006798.html (Al Jazeera)]
*At least 36 people die in a bus crash in [[People's Republic of China|China]]'s [[Shaanxi]] province. The bus was travelling between [[Chengdu]] and [[Luoyang]]. [http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/2017/08/11/least-36-people-dead-bus-crash-china/ (''The Telegraph'')]

**The Canadian government says at least one of their diplomats in [[Havana]] has been treated for hearing loss, following up on yesterday's report that American diplomats had similar injuries.
