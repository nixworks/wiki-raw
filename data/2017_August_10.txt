*Business and economy
**[[New York State]] based [[GateHouse Media]] announces an agreement to buy 11 U.S. newspapers from [[Georgia (U.S. state)|Georgia]]-based [[Morris Communications]]. Included in the deal is the [[Log Cabin Democrat]], founded in 1879. Also included are three Alaskan newspapers; the [[Juneau Empire]], the [[Peninsula Clarion]] and [[Homer News]]. The deal is expected to close on October 2. [http://www.adweek.com/digital/gatehouse-media-morris-communications/ (AdWeek)]
