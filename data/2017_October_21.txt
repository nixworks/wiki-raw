**The Prime Minister of Spain, [[Mariano Rajoy]], activates Article 155 of the [[Spanish Constitution of 1978|Constitution of Spain]]. The regional police and media will also be under its control. [https://www.nytimes.com/2017/10/21/world/europe/spain-catalonia-mariano-rajoy.html (The New York Times)]
