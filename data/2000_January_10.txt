;Arts and culture
*The [[Tama Center Station#Tama Toshi Monorail (Tama-Center Station)|Tama Toshi Monorail (Tama-Center Station)]] opens in [[Tokyo]], [[Japan]]. This is a raised station with two tracks and two opposed side platforms, with the station building located underneath. It is a standardized station building for this monorail line.

;Business and economy
*[[America On-line]] announces an agreement to buy [[Time Warner]] for $162 billion, becoming the largest-ever corporate merger.

;Disasters and accidents
*[[Crossair Flight 498]]
**[[Crossair]] suffers the first fatal accident in its 25-year history when Crossair Flight 498 crashes in [[Niederhasli]], [[Switzerland]], after take off from [[Zurich]], killing all 10 people on board.
*The [[Lady Franklin Point]] catches fire and is almost totally destroyed.
