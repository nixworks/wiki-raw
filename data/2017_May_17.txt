*[[Central African Republic Civil War (2012–present)|Central African Republic Civil War]]
**According to the [[International Committee of the Red Cross]], at least 115 human bodies are discovered in the border town of [[Bangassou]], more than previously thought. A few days prior, militias attacked the town, with the [[United Nations]] reporting that at least 26 people were killed. *[[War in Afghanistan (2015–present)|War in Afghanistan]]
**Four militants attack a state television building in [[Jalalabad]], killing at least six people. The militants are also killed after engaging with [[Afghan National Police|Afghan police]]. [[Islamic State of Iraq and the Levant – Khorasan Province|ISIL]] claimed responsibility for the attack. [http://www.bbc.com/news/world-asia-39946711 (BBC)]

*[[Ukraine–European Union relations]]
**Representatives of the [[European Parliament]] and [[European Council]] sign a document formally granting [[Ukraine|Ukrainians]] visa-free travel to the [[European Union]]. The visa-free regime is due to enter into force on June 11. [https://www.rferl.org/a/eu-ukraine-visa-free-travel-deal-signed/28492872.html (Radio Free Europe/Radio Liberty)]
*Two [[People's Liberation Army Air Force|Chinese aircraft]] intercept a [[Boeing WC-135 Constant Phoenix|US WC-135 aircraft]] over the [[East China Sea]]. [http://www.bbc.com/news/world-asia-china-39971267 (BBC)]

;Law and crime
*[[United States Army]] soldier [[Chelsea Manning]] is released from prison after serving seven years. [https://www.theguardian.com/us-news/2017/may/17/chelsea-manning-released-from-prison (''The Guardian'')]
