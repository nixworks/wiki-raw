*Scott N. Johansen, a [[Utah]] [[Juvenile Court]] judge, reverses his original order to remove a foster child from same [[LGBT Parenting|sex-parents]].   The state Division of Child and Family Services and foster parents all filed motions asking for the reconsideration and promising an appeal.  The new decision eliminates the phrase, "It is not in the best interest of children to be raised by same-sex couples" and strikes an order for the child to be placed with a non-same-sex couple. [https://www.nytimes.com/2015/11/14/us/utah-lesbian-couple-foster-child-ruling.html (New York Times)]
