**[[Battle of Aden (2018)]]
***The [[Southern Transitional Council]] (STC) takes control of the [[Yemen]]i port city of [[Aden]] following two days of fierce fighting in the city which has left dozens dead. Yemeni Prime Minister [[Ahmed Obeid bin Daghr]], and members of the [[President of Yemen|President]] [[Abdrabbuh Mansur Hadi]]-led government are confined to the presidential palace which has been surrounded.
