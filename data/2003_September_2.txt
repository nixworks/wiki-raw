<!-- All news items below this line -->{{Current events header|2003|09|02}}
* [[Al-Qaeda]] leader [[Osama bin Laden]] says that his organization is working on "''serious projects''", and that his priority is to use [[Biological warfare|biological weapons]] against the United States. Al Qaeda may already have such weapons, and be seeking means to transport and launch them.
* [[Indonesia]]: An Indonesian court sentences Muslim cleric [[Abu Bakar Bashir]] to four years in prison for treason against the Indonesian government. However, the court found insufficient proof that Bashir was the leader of the militant Islamic organization [[Jemaah Islamiyah]].<ref>[http://www.pbs.org/newshour/updates/indonesia_09-02-03.html ]{{dead link|date=October 2015}}</ref>
* [[Iraq]]: A [[car bomb]] explodes near the headquarters of Coalition trained police in [[Baghdad]], killing an Iraqi [[police officer]] and wounding up to 10 bystanders.<ref>[http://www.iht.com/articles/108526.html ]{{dead link|date=October 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.foxnews.com/story/0,2933,96174,00.html|title=Car Bomb Attack at Baghdad Police Headquarters|work=Fox News|accessdate=17 October 2015}}</ref><ref>[http://www.cnn.com/2003/WORLD/meast/09/02/sprj.irq.main/index.html ]{{dead link|date=October 2015}}</ref><ref name="cbsnews.com">{{cite web|url=http://www.cbsnews.com/stories/2003/02/24/iraq/main541815.shtml|title=Iraqi PM Appeals For Reconciliation|date=24 February 2003|publisher=|accessdate=17 October 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.msnbc.com/news/870749.asp?0cv=CA01|title=MSNBC|work=MSNBC|accessdate=17 October 2015}}</ref><ref>[http://www.csmonitor.com/newsinbrief/brieflies.html#WORLD6:44:36 ]{{dead link|date=October 2015}}</ref> Observers saw the incident as an attempt to destabilize the new [[Iraq]]i governing body. (See [[2003 occupation of Iraq timeline]])
* The [[Iraq]]i police handling the investigation say they have arrested 19 men in connection with the blast, many of them foreigners and all with admitted links to [[al-Qaeda]].<ref name="cbsnews.com"/>
* [[Astronomy]]: Astronomers announce the discovery of an asteroid ([[2003 QQ47|2003 QQ<sub>47</sub>]]) whose orbit has a remote chance of striking earth.
* ''[[SCO v. IBM]]'': SCO Germany is ordered to pay fine of 10,000 [[Euro]] because they were ordered to cease their allegations that Linux contains stolen intellectual property of SCO.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://slashdot.org/articles/03/09/02/1237244.shtml?tid=123&tid=99|title=SCO Fined in Munich For Linux Claims - Slashdot|publisher=|accessdate=17 October 2015}}</ref>
* [[Road map for peace]]: [[Israel]]i [[Defense Minister]] [[Shaul Mofaz]] states he favors expelling [[Palestinian people|Palestinian]] [[President]] [[Yasser Arafat]] by the end of the year, because Arafat is obstructing the United States led peace plan.  Arafat denies the statement and says he backs the peace plan (though he refuses to release control of Palestinian [[Security agency|security services]] to reformer [[Prime Minister of the Palestinian National Authority|Prime Minister]] [[Mahmoud Abbas]] to stop [[Insurgent|militant]] group attacks as mandated by the plan). Palestinian leaders call Defense Minister's remarks inflammatory.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.reuters.com/newsArticle.jhtml?type=worldNews&storyID=3371626|title=World News|publisher=|accessdate=17 October 2015}}</ref>
* [[Premier of Ontario]] [[Ernie Eves]] calls a [[Ontario general election, 2003|provincial election for October 2]]. Eves' incumbent [[Progressive Conservative Party of Ontario|Tories]], [[Dalton McGuinty]]'s [[Ontario Liberal Party|Liberals]], and [[Howard Hampton]]'s [[Ontario New Democratic Party|NDP]] are in the race.<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.cbc.ca/ontariovotes2003/|title=CBC - Ontario Votes 2003|publisher=|accessdate=17 October 2015}}</ref>
* [[Premier of Prince Edward Island]] [[Pat Binns]] calls a [[Prince Edward Island general election, 2003|provincial election for September 29]]. The incumbent [[Prince Edward Island Progressive Conservative Party|Tories]], [[Robert Ghiz]]'s [[Prince Edward Island Liberal Party|Liberals]], and [[Gary Robichaud]]'s [[Prince Edward Island New Democratic Party|NDP]] are in the race.<ref name="cbc.ca">{{cite news|url=http://www.cbc.ca/peivotes2003/|title=CBC - Prince Edward Island Votes 2003|publisher=|accessdate=17 October 2015}}</ref>
* A [[Wildfire|forest fire]] erupts in the Gorge of the [[Columbia River]] to the east of [[Cascade Locks, Oregon]], forcing the closure of a 47-mile section of [[Interstate 84 in Oregon|Interstate 84]] and the evacuation of Cascade Locks for at least one full day.
