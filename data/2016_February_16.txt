;International relations
*[[Cuba–United States relations]]
**The [[United States]] and [[Cuba]] sign an agreement restoring [[Airline|commercial air traffic]] between the two countries for the first time in 50 years. The [[United States Department of Transportation|U.S. Department of Transportation]] immediately opened bidding for as many as 110 U.S.-Cuba flights per day to [[Havana]] and nine other destinations. Travel from the U.S. is permitted in   12 authorized categories, which does not include [[tourism]]. [http://bigstory.ap.org/article/38bd18dd7e8449cebdcece9115962457/us-cuba-sign-deal-commercial-flights (AP)] [http://www.jamaicaobserver.com/news/US-authorizes-up-to-110-daily-flights-to-Cuba (AFP via ''The Jamaica Observer'')]
