*[[Charlie Hebdo shooting|''Charlie Hebdo'' shooting]]
**The two suspect brothers take a hostage at a sign printing company, Création Tendance Découverte, in the [[France|French]] town of [[Dammartin-en-Goële]]. [http://www.sbs.com.au/news/storystream/charlie-hebdo-gunshots-car-chase-near-manhunt-gunmen (AAP via SBS)]
***The standoff ends with the two brothers dead and the hostage released. [https://www.cnbc.com/id/102323454 (CNBC)] [http://www.bbc.com/news/world-europe-30752239 (BBC)] [http://edition.cnn.com/2015/01/09/europe/charlie-hebdo-paris-shooting/index.html (CNN)]
**[[Porte de Vincennes hostage crisis|A second hostage situation of unclear connection]] occurs at a Jewish market, [[Hypercacher]], in the eastern [[Paris]] suburb of [[Vincennes]]. There are two suspected hostage takers, [[Hayat Boumeddiene]], 26, and [[Amedy Coulibaly]], 32, who were previously suspected of shooting a policewoman dead earlier in the week. There are at least sixteen hostages. [http://www.heraldsun.com.au/news/world/charlie-hebdo-attack-shooting-at-paris-kosher-grocery/story-fni0xs61-1227180200292?from=herald+sun_rss (''Herald-Sun'')]
***The concurrent standoff ends with Coulibaly dead as well as four hostages. Four additional hostages and two police officers require hospitalization. Boumeddiene remains at large. [http://www.msnbc.com/msnbc/paris-suspects-cornered-hostage-charlie-hebdo (MSNBC)]
*[[2015 Baga massacre]]
**Refugees flee Nigeria's [[Borno State]] following the [[Boko Haram]] massacre in the town of Baga. 7,300 flee to neighbouring [[Chad]] while over 1,000 are trapped on the island of Kangala in [[Lake Chad]]. Nigeria's army vows to recapture the town, while [[Niger]] and [[Chad]] withdraw their forces from a [[Multinational Joint Task Force|transnational force]] tasked with combating militants. [http://www.unhcr.org/54afb6dc9.html (UNHCR)] [http://www.npr.org/blogs/thetwo-way/2015/01/09/376122497/reports-of-boko-haram-led-massacre-in-captured-nigerian-town (NPR via BBC)]

*A 193-vehicle pileup along a snowy [[Interstate 94]] highway in [[Kalamazoo County, Michigan|Kalamazoo County]], [[Michigan]] leaves one motorist dead and 20 injured.
