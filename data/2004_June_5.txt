Interior minister [[Dominique de Villepin]] states that the wedding is illegal and announces that the mayor will face censure. Mamère claims he is interpreting French law, inspired by [[same-sex marriage in the United States#2004 California|similar actions]] in [[San Francisco]] and other US cities.
