* ''[[2007 Glasgow International Airport attack|Glasgow International Airport attack]]:''
** A car on fire [[2007 Glasgow International Airport attack|crashes into the main terminal building]] of [[Glasgow Airport]]. Two people are arrested at the scene by police, with one man reportedly on fire as he is being arrested.
** The airport closes, with all plane flights cancelled and the airport evacuated.
** [[Cabinet Office Briefing Rooms|COBRA]] conducts an emergency meeting, and [[Prime Minister of the United Kingdom|Prime Minister]] [[Gordon Brown]] makes a statement afterward in which he thanks emergency services after today's attack and the [[2007 London car bombs|London car bombs plot]] yesterday.
** [[Home Secretary]] [[Jacqui Smith]] announces that the [[BIKINI state#Terrorism alerts|national security threat level]] is elevated from "severe" to "critical"&mdash;the highest possible, meaning "further attacks are imminent"&mdash;and everyone should be on "high alert."
