* [[Catalan regional election, 2017]]
** Voters in [[Catalonia]] confirm and lightly strengthen the pro-independance parties' majority within the [[Parliament of Catalonia|regional parliament]], with 70 out of 135 seats won, and a record [[Voter turnout|turnout]] of more than 80%.[https://www.theguardian.com/world/2017/dec/21/catalonia-election-set-to-give-victory-to-pro-independence-parties (''The Guardian'')]
** [[Carles Puigdemont]] and analysts declare the vote a victory for the [[Catalan declaration of independence|pro-independence movement]], and a setback for the [[Government of Spain|Spanish government]].[https://www.theguardian.com/world/2017/dec/21/catalonia-election-set-to-give-victory-to-pro-independence-parties (''The Guardian'')]
** [[Prime Minister of Spain|Prime Minister of Spain]] [[Mariano Rajoy]] and Spanish goverment's party, the [[People's Party of Catalonia]], falls from 11 to 4 seats. The unionist [[Ciutadans]] become the biggest party. [https://www.theguardian.com/world/live/2017/dec/21/catalonia-voters-results-regional-election-spain-live (''The Guardian'')]
