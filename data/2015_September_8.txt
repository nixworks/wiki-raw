*A massive sandstorm hits [[Lebanon]] and [[Syria]] as well as [[Jordan]], [[Israel]] and [[Egypt]]. Particularly hard hit were the 1.1 million Syrian refugees in Lebanon, many in flimsy tents in informal campsites.
