*[[December 2015 Hindu Kush earthquake]]
**At least 17 people are injured in [[Peshawar]], [[Pakistan]], and five people are hurt in [[Afghanistan|Afghanistan's]] [[Nangarhar Province]] after a 6.3-[[Moment magnitude scale|magnitude]] [[earthquake]] hit near the Afghanistan-[[Tajikistan]] border. The epicenter is in Afghanistan's quake-prone [[Ishkashim district]], a mountainous area where mainly impoverished farmers live in homes  made with mud, according to [[Peshawar District]] Officer Muhmand Asim Khan. Other details about damage or casualties are not presently available.
