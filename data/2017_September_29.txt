*[[Cuba–United States relations]]
**The [[United States Department of State|U.S. State Department]] removes all families of employees and nonessential personnel from Cuba following apparent [[Sonic weapon|sonic attacks]] that injured 21 [[Embassy of the United States, Havana|personnel]] on the island. Cuba denies any involvement and has authorized the [[Federal Bureau of Investigation|FBI]] to travel to the island to help the ongoing investigation into the causes. [http://www.cnn.com/2017/09/29/politics/us-cuba-sonic-attacks-pulls-out-diplomats/index.html (CNN)]
