*[[2017 Atlantic hurricane season]]
**[[Hurricane Irma]]
***Hurricane Irma strengthens to a maximum [[Saffir–Simpson scale#Category 5|Category 5 hurricane]], becoming the strongest Atlantic hurricane since 2005's [[Hurricane Wilma]] in terms of maximum sustained winds, described as "extremely dangerous." The [[National Hurricane Center]] (NHC) predicts that Irma could strengthen even more due to favorable conditions. [https://www.washingtonpost.com/news/capital-weather-gang/wp/2017/09/04/its-becoming-more-likely-hurricane-irma-will-at-least-affect-u-s-if-not-make-landfall/ (''Washington Post'')]
***The NHC says Irma is the most powerful Atlantic storm ever recorded outside the [[Caribbean Sea]] and the [[Gulf of Mexico]]. Ahead of Irma's landing,  [[President of the United States|President]] Donald Trump issues a state of emergency declaration for [[Florida]], [[Puerto Rico]], and the [[United States Virgin Islands|U.S. Virgin Islands]].
