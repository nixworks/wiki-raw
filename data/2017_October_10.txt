*[[October 2017 Northern California wildfires]]
**U.S. President [[Donald Trump]] declares the 17 [[Northern California]] wildfires a major disaster. The fires have killed at least 15 people with more than 150 others missing and at least 2,000 homes and businesses destroyed.
