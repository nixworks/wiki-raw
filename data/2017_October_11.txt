*[[Politics of Peru]]
**The [[Peruvian]] [[Pedro Pablo Kuczynski]] Administration through the [[Ministry of Justice (Peru)|Ministry of Justice of Peru]] appoints a new head to the Direction of Presidentials Graces in order to grant a pardon to 79-year-old former president [[Alberto Fujimori]] (1990-2000). In April 2009, a three-judge panel had sentenced Fujimori for acts of [[political corruption|corruption]] and [[Human rights#Violations|Human Rights violations]]. Fujimori, who has been in jail since [[2007]] and whose health has deteriorated, including alleged [[tongue cancer]], has applied for a pardon unsuccessfully on two occasions.
