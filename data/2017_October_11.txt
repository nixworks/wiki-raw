*[[Politics of Peru]]
**The [[Peruvian]] [[Pedro Pablo Kuczynski]] Administration through the [[Ministry of Justice (Peru)|Ministry of Justice]] appoints a new head to the Direction of Presidential Graces, which is believed to be a first step towards granting a pardon to 79-year-old former president [[Alberto Fujimori]] (1990-2000), although the president Kuczynski has denied it.
