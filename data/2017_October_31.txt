*[[2017 Lower Manhattan attack]]
**Eight people are killed and at least 11 others injured when a man in a rented [[pickup truck]] drives down a bike path along the [[West Side Highway]] in [[Lower Manhattan]]. The 29-year-old suspect, later identified as [[Uzbekistan]]i immigrant [[Sayfullo Saipov]], is shot and taken into custody by police. Police are treating this incident as an act of terrorism.
