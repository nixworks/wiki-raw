*[[North Korea–United States relations]]
**Following the death of 22-year-old [[United States|U.S.]] student [[Otto Warmbier]], touring firm [[Young Pioneer Tours]], who arranged Otto's visit to [[North Korea]], say they will no longer be taking U.S. citizens to North Korea as the "risk is too high". [[Beijing]]-based [[Koryo Tours]] say they are reviewing their positions on taking U.S. citizens to the country. [https://www.theguardian.com/world/2017/jun/20/tour-firm-otto-warmbier-stops-taking-us-citizens-north-korea-young-pioneer-tours (''The Guardian'')]

**Campaigning ends and voting begins in the suburbs of [[Atlanta]] in the [[U.S. state]] of [[Georgia (U.S. state)|Georgia]], where Republican [[Karen Handel]] is in a close race with Democratic challenger [[Jon Ossoff]].
