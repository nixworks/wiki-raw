*[[Georgia's  6th congressional district special election, 2017]]
**Campaigning ends and voting begins in the suburbs of [[Atlanta]] in the [[U.S. state]] of [[Georgia (U.S. state)|Georgia]], where Republican [[Karen Handel]] is in a close race with her Democratic challenger [[Jon Ossoff]]. [http://www.politico.com/story/2017/06/19/ossoff-handel-georgia-special-election-239733 (''Politico'')]
**The amount of money spent for all candidates, $56 million, sets a new record for a U.S. House of Representatives seat. The previous record was $29.5 million, set in a 2012 [[Florida]] race. [http://www.reuters.com/article/us-usa-politics-georgia-election-idUSKBN19A2TD (Reuters)]
