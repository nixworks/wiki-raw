A local police commander is assassinated in [[Baquba]]. A car bomb detonated near a US-Iraqi military convoy in [[Samarra]] kills three national guardsmen and three civilians. All these attacks occurred in the [[Sunni Triangle]]. [http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/middle_east/4129361.stm (BBC)]
