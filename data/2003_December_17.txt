* [[Capture of Saddam Hussein]]:
**[[Mowaffaq al-Rubaie]] says that [[Saddam Hussein]] will be tried in Iraq by an Iraqi court. Mowaffaq, a member of the Iraqi Governing Council, denies reports that the prisoner has been taken out of Baghdad. [http://www.washtimes.com/world/20031217-113503-9915r.htm]
