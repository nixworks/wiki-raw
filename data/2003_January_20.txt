* [[Finsbury Park mosque]] in [[Finsbury Park, London, England]], often associated with Islamic extremism, was raided by British police in an investigation related to the hunt for the poison [[ricin]].  Seven men who were apparently living at the mosque were arrested, and a [[replica firearm]], [[tear gas]] and a [[Electroshock weapon|stun gun]] were reported as having been found on the premises.<ref>{{cite news|author=Sheikh Abu Hamza |url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/england/2676481.stm |title=UK &#124; England &#124; Seven questioned after mosque raid |publisher=BBC News |date=2003-01-20 |accessdate=2012-02-11}}</ref>
* As part of the [[2003 invasion of Iraq|plan to invade Iraq]], British Defence Secretary [[Geoff Hoon]] announces that 26,000 British troops and equipment including 120 [[tank]]s will be sent to [[Kuwait]], joining the 5,000 troops already on their way there.<ref>{{cite news|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/uk/2675101.stm |title=UK &#124; UK sends 31,000 troops to Gulf |publisher=BBC News |date=2003-01-20 |accessdate=2012-02-11}}</ref>
