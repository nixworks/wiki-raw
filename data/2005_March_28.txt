** The [[Israeli Defence Forces]] arrested 8 [[Palestinians]] during a raid into [[Jenin]].  Israel claims the 8 men were members of [[Palestinian Islamic Jihad]] building a [[Qassam rocket]], and the [[Israeli Defence Forces|IDF]] have released a list of the 8 Palestinian arrested, stating their name and their alleged activities. According to that list, one of those arrested, [[Hasin Mahmed Mashkah]], was released in February as a part of the [[Prison]]er-release program involving 500 men. Residents dispute the Israeli claims and state that 3 of the men were members of the security services, and that the other 5 were not known militants.
