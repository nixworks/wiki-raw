* A [[Boeing 737]], [[Flash Airlines Flight 604|Flight 604]], flown by Egyptian charter company [[Flash Airlines]] headed for [[Cairo]] crashes into the [[Red Sea]] minutes after take-off from the holiday resort of [[Sharm el-Sheikh]] in Egypt. All 148 people on board are killed, of whom more than 120 were French tourists.
