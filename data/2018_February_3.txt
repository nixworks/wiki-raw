*[[Syrian Civil War]]
**The [[London]]-based [[Syrian Observatory for Human Rights|SOHR]] reports that unidentified rebel factions have shot down a [[Russia]]n [[Sukhoi Su-25]] jet over the [[Idlib Governorate|province of Idlib]], near [[Maarrat al-Nu'man]], also [[Russian Armed Forces casualties in Syria|killing its pilot]]. The pilot had escaped with his parachute but was killed on the ground while he fought with a pistol to evade capture. No groups claim responsibility. Russia's [[Ministry of Defence (Russia)|Ministry of Defence]] corroborates this version of events. [http://www.dw.com/en/syria-rebels-shoot-down-russian-jet-capture-pilot-monitor/a-42442483 (Deutsche Welle)]
**The [[Interfax]] agency says that [[Russia]] retaliates with a missile attack, killing at least 30 [[Al-Nusra Front|Al-Nusra]] militants in [[Northwestern Syria campaign (October 2017–present)|North-West Syria]] with [[3M-54 Kalibr|Kalibr]] "precision-guided" cruise missiles fired by its [[Mediterranean Sea|Mediterranean]] [[Russian military intervention in the Syrian Civil War|task force]] frigates and submarines. [https://www.usatoday.com/story/news/world/2018/02/03/syria-rebels-shoot-down-russian-jet-monitoring-group-says/303713002/ (USA Today)]
**According to [[Sputnik (news agency)|Sputnik]] agency, [[Tahrir al-Sham]], allegedly including Al-Nusra Front, claims in a social media post that it downed the Russian jet with a [[Shoulder-launched surface-to-air missile|shoulder-launched anti-aircraft missile]]. [https://sputniknews.com/middleeast/201802031061334492-syria-su25-jihadists-jet-missile/ (Sputnik News)]
*[[War in North-West Pakistan]]
**A [[Terrorism in Pakistan|suicide bombing]], revindicated in an email to journalists by the [[Tehrik-i-Taliban Pakistan|Pakistani Taliban]], kills at least 11 soldiers and injures 13 at an army unit camp's sports area in the [[Kabal Tehsil|Kabal]] area of the [[Swat Valley]], in [[Khyber Pakhtunkhwa|Northwestern Pakistan]], according to [[Pakistan Army]] figures. [http://www.thejakartapost.com/news/2018/02/04/suicide-bomber-kills-11-soldiers-in-nw-pakistan.html (AFP via ''The Jakarta Post'')]
