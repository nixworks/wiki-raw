*[[Immigration policy of Donald Trump]], [[Executive Order 13780]]
**The [[Supreme Court of the United States]] partially reinstates President [[Donald Trump]]'s [[Executive Order 13780|travel ban]] and invalidates certain injunctions that were put on the order by two federal appeals courts earlier. Final oral hearings concerning the legality of the order will be held in October 2017 at the Supreme Court. [http://www.bbc.com/news/world-us-canada-40409490 (BBC News)]
