*[[Presidency of Rodrigo Duterte]]
**[[Philippines]] President [[Rodrigo Duterte]] makes another lengthy absence from any public appearance after missing the [[Independence Day (Philippines)|Independence Day]] celebrations earlier in June. Rumors about his health continue to circulate among the Filipino populace although government officials deny any issues.
