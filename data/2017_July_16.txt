;Sports
*[[2017 Wimbledon Championships]]
**In [[tennis]], [[Roger Federer]] of [[Switzerland]] defeats [[Marin Čilić]] of [[Croatia]] 6–3, 6–1, 6–4 to win the [[2017 Wimbledon Championships – Men's Singles|men's singles]] tournament. Federer wins his eighth Wimbledon singles title, surpassing [[William Renshaw]] and [[Pete Sampras]] for the most by a male player; becomes the oldest player in the Open Era to win the Wimbledon men's singles; becomes the first player to win the men's singles at Wimbledon without losing a set since [[Björn Borg]] in [[1976 Wimbledon Championships – Men's Singles|1976]]; and extends his Open Era record for [[Grand Slam (tennis)|Grand Slam]] men's singles titles to 19. [http://www.bbc.com/sport/tennis/40625069 (BBC Sport)]
