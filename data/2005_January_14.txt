* In what is expected to be one of the major scientific events of this decade, the [[Huygens probe]] successfully lands on [[Saturn]]'s largest moon [[Titan (moon)|Titan]]. Data sent back from the probe via the [[Cassini–Huygens|Cassini]] orbiter is now being analysed. Three low-resolution pictures have been released, including one from an altitude of 16&nbsp;km that appears to show [[:Image:050114huygens1.jpg|channels cut by liquid]], and another from the [[:Image:Huygens landing01 L.jpg|surface of Titan showing rocks or ice]].
