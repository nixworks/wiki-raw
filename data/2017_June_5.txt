*[[2016–17 Turkish purges]]
**The [[Ministry of the Interior (Turkey)|Turkish interior ministry]] announces that 130 people, living outside the country and suspected of militant links, will lose their  [[Turkish nationality law|citizenship]] unless they return to Turkey within three months and meet government standards.
