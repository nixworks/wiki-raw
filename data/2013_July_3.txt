*[[2013 Egyptian coup d'etat]]:
**[[Egyptian Army|Egypt's military]] deposes [[Egyptian president|President]] [[Mohamed Morsi]], and installs [[Adly Mansour]], the head of the [[Supreme Constitutional Court of Egypt|Supreme Constitutional Court]], as the country's new interim leader. [http://www.cnn.com/2013/07/03/world/meast/egypt-protests/index.html?hpt=hp_t1 (CNN)]
**The [[Egyptian Army|Egyptian military]] takes control of state TV studios and places a travel ban on [[Egyptian president]] [[Mohamed Morsi]] and other [[Muslim Brotherhood in Egypt|Muslim Brotherhood]] leaders as a military [[coup d'etat]] unfolds, following a [[July 2013 Egyptian protests|week of anti-government unrest]]. [https://www.independent.co.uk/news/world/africa/egypt-protests-military-coup-under-way-as-time-runs-out-for-mohamed-morsi-8683985.html (''The Independent'')]
**[[Mohamed Morsi|President Morsi]] is reportedly placed under [[house arrest]] as troops deploy across Cairo and  armored vehicles and soldiers mass at [[Cairo University]] and other key sites. [http://www.timesofisrael.com/egyptian-leaders-meet-to-plan-post-morsi-transition/ (''The Times of Israel'')]
*[[Drone attacks in Pakistan|U.S. drone attacks on Pakistan]]:
**The death toll from a United States [[Drone attacks in Pakistan|drone strike]] on a militants camp in the [[North Waziristan]] area of [[Pakistan]] rises to 16. [http://www.huffingtonpost.com/huff-wires/20130703/as-pakistan/?utm_hp_ref=homepage&ir=homepage (AP via Huffington Post)]
