*[[2015 FIFA corruption case]]
**[[UEFA]] President [[Michel Platini]], who had been suspended by the [[FIFA Ethics Committee]] last fall, announces his resignation following the [[Court of Arbitration for Sport]] decision  that let stand his ban, shortened to four years which corresponds to the duration of his remaining time in office.
