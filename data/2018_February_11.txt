*[[2018 Saratov Airlines Antonov An-148 crash]]
**An [[Antonov An-148]] of [[Saratov Airlines]] with 71 people on board crashes shortly after take-off from [[Domodedovo Airport]], [[Moscow]], Russia. [[TASS]] agency cites an emergency source stating that none of the 65 passengers and 6 crew members survive. *[[Sri Lankan local elections, 2018]]
**In provisional results with most votes in, the opposition [[Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna|SLPP]] ([[Mahinda Rajapaksa|Rajapaksa]]) is projected to win 44.05% of the vote, the ruling coalition parties [[United National Party|UNP]] ([[Ranil Wickremesinghe|Wickremesinghe]]) and [[Sri Lanka Freedom Party|SLFP]] ([[Maithripala Sirisena|Sirisena]]) gather 31.65% and 9.52% respectively. [[Rosy Senanayake]] (UNP) is set to become the first female mayor of the capital [[Colombo]].
