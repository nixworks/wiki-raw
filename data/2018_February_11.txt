*[[Politics of Sri Lanka]]
**In provisional [[Sri Lankan local elections, 2018|local elections]] results with most votes in, the opposition [[Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna|SLPP]] ([[Mahinda Rajapaksa|Rajapaksa]]) is projected to win 44.05% of the vote, the ruling coalition parties [[UNP]] ([[Ranil Wickremesinghe|Wickremesinghe]]) and [[SLFP]] ([[Maithripala Sirisena|Sirisena]]) gather 31.65% and 9.52% respectively.
