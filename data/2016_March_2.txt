**[[Department of Foreign Affairs (Philippines)|Philippines Foreign Secretary]] [[Albert del Rosario]] says [[Maritime law enforcement agencies in China|Chinese coast guard]] ships that were sighted two weeks ago near the disputed Jackson Atoll in the northeast [[Dangerous Ground (South China Sea)#NE|Dangerous Ground]] are no longer there today. *[[European migrant crisis]]
**The [[European Union]] launches a new aid program  worth an initial 700 million euros (US$760 million) to address the growing refugee crisis in [[Greece]]. [[European Commissioner for Humanitarian Aid and Civil Protection|EU Humanitarian Aid Commissioner]] [[Christos Stylianides]] says the so-called Emergency Assistance Instrument will be used  to help migrants trapped in Greece and, if needed, in  other countries on the migration route front lines. Approval by a  majority of EU members is required. The next EU meeting is scheduled for Monday. [https://www.nytimes.com/2016/03/03/world/europe/eu-aid-migrants-greece.html (''The New York Times'')] [https://www.reuters.com/article/us-europe-migrants-eu-idUSKCN0W41DB (Reuters)] [http://www.ekathimerini.com/206539/article/ekathimerini/news/eu-eyes-spending-700-million-euros-for-refugee-crisis (''Kathimerini'')]
