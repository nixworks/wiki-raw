*[[Uber protests and legal actions]]
**The [[European Court of Justice]] (ECJ) rules that [[Uber (company)|Uber]] is officially a transport company and not a digital service, thus requiring it to accept stricter regulation and licensing within the [[European Union]]. The case arose after Uber was told to obey local [[Taxicab|taxi]] rules in [[Barcelona]].
