* A [[bomb]] explosion near a convoy of cars carrying [[Somalia]]'s [[Prime Minister of Somalia|Prime Minister]] [[Ali Mohammed Ghedi]] in [[Mogadishu]]. Although the PM escapes unhurt, 9 people have been killed and 20 others wounded. [http://www.alertnet.org/thenews/newsdesk/L06777799.htm (Reuters)] (Link dead as of 20:57, 14 January 2007 (UTC)) 
* Former [[President of Peru|Peruvian President]] [[Alberto Fujimori]] arrives in [[Santiago, Chile]] after being exiled in [[Japan]] since [[2000]]. Although he is the subject of an [[Interpol]] arrest warrant, the [[Chile]]an government said he cannot be arrested without an order from a Chilean judge. Fujimori arrives at a time of tension between [[Chile]] and [[Peru]] over sea boundaries. [http://www.cnn.com/2005/WORLD/americas/11/06/chile.fujimori.ap/index.html (CNN)] (Link dead as of 22:17, 14 January 2007 (UTC))
* People in several parts of [[Germany]] report several [[Meteoroid#Fireball|fireballs]] in the sky, leading to speculation that they may be [[Unidentified flying object|UFO]]s. Scientists report that the sightings are of the [[Taurid meteor shower]]. * [[Demographics of Azerbaijan|Azerbaijani citizens]] go to the polls in the [[Azerbaijan parliamentary election, 2005]]. Opposition parties have alleged that there is [[voting fraud]].
