*[[Temporary Protected Status]]
**The [[United States]] will end the so-called [[temporary protected status]] program (TPS) for more than 300,000 refugees from several countries: [[El Salvador]], [[Haiti]], [[Honduras]], [[Nepal]], [[Nicaragua]], [[Somalia]], [[South Sudan]], [[Sudan]], [[Syria]] and [[Yemen]]. Many of the refugees have already left for [[Canada]], where it is easier to get [[Canadian immigration and refugee law|asylum]].
