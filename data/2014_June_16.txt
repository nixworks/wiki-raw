**The [[Pakistan Air Force]] conducts airstrikes against 6 [[Tehrik-i-Taliban Pakistan]] positions in [[North Waziristan]], killing 27 militants. [http://www.nytimes.com/2014/06/17/world/asia/pakistan-hits-taliban-strongholds-with-airstrikes.html (''New York Times'')]
*[[Syrian Civil War]]:
**A [[barrel bomb]] is dropped from a helicopter in [[Aleppo]], [[Syria]], killing at least 25 people. [http://www.reuters.com/article/2014/06/16/us-syria-crisis-barrelbombs-idUSKBN0ER1U720140616 (Reuters)]

**[[Ukraine]]'s president [[Petro Poroshenko]] says he will put forward a detailed peace plan that will include a ceasefire in the conflict. [http://www.voanews.com/content/ukraine-president-promises-peace-plan-ceasefire/1937967.html (Voice of America)]
*[[2014 anti-Muslim riots in Sri Lanka|Violent riots]] between [[Muslims]] and [[Buddhists]] rage in the town of [[Welipitiya Divisional Secretariat|Welipitiya]], [[Sri Lanka]], leaving 3 dead, 78 injured, and multiple [[mosques]] and shops burned or vandalized. [http://www.nytimes.com/2014/06/17/world/asia/deadly-religious-violence-erupts-in-sri-lanka.html?partner=rss&emc=rss&_r=0 (''New York Times'')]
*The [[Al-Shabaab (militant group)|Al-Shabaab]] militant group launches a second attack near the [[Kenya]]n town of [[Mpeketoni]] killing another ten people. [http://www.bbc.com/news/world-africa-27882084# (BBC)]

;Disasters and accidents
*June 16-18 Tornado Outbreak begins. Twin EF4 tornadoes devastate northeast Nebraska. A town particularly hit hard being [[Pilger,_Nebraska|Pilger, NE]] where a young girl is killed. At night several other tornadoes touch down and one of them, an EF3, tears through the southwest portions of [[Madison, Wisconsin]]. [http://www.ustornadoes.com/2014/06/23/tornado-digest-after-three-big-days-in-june-will-we-finish-the-month-strong/ June 16-18 Tornado Outbreak (www.ustornadoes.com)]

[http://www.smh.com.au/world/the-putin-slur-heard-round-the-world-delights-ukrainians-angers-russians-20140616-zs8zh.html#ixzz34pGNG5PN (Sydney Morning Herald)]
