* [[Same-sex marriage in Canada]]: A [[British Columbia]] court rules that [[gay|same-sex]] couples may [[same-sex marriage|get married]] in that province, effective immediately. This decision is similar to the [[Ontario]] decision on [[June 2003|June 10, 2003]]. [http://cnews.canoe.ca/CNEWS/Canada/2003/07/08/130210-cp.html (Canada News)]
