**Voters in [[Slovenia]] go to the polls to elect their [[President of Slovenia|president]]. *[[Argentine legislative election, 2017]]
**Voters in [[Argentina]] go to the polls to elect half of the members of the [[Argentine Chamber of Deputies|Chamber of Deputies]] and [[Argentine Senate|Senate]]. ''[[Cambiemos]]'', the national government coalition led by president [[Mauricio Macri]], got a clear victory in the four most populated provinces and the city of Buenos Aires. Former president [[Cristina Fernández de Kirchner]] ran for senator of Buenos and ended second, leading the opposition. [https://www.washingtonpost.com/politics/congress/argentina-election-gives-president-a-legislative-majority/2017/10/22/ccea5a36-b78f-11e7-9b93-b97043e57a22_story.html?utm_term=.0377e5e41c21 (''The Washington Post'')]
