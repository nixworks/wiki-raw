* The [[Islam]]ic Commission of Spain, that country's largest Muslim group, issues a [[fatwa]] against [[al-Qaeda]] leader [[Osama Bin Ladin]], ruling that his actions have made him an [[apostate]], that he should not be regarded as a Muslim, and that his claim that the [[Qur'an]] justifies his [[terrorism|terrorist]] actions constitutes [[istihlal]], the sin of making up one's own law. It is claimed to be the first fatwa to be pronounced against Bin Ladin, and to have the tacit support of Muslim leaders in several Islamic countries. [http://www.cbc.ca/world/story/2005/03/11/fatwa-050311.html (CBC)]
