* [[Prince William, Duke of Cambridge|Prince William]], an officer in the [[Blues and Royals]], laid a [[wreath]] for the first time by the [[Cenotaph]] as his grandmother [[Elizabeth II|The Queen]] and his father [[Charles, Prince of Wales|The Prince of Wales]], looked on. * A majority of [[France|French]] citizens would support a union with the French-speaking Belgian region of [[Wallonia]] if [[Belgium]] were to cease to exist, according to a survey. [http://www.lejdd.fr/sondages/70.html (Journal du Dimanche)]
* A similar survey held in the [[Netherlands]] shows that 45% of the Dutch would support a union with [[Flanders]], whereas 49% would oppose such a union. [http://www.trouw.nl/laatstenieuws/ln_binnenland/article843379.ece/Groot_deel_Nederlanders_voor_fusie_met_Vlaanderen (Trouw)]
* [[Danilo Türk]] wins the [[Slovenian presidential election, 2007|2007 Slovenian presidential elections]] with a large margin. [http://news.yahoo.com/s/nm/20071111/wl_nm/slovenia_president_dc_3 (Reuters via Yahoo! News)]
* Former [[Khmer Rouge]] Foreign Minister, [[Ieng Sary]] and his wife, [[Ieng Thirith]] are arrested in [[Phnom Penh]] to face charges before that country's [[U.N.]] [[Khmer Rouge Tribunal|genocide tribunal]]. [http://www.foxnews.com/wires/2007Nov12/0,4670,CambodiaKhmerRouge,00.html (AP via FOX News)]
* The [[Ulster Defence Association]] announces that its [[Ulster Freedom Fighters]]' units are to stand down from midnight. [http://www.rte.ie/news/2007/1111/uda.html (RTÉ)]
* [[Gabriele Sandri]], a 26-year-old supporter of the sports club [[S.S. Lazio]] is killed by [[Polizia di Stato|police]] in a [[filling station|service station]] near [[Arezzo]], [[Italy]]. [[Football (soccer)|Football]] fans later clash with police in most Italian [[stadium]]s. [http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/europe/7090017.stm (BBC)]
* [[Transport for London]] takes over the [[North London Line]] and the other suburban rail lines hitherto operated by [[Silverlink]] to form the [[London Overground]]. While remaining part of the [[National Rail]] network, the contracting authority for the franchise is Transport for London, rather than central Government.
