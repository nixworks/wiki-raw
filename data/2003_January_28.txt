* An election in the [[U.S. state|state]] of [[Oregon]] to pass a temporary three-year income tax failed with 54% of the votes voting against and 44% voting for. This forced the first layoffs in the [[Oregon State Police]] since its creation in 1931, and other actions including cutbacks in many of the local school districts.
