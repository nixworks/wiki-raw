* The [[World Health Organisation]] says that six months into its project against [[AIDS]], 440,000 people in developing nations have received [[antiretroviral drugs]]. Despite being 60,000 short of its target, the organisation says it is still hopeful of achieving its aim of distributing to 3,000,000 people by the end of 2005 [http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/health/3884083.stm (BBC)]
