;Armed conflicts and attacks
*[[2014 pro-Russian unrest in Ukraine]]:
**Pro-Russian separatist commander [[Alexander Khodakovsky]] of the [[2014 insurgency in Donbass#Vostok_Battalion|Vostok Battalion]] confirms that the rebels did possess the BUK missile system - believed to have shot down [[Malaysia Airlines Flight 17]] - originating from Russia, and it could have been sent back to Russia subsequently to remove proof of its presence. [https://www.reuters.com/article/2014/07/23/us-ukraine-crisis-commander-exclusive-idUSKBN0FS1V920140723 (Reuters)]
**The [[Red Cross]] officially declares the [[Pro-Russian conflict in Ukraine|fighting]] in Ukraine as a civil war. The formal classification means participants in the fighting between government forces and pro-Russian separatists in the east could eventually be prosecuted for [[War crime|war crimes]] in international courts. [http://www.thelocal.ch/20140723/red-cross-declares-civil-war-in-ukraine (The Local)]
**Ukrainian forces recapture the villages of [[Karlivka, Donetsk Oblast|Karlivka]], Netailove, and Pervomaiske in [[Donetsk Oblast]] from pro-Russian separatists. [http://www.kyivpost.com/content/ukraine/ukrainian-troops-free-three-villages-in-donetsk-region-fighting-for-lysychansk-357583.html (''Kyiv Post'')]
**Two Ukrainian [[Sukhoi Su-25]] military fighter jets are shot down by pro-Russian separatists in [[Savur Mogila]]. [http://www.chron.com/news/world/article/2-Ukrainian-military-fighter-jets-shot-down-5640344.php (AP via Chron News)]
**The bodies of crash victims of [[Malaysia Airlines Flight 17]] are returned from Eastern [[Ukraine]] to the [[Netherlands]] together with [[flight recorder]]s from the flight. [http://www.bworldonline.com/content.php?section=World&title=Netherlands-mourns-as-bodies-of-MH17-crash-victims-return&id=91363 (Business World Online)]
