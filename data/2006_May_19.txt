* [[2006 Iranian sumptuary law controversy]]: 
** The Canadian ''[[National Post]]'' reports that certain "Iranian expatriates living in Canada" are claiming that the [[Iran]]ian [[Majlis of Iran|parliament]] has passed [[2006 Iranian sumptuary law|a law]] that would require the country's non-Muslim religious [[minority group|minorities]] to wear distinctive markings: yellow ribbons for [[Jews]], red for [[Christianity|Christians]] and blue for [[Zoroastrianism|Zoroastrians]]. After great controversy, the ''National Post'' retracts its original report.
