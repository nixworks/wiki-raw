*[[Saudi Arabian-led intervention in Yemen]]
**[[Houthis]] say that they launched a [[Volcano H-2]] missile targeting at the Al-Yamamah Palace in the Saudi capital city of [[Riyadh]]. According to the Saudis, the missile was intercepted south of Riyadh and caused no casualties. [https://www.wsj.com/articles/saudi-arabia-intercepts-missile-fired-by-yemen-houthi-rebels-1513686517 (''Wall Street Journal'')]
**The Secretary-General of the [[Organization of Islamic Cooperation|OIC]], [[Yousef Al-Othaimeen]], says the repeated launch of ballistic missiles at Saudi Arabia served to confirm the Houthis' "hostility and criminality" and further claimed "The Houthis are trying to destabilize [[Saudi Arabia]] and the entire region." [http://aa.com.tr/en/middle-east/islamic-organization-condemns-houthi-missile-attack/1009341 (AA)]
