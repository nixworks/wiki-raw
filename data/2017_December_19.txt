*[[Saudi Arabian-led intervention in Yemen]]
**[[Houthis]] say that they launched a [[Volcano H-2]] missile targeting at the Al-Yamamah Palace in the Saudi capital city of [[Riyadh]]. According to the Saudis, the missile was intercepted south of Riyadh and caused no casualties. [https://www.wsj.com/articles/saudi-arabia-intercepts-missile-fired-by-yemen-houthi-rebels-1513686517 (''Wall Street Journal'')]
**The Secretary-General of the [[Organization of Islamic Cooperation|OIC]], [[Yousef Al-Othaimeen]], says the repeated launch of ballistic missiles at Saudi Arabia served to confirm the Houthis' "hostility and criminality" and further claimed "The Houthis are trying to destabilize [[Saudi Arabia]] and the entire region." [http://aa.com.tr/en/middle-east/islamic-organization-condemns-houthi-missile-attack/1009341 (AA)]

*[[Tax Cuts and Jobs Act of 2017]]
**The [[United States]] [[United States House of Representatives|House of Representatives]] passes the [[Tax Cuts and Jobs Act of 2017]] by 227 votes to 203. It is now to be sent to the [[United States Senate]] for a vote, possibly even tonight. [https://www.nbcnews.com/politics/congress/republican-tax-bill-house-senate-trump-n831161 (NBC News)]
**The Senate's parliamentarian found that several provisions in the House bill violated Senate rules, which forces the House of Representatives to call a second vote on an altered version of the legislation that has the violating provisions removed. The second vote by the House of Representatives is expected to take place on December 20, 2017. [https://www.nbcnews.com/politics/congress/republican-tax-bill-house-senate-trump-n831161 (NBC News)]
