*[[Upper Big Branch Mine disaster]]
**A court convicts [[Don Blankenship|Donald Blankenship]], former [[Chief executive officer|CEO]] of [[Massey Energy|Massey Energy Company]], of violating  [[safety standards]] that led to the deaths of 29 [[Upper Big Branch Mine disaster|Upper Big Branch]] mine workers in [[Montcoal, West Virginia]] in 2010. He is acquitted of [[making false statements]] and [[Mining#Safety|deceiving regulators]]. Blankenship, who faces up to one year in prison and a fine of $250,000, is the most prominent American coal executive ever to be convicted of a charge connected to the deaths of miners.
