* A [[Russian Navy]] fighter jet crashes and sinks to a depth of 1,100 meters near [[Shetland Islands|Shetland]] in the [[Norwegian Sea]] during a military exercise. Due to the plane's reportedly containing secret [[high tech|high-tech]] military equipment, Russian authorities have decided to destroy it using underwater bombs.
