* A [[truck bomb]] destroys a military hospital in [[Mozdok]] in Southern Russia, near [[Chechnya]], killing 41 and wounding at least 76. The Russian government blames the attack on [[Second Chechen War|Chechen separatists]]. A media spokesman for rebel political leader, [[Aslan Maskhadov]], denied any connection with the incident.<ref>[http://www.washingtonpost.com/wp-dyn/articles/A13500-2003Aug1.html ]{{dead link|date=September 2015}}</ref>
* [[North Korea]] agrees to multilateral talks in its [[North Korea nuclear weapons program|nuclear standoff]] with Japan, [[South Korea]], Russia, The United States, and the People's Republic of China.<ref>[http://www.iht.com/articles/104953.html ]{{dead link|date=September 2015}}</ref>
