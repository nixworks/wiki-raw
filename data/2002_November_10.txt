* According to the Guardian newspaper, [[Gauthier Hulot]] of the [[Institut de Physique du Globe de Paris|Paris Geophysical Institute]] has discovered evidence of a reduction of the [[Earth's magnetic field]] over the last two hundred years. It is possible that this may be a prelude to a reversal of polarity of the Earth's magnetic field over the next few hundred years.
