* The [[2005 Atlantic hurricane season]] nears its official end but the 26th named storm of the season, [[2005 Atlantic hurricane season#Tropical Storm Epsilon|Tropical Storm Epsilon]], forms from a non-tropical low east of [[Bermuda]]. * [[Virginia]] [[Governor of Virginia|Governor]] [[Mark R. Warner]] grants [[Pardon|clemency]] in the case of convicted murderer [[Robin Lovitt]]. It was about 24 hours before Lovitt was scheduled to be [[capital punishment|executed]]. Evidence against Lovitt had been illegally destroyed after his trial by a court clerk, preventing [[DNA]] testing that may have cleared him of the crime. Lovitt's execution was to be the 1,000th execution in the [[United States]] since the [[Supreme Court of the United States|Supreme Court]] [[Gregg v. Georgia|reinstated the death penalty]] in [[1976]].
