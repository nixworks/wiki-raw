*[[Crime in Ohio]]
**A judge, Joseph Bruzzese, Jr., was ambushed and shot as he was walking from his vehicle to a court house in [[Steubenville, Ohio]]. Judge Bruzzese returned fire with his own weapon, but was injured and airlifted to a local hospital. A probation officer near the shooting shot and killed the gunman. A second person in the suspects vehicle was arrested. [http://abcnews.go.com/US/wireStory/judge-shot-wounded-ohio-courthouse-suspect-killed-49334444 (ABC News)]
