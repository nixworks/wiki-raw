*[[Syrian Civil War]]
**[[Syrian opposition]] politicians and rebels, attending a two-day meeting hosted by [[Saudi Arabia]] in [[Riyadh]], agree on a framework that would guide proposed talks to end  [[Syria|Syria's]] nearly five-year conflict: a civil, democratic Syria with  a transitional government that would respect human rights and the territorial integrity of the country; and, safeguard state institutions while restructuring security forces. [[Ahrar ash-Sham]], a Saudi-backed ultraconservative group, withdrew from the talks at the end of the meeting. [http://www.bbc.com/news/world-middle-east-35060935 (BBC)] [http://bigstory.ap.org/article/ad4e978fab86467e82ae32cde60f26f0/saudi-fm-syrias-assad-must-leave-or-be-forced-out (AP)]
