*[[Bulgarian parliamentary election, 2014]]
**Bulgarians vote in a snap election, following the resignation of a [[Bulgarian Socialist Party|Socialist]]-led government in July; the number of parties in the [[National Assembly (Bulgaria)|National Assembly]] grows from four to eight, and the centre-right [[GERB]] becomes the biggest party but falls short of an overall majority.
