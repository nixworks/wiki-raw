;Politics and elections
*[[Fatah–Hamas reconciliation process]]
**Palestinian factions, [[Hamas]] and [[Fatah]], sign a unity deal in [[Cairo]], that is publicly supported by [[Egypt]] and [[Saudi Arabia]]. The  Fatah-controlled  [[Palestinian National Authority]], along with [[Israel]], currently governs the [[West Bank]]. The PNA will take administrative control of the [[Gaza Strip]], and, along with Hamas, police its borders. The parties consider the agreement a first step, and, if productive, plan to meet next month to address the remaining issues. [[Israel]] opposes any involvement by Hamas in a Palestinian government. [https://www.nytimes.com/2017/10/12/world/middleeast/palestinians-fatah-hamas-gaza.html (''The New York Times'')] [http://www.bbc.com/news/world-middle-east-41591450 (BBC)]
