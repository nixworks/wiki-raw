**A gunman opens fire at a [[Congressional Baseball Game|Congressional Baseball]] practice, injuring five people, including United States Congressman [[Steve Scalise]] (R-[[Louisiana's 1st congressional district|LA-01]]) who is in critical condition. *[[Presidency of Donald Trump]]
**Nearly 200 members of the [[Democratic Party (United States)|Democratic Party]] in the [[United States Congress]] file a lawsuit in the federal court, claiming that [[President of the United States|U.S. President]] Trump profits from business dealings with foreign governments, in violation of the [[United States Constitution]]’s [[Title of Nobility Clause|emolument clauses]]. This is the third such suit of its kind, following on the heels of the most recent suit by the Attorneys General of [[Maryland]] and the [[District of Columbia]].[https://www.nytimes.com/2017/06/14/us/politics/democrats-in-congress-to-sue-trump-over-foreign-business-dealings.html (''The New York Times'')]

**[[Australia]] agrees to pay AUS$70 million in compensation to 1,905 people who have been detained on [[Manus Island]] since 2012, so avoiding a public trial against the government and the [[Transfield]] and [[G4S]] private companies for the detainees' degrading and cruel treatment. [http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/2017/06/14/manus-island-detainees-win-42-million-payout-australia-degrading/ (''The Telegraph'')]
