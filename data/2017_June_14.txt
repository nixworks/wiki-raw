*[[Grenfell Tower fire]]
**[[Grenfell Tower fire|A large fire]] consumes the 27-storey Grenfell Tower apartment block in [[North Kensington]], West London, with the entire building evacuated and over 40 fire engines and 200 firefighters battling the blaze. A number of fatalities are reported and more people are treated for injuries, with more people possibly being trapped inside.
