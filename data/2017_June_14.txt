*[[Grenfell Tower fire]]
**[[Grenfell Tower fire|A large fire]] consumes the 24-storey Grenfell Tower apartment block in [[North Kensington]], West London, with the entire building evacuated and over 40 fire engines and 200 firefighters battling the blaze.
