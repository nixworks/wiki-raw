;Business and economics
*[[Dow Chemical Company|Dow Chemical]] agrees to pay $835 million to settle a decade-long, [[Price fixing|price-fixing]], [[Class action|class-action]] dispute. Dow states that the uncertainty about the U.S. Supreme Court makeup following [[Antonin Scalia|Justice Antonin Scalia's]] death means there is an "increased likelihood for unfavorable outcomes for business involved in class action suits." The urethane chemicals case, from 2005, was against a number of companies; Dow had been the only defendant not to settle. [https://www.reuters.com/article/us-usa-court-classactions-idUSKCN0VZ2TF (Reuters)] [http://www.winnipegsun.com/2016/02/26/dow-chemical-settles-price-fixing-case (Reuters via ''Winnipeg Sun'')]
*A federal appeals court rules in favor of [[Samsung]] on several issues arising out of that company's long-running [[patent]] dispute with [[Apple Inc.|Apple]], overturning a $120 million jury verdict in Apple's favor in [[2014]]. [https://www.reuters.com/article/apple-samsung-elec-appeal-idUSKCN0VZ27R (Reuters)]

;International Relations 
*[[Syrian peace process|Syrian Civil War peace process]]
**The Syrian cessation of hostilities truce is in effect, as of midnight, Saturday,  local [[Syria]]n time (10:00 p.m. UTC Friday/5:00 p.m. EST Friday). [https://www.reuters.com/article/us-mideast-crisis-syria-cessation-idUSKCN0VZ2Z6 (Reuters)] 
**The [[United Nations Security Council]], via resolution 2268, endorses the [[truce]] which has been agreed to by  nearly 100 rebel factions. The ceasefire   does not apply to  military actions against terrorist organizations, such as [[Islamic State of Iraq and the Levant|ISIL]] and [[al-Nusra Front|Jabhat al-Nusra]] (al-Qaeda in  Syria). [https://www.rt.com/news/333784-un-security-syria-resolution/ (RT)] [http://www.state.gov/r/pa/prs/ps/2016/02/253115.htm (text via U.S. State Dept.)]
**United Nations special envoy [[Staffan de Mistura]] says, assuming the ceasefire holds, he will reconvene formal peace talks in [[Geneva]], [[Switzerland]],  on Monday, March 7.  [http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/worldnews/middleeast/syria/12175668/Syrian-peace-talks-to-resume-if-ceasefire-holds.html (''The Daily Telegraph'')]
