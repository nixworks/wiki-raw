*[[Aleppo offensive (November 2016)]]
**[[Syrian Armed Forces]] have captured the entire Hanano district in eastern [[Aleppo]] and are working on removing [[land mine|mines]] and [[Improvised Explosive Device|improvised explosive devices]] from the district, while the rebels blame lack of hospitals for collapsing defences. **[[Syrian Armed Forces]], [[Liwa al-Quds]] and other allied forces capture Jabal Badro Neighbourhood, Ard Hamra area and began advance at Al-Sakhour district. [https://southfront.org/al-nusra-al-qaeda-defensese-collapse-syrian-army-ready-to-split-eastern-aleppo-pocket-into-two/ (South Front)]
**The [[Syrian Observatory for Human Rights]] claims that nearly ten thousand civilians have fled [[Aleppo]] in the past day. [https://www.yahoo.com/news/nearly-10-000-civilians-flee-east-aleppo-monitor-224230423.html (AFP via Yahoo! News)]
