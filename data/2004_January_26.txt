* [[Avian influenza]] has now been detected in a total of nine countries, with Pakistan and [[Laos]] as the latest additions. Pakistan has detected less dangerous strains H7 and H9. A six-year-old [[Thailand|Thai]] boy became the seventh victim in Asia, with another ten suspected cases in the country.  Around {{Nowrap|19 million}} chickens have been slaughtered as a result of fighting the spread of the flu.  The [[World Health Organisation]] expresses concern about a serious human outbreak.
