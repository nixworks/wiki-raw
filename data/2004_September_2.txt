* A night-time fire breaks out in the [[Duchess Anna Amalia Library]] (a [[United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization|UNESCO]] [[World Heritage Site]]) in [[Weimar]], [[Thuringia]], Germany. Damage is estimated in the millions of dollars and 40,000 to 50,000 books were destroyed. An authentic [[Martin Luther|Lutheran]] Bible from 1534 was saved. The library contains more than 1,000,000 volumes, including the Duchess' 13,000-volume music collection, the world's largest collection of materials relating to [[Goethe|Goethe's]] masterpiece ''[[Faust, Part 1|Faust]],'' and an important collection of [[William Shakespeare|Shakespeariana]].
