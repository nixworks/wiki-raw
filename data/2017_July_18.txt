*[[Ukrainian crisis]]
**DPR leader [[Alexander Zakharchenko]] announced that the self-declared [[Donetsk People's Republic]] (DPR) and [[Luhansk People's Republic]] (LPR) would form a new state called [[Little Russia|Malorossiya]] (Little Russia) as an official successor to what he called the "[[failed state]]" of [[Ukraine]], with [[Donetsk]] replacing [[Kiev]] as the country's capital. Ukrainian president [[Petro Poroshenko]] and LPR leader [[Igor Plotnitsky]] denounced the plans.
