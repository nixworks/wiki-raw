*[[Panama Papers]]
**A leak of 11.5 million documents from law firm [[Mossack Fonseca]] reveals details of [[shell company|shell companies]] used by 12 current or former world leaders including [[President of Russia]] [[Vladimir Putin]] whose aides allegedly shuffled billions of dollars. [http://www.msn.com/en-au/news/world/vladimir-putin-associates-jackie-chan-identified-in-unprecedented-leak-of-offshore-financial-records/ar-BBriy6Y?li=AAgfIYZ&ocid=UP94DHP (ABC News via 9MSN)]
**The documents, 2.6 terabytes of information, were obtained more than a year ago from an anonymous source by the German newspaper ''[[Süddeutsche Zeitung]]''. [[Journalist]]s from more than 80 countries have been working with this data. The documents expose holdings of 12 current and former world leaders, and 128 more politicians and public officials worldwide. The national leaders named include [[Prime Minister of Pakistan|Pakistan Prime Minister]] [[Nawaz Sharif]]; [[President of Ukraine|Ukraine's President]] [[Petro Poroshenko]], and [[Prime Minister of Iceland|Iceland's Prime Minister]] [[Sigmundur Davíð Gunnlaugsson]]. [http://www.theguardian.com/news/2016/apr/03/the-panama-papers-how-the-worlds-rich-and-famous-hide-their-money-offshore (''The Guardian'')][http://www.usatoday.com/story/news/2016/04/03/panama-papers-explainer-what-you-should-know/82591116/ (''USA Today'')]
