*[[Israeli–Palestinian conflict]]
**[[Palestinians|Palestinian people]] and the [[Israel Defense Forces|Israeli army]] scuffle in [[Jerusalem]], [[Bethlehem]] and at the [[Kalandia|Qalandia]] border crossing over the [[status of Jerusalem]]. Clashes are also reported in [[Gaza]], [[Ramallah]], [[Hebron]] and [[Qalqilya]]. One source claims a Palestinian person is seriously injured by Israeli soldiers near [[Ramallah]].
