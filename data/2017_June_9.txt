*[[United Kingdom general election, 2017]]
**Results of yesterday's election are counted, revealing a [[hung parliament]] as the [[Conservative Party (UK)|Conservative Party]] fails to retain its [[majority government]], despite remaining the party with the most seats. Prime Minister [[Theresa May]] will seek permission from [[Buckingham Palace]] to form a [[minority government]] with the support of the Northern Irish [[Democratic Unionist Party]]. [http://www.bbc.com/news/election-2017-40219030 (BBC)]
**Significant gains are made by the opposition [[Labour Party (UK)|Labour Party]] led by [[Jeremy Corbyn]], while the [[Scottish National Party]]'s dominance of Scottish seats is reduced. [https://www.theguardian.com/politics/live/2017/jun/08/general-election-results-2017-uk-live-labour-tories-corbyn-may-election-results-live-news-line (''The Guardian'')]
**[[UK Independence Party]] leader [[Paul Nuttall]] resigns; his party's share of the [[popular vote]] of 1.8% was 10% lower than in 2015 (12.6%). [http://www.independent.co.uk/News/uk/politics/election-results-latest-ukip-seats-vote-share-paul-nuttall-a7780406.html (''The Independent'')]
*[[Government of Japan]]
**The [[National Diet]] of [[Japan]] passes legislation allowing Emperor [[Akihito]] to [[abdication|abdicate]]. The abdication is to take place within three years.
