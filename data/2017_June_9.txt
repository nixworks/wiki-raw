*[[United Kingdom general election, 2017]]
**Results of yesterday's [[snap election]] are counted, revealing a [[hung parliament]] as the [[Conservative Party (UK)|Conservative Party]] fails to retain its [[Majority government|parliament majority]], despite remaining the party with the most seats. Prime Minister [[Theresa May]] announced that she will form [[Second May ministry|a minority government]] with [[Con–DUP pact|the support]] of the Northern Irish [[Democratic Unionist Party]].
