* [[Egypt]]ian [[Foreign Minister]] [[Ahmad Maher (diplomat)|Ahmed Maher]] is assaulted by [[Palestinians]] during [[prayer]] at the [[al-Aqsa Mosque]] in [[Jerusalem]]. After several hours care in the hospital, he was escorted to his airplane by an Israeli cardiologist and paramedic.
