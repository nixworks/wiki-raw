*[[South Australia]] has finalized a deal with [[Tesla, Inc.|Tesla]], spearheaded by tweets originating from CEO of Tesla [[Elon Musk]] to install a battery system in the state. Tesla said in a statement that upon completion by December 2017 , the system would be the largest [[lithium-ion battery]] storage project in the world, overtaking an 80 [[megawatt-hour]] power station at [[Mira Loma]] in [[California]] also built using Tesla batteries.
