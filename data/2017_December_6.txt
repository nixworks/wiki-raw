*[[Positions on Jerusalem]]
**[[U.S. President]] [[Donald Trump]] will recognize Jerusalem as Israel's capital, senior administration officials have said. This backs an [[Israel]] claim of controlling the entire city as its indivisible capital. The expected move of the [[United States]] embassy would set a [[precedent]]. [http://www.bbc.com/news/world-middle-east-42246564 (BBC)]
***The [[Palestinians]] say such decision would mean a "kiss of death" to the [[two-state solution]] of the [[Israeli–Palestinian conflict]]. [https://www.reuters.com/article/us-usa-trump-israel/in-break-with-decades-of-u-s-policy-trump-to-recognize-jerusalem-as-israels-capital-idUSKBN1DZ04V (Reuters)] [https://www.express.co.uk/news/world/888739/Donald-Trump-Jerusalem-Israel-capital-news-US-Manuel-Hassassian (''Express UK'')]
***[[Pope Francis]] urges the [[status quo]] for Jerusalem and calls for "[[wisdom]] and [[prudence]]" to avoid further conflict. [http://abcnews.go.com/International/wireStory/pope-francis-urges-status-quo-jerusalem-calls-wisdom-51608418 (ABC News)]
