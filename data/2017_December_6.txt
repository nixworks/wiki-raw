;International relations
*[[Positions on Jerusalem]], [[Israel–United States relations]]
**[[U.S. President]] [[Donald Trump]] [[Status of Jerusalem|recognizes]] Jerusalem as the capital of Israel and says the U.S. starts the process of moving its embassy from Tel Aviv to Jerusalem. The U.S. State Department issues an internal travel warning to Israel, Jerusalem and the West Bank until December 20. The U.S. embassy in [[Amman]] temporarily suspends routine public services and stops embassy travel around [[Jordan]]. Embassies in Berlin and London issue vigilance warnings to U.S. citizens. [http://edition.cnn.com/2017/12/06/politics/president-donald-trump-jerusalem/index.html (CNN)] [https://www.reuters.com/article/us-usa-trump-israel-cable/u-s-asks-officials-to-defer-travel-to-israel-west-bank-to-december-20-idUSKBN1E02HU (Reuters)] [http://www.bbc.com/news/live/world-middle-east-42255592 (BBC)]
**Israeli Prime Minister [[Benjamin Netanyahu]] praises the announcement as a "historic landmark." [http://www.bbc.com/news/world-middle-east-42246564 (BBC)]
**[[President of the State of Palestine]] [[Mahmoud Abbas]] says the move undermines the U.S. position as a peace mediator while the Palestinian Islamist group [[Hamas]] says that the decision would "open the gates of hell on U.S. interests in the region." [http://www.bbc.com/news/world-middle-east-42250340 (BBC)]
**The [[Arab League]] calls it "a dangerous measure that would have repercussions" and also questions the future role of the U.S. as a "trusted mediator" in peace talks. [http://www.bbc.com/news/world-middle-east-42250340 (BBC)]
**Protests against the decision break out in Jordan, Palestine and Turkey. [http://www.bbc.com/news/world-us-canada-42259443 (BBC)], [https://www.reuters.com/article/us-usa-trump-israel/trump-recognizes-jerusalem-as-israels-capital-defying-allies-and-foes-idUSKBN1E01PS (Reuters)]
**The decision draws critical reaction from key U.S. allies that include the EU, France, Germany and the United Kingdom. [http://www.chicagotribune.com/news/nationworld/ct-jerusalem-israel-capital-declaration-20171206-story.html (''Chicago Tribune'')][http://www.bbc.com/news/world-middle-east-42250340 (BBC)]
**[[Pope Francis]] urges respect for the [[status quo]] for Jerusalem and calls for "[[wisdom]] and [[prudence]]" to avoid further conflict. [http://abcnews.go.com/International/wireStory/pope-francis-urges-status-quo-jerusalem-calls-wisdom-51608418 (ABC News)]
**[[Secretary-General of the United Nations]] [[António Guterres]] criticizes the announcement, saying that it "would jeopardise the prospect of peace for Israelis and Palestinians".[http://www.bbc.com/news/world-us-canada-42259443 (BBC)]
**8 of the 15 current [[United Nations  Security Council]] members hold an urgent meeting on the U.S. decision. [http://www.bbc.com/news/world-us-canada-42259443 (BBC)]
