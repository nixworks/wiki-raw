*[[2018 Vienna stabbing]]
**Two separate stabbings occur in [[Vienna]]. Four people are seriously injured outside a Japanese restaurant; later, a quarrel between two Afghani men results in another stabbing. The perpetrator in the second case has been arrested. Connections between the two events are unclear as of now.
