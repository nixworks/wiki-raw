* Physicists at [[Brookhaven National Laboratory]] announce that they have created a newly discovered state of [[matter]] by smashing [[atom]]s in the [[Relativistic Heavy Ion Collider]]. This new state of matter behaves like a hot and dense [[liquid]] made up of basic atomic particles such as [[quark]]s and [[gluon]]s. Researchers claim that all matter in the [[universe]] for a fraction of a second after the [[Big Bang]] was in the form of this liquid. [http://www.bnl.gov/bnlweb/pubaf/pr/PR_display.asp?prID=05-38 (BNL News)]
