*[[Iraqi Kurdistan independence referendum, 2017|Kurdish independence]]
**The results of an [[independence referendum]] by [[Iraqi Kurdistan]] is to be announced. The vote is widely expected to be in favor of independence. [[Iran]] and [[Turkey]] have vowed that there may be violence if the result was independence.
