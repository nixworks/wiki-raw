* The office of the [[United States Attorney for the Southern District of New York]] announces that 10 individuals, including assistant coaches at four [[National Collegiate Athletic Association|NCAA]] [[NCAA Division I|Division I]] men's [[basketball]] programs—[[Arizona Wildcats men's basketball|Arizona]], [[Auburn Tigers men's basketball|Auburn]], [[Oklahoma State Cowboys basketball|Oklahoma State]], and [[USC Trojans men's basketball|USC]]—have been arrested on federal corruption charges.
