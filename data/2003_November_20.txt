* The [[FTAA]] negotiations in [[Miami]] end one day early; a menu approach is adopted to assure the future of the agreement, allowing individual countries to opt out of controversial or unacceptable provisions. * [[Terrorism|Terrorist attacks]]: Bombs hit [[Istanbul]], [[Turkey]]. At least 25 are killed and hundreds injured in two [[Car bomb|car-bomb]] blasts that devastate the Turkish headquarters of [[HSBC Bank (Turkey)|HSBC]] and the British [[Consular office|consulate]]. British Consul-General [[Roger Short]] is among the dead. The [[Great Eastern Islamic Raiders' Front]] (IBDA-C), a radical [[Islamist]] group, claims responsibility.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/europe/3222608.stm|title=BBC NEWS - Europe - Istanbul rocked by double bombing|publisher=|accessdate=22 December 2015}}</ref>
