*''[[Wikimedia Foundation v. NSA]]''
**The United States [[United States Court of Appeals for the Fourth Circuit|Fourth Circuit Court of Appeals]] rules that [[Wikimedia Foundation]] has the legal right to challenge the [[National Security Agency]] [[Upstream collection|Upstream program]] of mass online surveillance. This decision reinstates the suit that had been dismissed by a [[United States District Court for the District of Maryland|Federal district judge]] in [[Baltimore]], [[Maryland]]. Eight other organizations, including [[Amnesty International USA]] and the [[Global Fund for Women]], are also plaintiffs in this case. [http://www.reuters.com/article/us-wikipedia-nsa-idUSKBN18J206 (Reuters)]
