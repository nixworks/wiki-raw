*[[Rwandan constitutional referendum, 2015]]
**[[Rwanda]]ns vote overwhelmingly to lift [[Constitution of Rwanda|constitutional restrictions]] that will allow [[President of Rwanda|President]] [[Paul Kagame]] to run for more terms in office. The National Electoral Commission reports more than 98 percent of voters approved the referendum. Final official results will be released Monday.
