*[[2017–18 South Pacific cyclone season]]
**[[Tonga]] is nervously waiting for a direct hit from [[2017–18 South Pacific cyclone season#Severe Tropical Cyclone Gita|tropical cyclone Gita]], forecast to become a category 5 event. [[Tropical cyclone]] Gita is due to make landfall about 7pm (local time is the same as NZT). It has strengthened overnight and is [[Weather forecasting|forecast]] to strike Tonga. A [[state of emergency]] has been declared in the island [[kingdom]] and it is thought the [[cyclone]] will be the strongest [[storm]] ever to hit [[History of Tonga|Tonga in history]]. *[[Saratov Airlines Flight 703]]
**[[Emergency]] teams comb snowy fields outside [[Moscow]] Monday for debris from a crashed [[Russian]] [[airliner]] and the remains of the 71 people who died. [[Transport]] Minister [[Maksim Sokolov]] says the search for victims' remains will take a week. He notes that emergency teams already have found the plane's [[flight data recorder]], which is crucial for determining the crash's cause.
