*[[Hurricane Harvey]]
**[[Hurricane Harvey]] is projected to possibly become the second costliest [[hurricane]] in the [[United States]]'s history, with an estimated total as high as [[United States Dollar|US$]]108 billion. In comparison, [[Hurricane Katrina]]'s total damage is estimated to be around US$118 to US$160 billion. [http://abcnews.go.com/US/hurricane-harvey-projected-2nd-costliest-storm-us-history/story?id=49565583 (ABC News)]
*[[2017 California wildfires]]
**The La Tuna Canyon fire continues to burn in [[Los Angeles, California]], becoming the largest fire in the city's history. Los Angeles Mayor [[Eric Garcetti]] declares a [[state of emergency]] for the city. [http://www.cnn.com/2017/09/02/us/los-angeles-wildfire/index.html (CNN)] [https://www.nbcnews.com/news/us-news/la-tuna-fire-los-angeles-grows-thousands-acres-largest-brush-n798326 (NBC News)]
*[[Aftermath of World War II]]
**Large portions of [[Frankfurt]], [[Germany]], are evacuated as local authorities work to defuse the [[unexploded ordnance]] from a powerful [[Royal Air Force]] bomb. The evacuation is the largest to occur in Europe since [[World War II]]. [http://www.cnn.com/2017/09/03/europe/germany-frankfurt-bomb-evacuation/index.html (CNN)]
