*[[Hurricane Harvey]] 
**[[Hurricane Harvey]] is projected to possibly become the second costliest Hurricane in the [[United States]]'s history, with an estimated total as high as [[United States Dollar|US$]]108 billion.  In comparison, [[Hurricane Katrina]]'s total damage is estimated to be around US$118 to US$160 billion. **A seismic tremor is detected near an underground nuclear test site in [[North Korea]], with [[Japan]] claiming that its neighbour has  conducted its sixth nuclear test.
