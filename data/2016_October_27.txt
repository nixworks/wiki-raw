*[[NASA]] announces the return of the final piece of data collected during ''[[New Horizons]]''' flyby of [[Pluto]] in July 2015. The data, taking about five hours at [[light speed]] to travel across 5.5 billion kilometers (3.4 billion miles), arrived at the [[Johns Hopkins Applied Physics Laboratory]] on the morning of October 25.
