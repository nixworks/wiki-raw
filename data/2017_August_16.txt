*Several [[List of monuments and memorials of the Confederate States of America|Confederate monuments]] are taken down overnight in the city of [[Baltimore]], [[Maryland]], including the [[Stonewall Jackson and Robert E. Lee Monument]], [[Confederate Soldiers and Sailors Monument]], [[Confederate Women's Monument]] and the [[Roger B. Taney (sculpture)|Roger B. Taney sculpture]]. The [[Baltimore City Council]] unanimously voted to remove and destroy the monuments on August 14.
