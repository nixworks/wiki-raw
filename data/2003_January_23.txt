* 350 Australian troops begin their journey towards Iraq, joining the US and UK troops already assembled there. Prime Minister [[John Howard]] said that "pre-positioning troops increased the likelihood of (the crisis) being resolved peacefully". * Fast food restaurant chain [[McDonald's]] reports a quarterly loss for the first time. The loss amounted to US${{Nowrap|344 million}}. Shares in the company fell around 3% on the news.<ref>{{cite news|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/business/2688665.stm |title=Business &#124; McDonald's posts first-ever loss |publisher=BBC News |date=2003-01-23 |accessdate=2012-02-11}}</ref>
* The last signal from [[Pioneer 10]] was detected before losing signal with it completely.
