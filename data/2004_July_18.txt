* The trial for the murder of Canadian [[journalist]] [[Zahra Kazemi]] in [[Iran]] ended abruptly on the second day of the proceedings.  The lawyers of the Kazemi family insisted that the time has not been enough for proofs to be given, [[witness]]es to be brought to court, and the [[murderer]] to be identified.
