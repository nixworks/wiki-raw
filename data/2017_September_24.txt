*[[German federal election, 2017]]
**Voters in [[Germany]] go to the polls to elect members of the [[Bundestag]]. [[Chancellor of Germany|Chancellor]] [[Angela Merkel]] of the [[Christian Democratic Union of Germany|Christian Democratic Union]] is widely expected to win a fourth term in office. [http://www.dw.com/en/merkel-and-schulz-in-last-minute-bid-to-win-voters/a-40648409 (DW)]
**[[Exit poll]]s indicate Merkel has won a fourth term in power, and that [[Alternative for Germany]] will enter the [[Bundestag]] for the first time. [http://www.bbc.com/news/world-europe-41376577 (BBC)]

;Transport
* In [[Dubai]], [[United Arab Emirates]],  residents will receive free parking spots and free Salik tag among other incentives if they choose to own an [[electric vehicle]] and leave their fuel-powered cars behind, officials announced on Sunday. E-car users will be exempted from [[Roads and Transport Authority (Dubai)|RTA]] registration and renewal vehicle fees, and will get free [[Salik (road toll)|Salik]] tag upon registration. [https://www.khaleejtimes.com/news/transport/free-salik-tag-free-parking-for-electric-vehicles-in-dubai (Khaleej Times)]
