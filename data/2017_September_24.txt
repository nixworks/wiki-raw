;Transport
* In [[Dubai]], [[United Arab Emirates]],  residents will receive free parking spots and free Salik tag among other incentives if they choose to own an [[electric vehicle]] and leave their fuel-powered cars behind, officials announced on Sunday. E-car users will be exempted from [[Roads and Transport Authority (Dubai)|RTA]] registration and renewal vehicle fees, and will get free [[Salik (road toll)|Salik]] tag upon registration. [https://www.khaleejtimes.com/news/transport/free-salik-tag-free-parking-for-electric-vehicles-in-dubai (Khaleej Times)]
