* In [[Canada]], after 5 days of non-stop rain, the city of [[Calgary, AB]] is under its first local state of emergency. The [[Elbow River]] has risen to an extreme high and the town of [[Bragg Creek, Alberta|Bragg Creek]], just west of the city, is totally evacuated. This would be the first of 3 waves of major flooding.
