*[[New Zealand general election, 2017]]
**Voters in [[New Zealand]] go to the polls, electing all members of their [[New Zealand House of Representatives|House of Representatives]] and the consequent [[Prime Minister of New Zealand|Prime Minister]]. [[Opinion polling for the New Zealand general election, 2017|Opinion polling]] predicts that the long-incumbent [[New Zealand National Party|National Party]], led by [[Bill English]] [[New Zealand National Party leadership election, 2016|since December 2016]], will face its strongest challenge in years, as support for the [[New Zealand Labour Party|Labour Party]] has surged since [[Jacinda Ardern]] [[New Zealand Labour Party leadership election, 2017|became party leader seven weeks ago]].
