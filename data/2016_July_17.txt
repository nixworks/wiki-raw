;Sport
*[[2016 Open Championship]]
**In [[golf]], [[Henrik Stenson]] wins the 2016 Open Championship defeating [[Phil Mickelson]] after a closely fought battle in the final round at the [[Royal Troon Golf Club]] in Scotland. Stenson's total of 264, 20 under par, is the lowest stroke total ever recorded in any [[Men's major golf championships|major championship]] and ties [[Jason Day]]'s performance at [[2015 PGA Championship|last year's PGA Championship]] for the lowest score in relation to par at a major. It is also Stenson's first victory in a major tournament. [https://www.theguardian.com/sport/2016/jul/17/henrik-stenson-phil-mickelson-open (''The Guardian'')]
