* [[Uganda Human Rights Commission]], in its 2004 report, states that at least 4,000 children who were among the tens of thousands abducted by the [[Lord's Resistance Army]] rebels cannot be traced. This estimate is much lower than the previous estimate of 20,000 kidnapped children released by the most recent [[Human Rights Watch]] report. The UHRC accuses the [[Uganda People's Defence Forces]] (UPDF) of torturing the [[Acholi]] people of northern Uganda through brutal methods to extract information or to instill discipline in suspects.
