**The [[Russia|Russian]] military launches [[Cruise missile|cruise missile]] strikes from the [[Mediterranean Sea|Mediterranean]] on ISIS positions in [[Al-Raqqah]], the [[Islamic State of Iraq and the Levant]]'s de facto capital in [[Syria]], [[Aleppo Governorate|Aleppo]], and [[Idlib Governorate|Idlib]]. A [[United States|U.S.]] official confirms that Russia has conducted a significant number of strikes in Syria using both sea-launched cruise missiles and long-range bombers. *[[Refugees of the Syrian Civil War]]
**More than half of the [[States of the United States|state]] governors in the [[United States]] reject accepting asylum seekers from [[Syria]] after the [[November 2015 Paris attacks|attacks]] in [[Paris]]. All except one of them are [[Republican Party (United States)|Republicans]]. [http://www.cnn.com/2015/11/16/world/paris-attacks-syrian-refugees-backlash/index.html (CNN)]
**[[Obama administration]] officials hold a conference call with 34 governors to assure them, "refugees would undergo the most rigorous screening and security vetting of any category of traveler to the [[United States]]." A spokesperson for [[Governor of Michigan|Michigan Gov.]] [[Rick Snyder]] said that he has asked federal officials for "more robust data" and an "enhanced flow of information." [http://www.trust.org/item/20151118015212-z9u0v (Reuters)], [http://abcnews.go.com/US/wireStory/white-house-plans-call-governors-syrian-refugees-35262385 (AP via ABC News)]
*The [[President of the Czech Republic|president]] of the [[Czech Republic]], [[Miloš Zeman]], marks the 26th anniversary of the [[Velvet Revolution]] by attending an anti-Islam rally in [[Prague]] with the "Bloc against Islam" group which supports the president. The rally is also attended by observers from the [[Germany|German]] protest movement, [[Pegida|PEGIDA]] (Patriotic Europeans Against the Islamisation of the Occident) and [[Tommy Robinson (activist)|Tommy Robinson]], a prominent [[United Kingdom|British]] anti-Islam activist. Zeman tells the crowd of several thousand that they aren't extremists for advocating closing the Republic’s borders to [[Muslim|Muslim]] migrants. [http://abcnews.go.com/International/wireStory/czech-president-attends-addresses-rally-muslims-35252147 (AP via ABC News)], [http://www.breitbart.com/london/2015/11/17/eastern-europe-rising-czech-president-speaks-anti-islam-rally-pegida-leaders-tommy-robinson/ (Breitbart)]
