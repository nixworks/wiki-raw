;Science and technology
*[[Norway]] becomes the first country to start a complete [[FM radio switch-off|switch-off]] of national [[FM radio]] stations. The change, from the analogue FM system to [[digital audio broadcasting]], is expected to be completed by the end of the year. Some, such as the Norwegian Local Radio Association, are concerned about the impact on motorists and the elderly. [https://www.newscientist.com/article/2117569-norway-is-first-country-to-turn-off-fm-radio-and-go-digital-only/ (''New Scientist'')] [http://www.bbc.com/news/world-europe-38578050 (BBC)]
