*Extreme-right candidate [[Jean-Marie Le Pen]] qualifies for second round of [[French presidential election, 2002|French Presidential Elections]]. Right-wing incumbent [[Jacques Chirac]] places first. Socialist prime minister [[Lionel Jospin]] places third and announces his retirement from politics. Thousands take to the street all over France to protest against Le Pen.
**Chirac (19.88%), Le Pen (16.86%), [[Lionel Jospin|Jospin]] (16.18%), [[François Bayrou|Bayrou]] (6.5%), [[Arlette Laguiller|Laguiller]] (5.72%), [[Noël Mamère|Mamere]] (5.25%), [[Jean-Pierre Chevènement|Chevenement]] (5.32%), [[Olivier Besancenot|Besancenot]] (4.5%), [[Jean Saint-Josse|Saint-Josse]] (4.0%), [[Robert Hue|Hue]](3.6%). Turnout 75%.
