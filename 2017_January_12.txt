*[[Cuba–United States relations]]
**President [[Barack Obama]] announces the end of the [[wet feet, dry feet policy]], which previously allowed undocumented immigrants from Cuba to become permanent residents. [http://www.cnn.com/2017/01/12/politics/us-to-end-wet-foot-dry-foot-policy-for-cubans/index.html (CNN)]
*[[Operation Atlantic Resolve]]
**3,000 [[United States Armed Forces|U.S. troops]] are deployed in [[Poland]] as part of [[NATO]]'s [[Operation Atlantic Resolve]].  [[Poland]] says that the deployment is a necessary response to Russian military exercises near its border and [[Russian military intervention in Ukraine (2014–present)|its intervention in Ukraine]] while [[Russia]] condemns the move as a "threat to national interests and security". [http://www.bbc.com/news/world-europe-38592448 (BBC)], [http://www.aljazeera.com/news/2017/01/troops-poland-threat-russia-security-170112144005610.html (Al Jazeera)]
*[[European migrant crisis]]
**[[Germany]] announces plans to start deporting newly-arrived migrants to [[Greece]] starting in March. According to [[European Union|EU]]'s [[Dublin Regulation]], [[asylum seeker]]s must file for asylum in the [[Member state of the European Union|member state]] they arrived in. [http://www.aljazeera.com/news/2017/01/germany-return-refugees-greece-starting-march-170112123822339.html (Al Jazeera)]
