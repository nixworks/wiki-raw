**[[President of Peru|Peruvian  President]] [[Pedro Pablo Kuczynski]]'s government creates a temporary visa for thousands of [[Venezuela]]ns whose country  is mired in a deep economic crisis. "This is part of our migratory policy that aims to build bridges not walls", says [[Ministry of the Interior (Peru)|Interior Minister]] Carlos Basombrio.
