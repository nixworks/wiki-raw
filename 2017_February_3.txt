*[[Fukushima Daiichi nuclear disaster]]
**According to [[Tohoku Electric Power]], radiations levels within one of the reactors of [[Onagawa Nuclear Power Plant]], damaged by [[2011 Tōhoku earthquake and tsunami|an earthquake and tsunami]] in 2011, are at its highest level since experiencing a meltdown, with a reading of 530 [[sieverts]] an hour.
