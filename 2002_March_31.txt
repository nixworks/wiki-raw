*Israeli-Palestinian conflict: Israeli troops exchange gunfire with guards of [[Yasser Arafat|Yasir Arafat]] in [[Ramallah]]. A [[suicide bomber]] identified as [[Shadi Tubasi]], a resident of the refugee camp [[Jenin]], kills 14 and wounds more than 40 in [[Haifa]]. Later, a suicide bomber wounds four members of an [[intensive-care unit]], one critically, in a [[paramedic]]s' dispatch station in [[Efrat]]. In the past 18 months, according to the [[Associated Press]], 1262 people have been killed on the Palestinian side and on 401 on the Israeli side.
