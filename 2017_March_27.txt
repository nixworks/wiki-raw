;Sports
*The [[National Football League]] (NFL) owners approve, by a 31–1 vote, the move of the [[Oakland Raiders]] to [[Las Vegas]], [[Nevada]], for the 2019 or 2020 season. The Las Vegas Raiders will become the second [[Major professional sports leagues in the United States and Canada|top-level professional sports]] franchise in the city, following the [[National Hockey League]]'s [[Vegas Golden Knights]], which will begin play in [[2017–18 NHL season|October 2017]]. The move now awaits completion of a [[Proposed Las Vegas football stadium|new Raiders stadium]], until which time the Raiders will continue playing home games in [[Oakland, California|Oakland]]. [[Bank of America]] has issued the Raiders [[United States dollar|$]]650 million in credit to purchase land and build the new stadium in [[Clark County, Nevada|Clark County]]. [http://profootballtalk.nbcsports.com/2017/03/27/raiders-vote-coming-soon/ (NBC Sports)], [https://www.washingtonpost.com/news/sports/wp/2017/03/27/nfl-approves-raiders-move-to-las-vegas/?pushid=58d9563104329f1d0000000b&tid=notifi_push_breaking-news&utm_term=.2fd25da52020 (''The Washington Post'')]
