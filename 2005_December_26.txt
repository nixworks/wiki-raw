* 15-year-old Jane Creba is killed and six others are wounded in [[Toronto]]'s [[Boxing Day shooting]], when two groups of gunmen open fire outside a [[Foot Locker, Inc.|Foot Locker]] store near the [[Toronto Eaton Centre|Eaton Centre]] in Toronto. [http://www.cnn.com/2005/WORLD/asiapcf/12/26/tsunami.main/index.html (CNN)]
* A [[December 2005 St. Petersburg gas attack|gas attack]] occurs at an outlet of the Maksidom chain on Moskovsky Prospect in [[Saint Petersburg]], [[Russia]], and leads to the hospitalization of numerous victims. [http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/world/europe/4560344.stm (BBC)]
* The [[prime minister of Israel]], [[Ariel Sharon]], will undergo a [[cardiac catheterization]] to seal a hole in his heart. The surgery is a routine procedure for this congenital defect, according to doctors in [[Jerusalem]]. [http://edition.cnn.com/2005/WORLD/meast/12/26/sharon.ap/ (CNN)]
* A [[Qassam rocket]] fired at the Israeli kibbutz [[Sa'ad (kibbutz)|Sa'ad]] lands near a [[preschool]]. No injuries are reported. [http://www.ynetnews.com/articles/0,7340,L-3190485,00.html (Ynetnews)]
* ''[[SeaQuest DSV]]'' is released on DVD for the first time.
* [[Kerry Packer]], major shareholder of [[Publishing and Broadcasting Limited|PBL]], dies of [[kidney failure]] at the age of 68.
