* [[Vanessa Legget]] is found in contempt by a Federal Court for refusing to release notes made for her book on the [[Doris Angleton]] murder.
* * The [[27th G8 summit]] takes place in [[Genoa]], Italy until July 22nd. Massive demonstrations against the meeting by [[anti-globalisation]] groups. One demonstrator, [[Carlo Giuliani]], is shot dead by a [[carabinieri|carabiniere]] and several others are badly injured during an attack by the police on a school which the protesters were using as their headquarters.
