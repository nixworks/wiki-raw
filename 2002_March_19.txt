*The [[Zimbabwe]] Congress of Trade Unions calls a three-day [[general strike]] protesting police interference with union meetings, harassment of [[trade union]]ists, and general lawlessness following the general elections, which has led to slow-downs in business.  In [[South Korea]], 5,600 power plant workers are on the 25th day of their strike, protesting the [[neoliberalism|neoliberal]] government plans to [[privatize]] the state-run electricity plants.  A vote among the remaining power plant workers to strike is cancelled due to company interference.
