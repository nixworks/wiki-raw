Events on [[November 11]] [[2011]]:

;Armed conflicts and attacks
*[[Arab Spring]]:
**[[2011 Syrian uprising]]: 250 Syrians, mostly civilians, have been killed  so far this month, as the violence escalates in Syria's eight-month-old crackdown on [[2011 Syrian uprising |pro-democracy protests]] and attacks on security forces increase. [http://www.cbc.ca/news/world/story/2011/11/11/syria-death-toll.html (CBC)] [http://www.msnbc.msn.com/id/45252656/ns/world_news-mideast_n_africa/#.Tr8xpz2VrUB (MSNBC)]
**[[2011 Yemeni uprising]]: At least seven people die after forces loyal to the [[President of Yemen]] [[Ali Abdullah Saleh]] shell the city of [[Taez]]. A further 32 were injured. [http://english.alarabiya.net/articles/2011/11/11/176529.html (''Al-Arabiya'')] [http://www.cnn.com/2011/11/11/world/meast/yemen-unrest/index.html?hpt=wo_c2 (CNN)]
*An armed hijacker claiming to have a bomb takes control of a [[ferry]] on the [[Sea of Marmara]] near the [[Turkey|Turkish]] port of [[Izmit]] but is later killed by the [[Turkish Armed Forces|Turkish military]]. [http://www.google.com/hostednews/ap/article/ALeqM5jYhjEzfeWcoUHJSouTbGZSXwyTSQ?docId=fd2c648f55224d4bb478ace076f48afa (AP via Google)] [http://www.jpost.com/MiddleEast/Article.aspx?id=245285 (''Jerusalem Post'')]
