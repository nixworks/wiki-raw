* [[Mohamed ElBaradei]], head of the [[International Atomic Energy Agency]] (IAEA), states there is no evidence to suggest [[Iran]] is developing [[nuclear weapon]]s. [http://www.washingtonpost.com/wp-dyn/articles/A27319-2005Feb15.html (Washington Post)]
* [[NHL Commissioner]] [[Gary Bettman]] announces the cancellation of the [[2004–05 NHL season|2004–05 season]] because of the ongoing [[2004–05 NHL lockout|lockout]]. It is the first time a North American [[professional sports]] league has cancelled an entire season due to a labor dispute. [http://tsn.ca/nhl/news_story.asp?ID=115107&hubName=nhl (TSN)]
