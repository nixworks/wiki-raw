*[[2014 FIFA World Cup]]
**[[Uruguay national football team|Uruguay]]'s star striker [[Luis Suárez]] is expelled from the tournament, banned for nine international matches and suspended from all football-related activity for four months for biting [[Giorgio Chiellini]] of [[Italy national football team|Italy]] in Tuesday's [[2014 FIFA World Cup Group D|Group D]] match.
