* A [[London]] newspaper reports that [[Merrill Lynch]], [[New York]], is trying to persuade at least seventeen members of the [[derivative (finance)|corporate derivatives]] team of rival investment bank [[JP Morgan]] to defect. All 25 EU member states must ratify the treaty for it to come into effect - 9 have done so to date, but only [[Spain]] has [[History of the European Constitution|previously held]] a referendum.
