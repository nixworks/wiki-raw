* [[Impeachment]] proceedings against [[Philippines|Philippine]] President [[Joseph Estrada]], accused of playing [[Jueteng]], end preeminently and trigger the second [[EDSA Dos|EDSA People Power Revolution]] or [[People Power II]]. His Vice-President [[Gloria Macapagal-Arroyo]] succeeds him as the 14th President of the Republic.
