* [[Belgium]] is the second European country (after the [[Netherlands]]) to legalise [[euthanasia]].
* [[Stock market downturn of 2002]]: Global indices sink heavily today, with the [[Nasdaq]] falling 3% to a 6-year low of 1,184.94. The yield of the [[United States Department of the Treasury|U.S. Treasury]]'s 10-year bond sank to a 40-year low of 3.70%, with the 2-year bond yield falling to a record low of 1.89%. Meanwhile, concern of the [[2003 invasion of Iraq|U.S. plan to invade Iraq]] pushed oil prices to over $30 a barrel, not seen since February 2001.
*  [[Spratly Islands]]:  The Governor of the [[Philippines|Philippine]] state of [[Palawan]] has sent Philippine soldiers to take possession of the uninhabited oil-rich Spratly Islands, which are claimed by the Philippines, [[Brunei]], the People's Republic of China, [[Malaysia]], the [[Republic of China]] ([[Taiwan]]), and [[Vietnam]].
