**[[Ministry of Interior (Iraq)|Iraqi Interior Minister]] [[Mohammed Al-Ghabban|Mohammed Salem Al-Ghabban]] says he has submitted his resignation. *[[Conservative Party (UK) leadership election, 2016]]
**[[Home Secretary]] [[Theresa May]] gets 165 votes after the first ballot of [[Conservative Party (UK)|Conservative]] members of parliament  to select a new [[Leader of the Conservative Party (UK)|Leader]] and the next [[Prime Minister of the United Kingdom|Prime Minister]].  Her nearest rival,  [[Department of Energy and Climate Change#Ministers|Minister of State for Energy]] [[Andrea Leadsom]], picked up 66 votes.  [[Secretary of State for Justice]] [[Michael Gove]] won 48 votes, and  [[Secretary of State for Work and Pensions]] [[Stephen Crabb]] got 34 votes. Former [[Secretary of State for Defence]] [[Liam Fox]] is eliminated from consideration as he received just 16. Later, Crabb pulled out of the race  and endorsed front-runner Theresa May. Fox also endorsed May. [http://in.reuters.com/article/britain-eu-wrapup-idINKCN0ZL1EM (Reuters)] [http://news.sky.com/story/1722116/may-wins-first-tory-vote-as-fox-and-crabb-out (Sky News)] [http://www.reuters.com/article/us-britain-eu-crabb-idUSKCN0ZL2GI (Reuters²)]
