* The [[Sudan]]ese government announces the lifting of a partial ban on [[United Nations]] operations in the [[Darfur conflict|conflict-hit Darfur region]]. The ban was made after the government accused the UN of transporting a rebel leader who opposes a recent peace deal. [http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/africa/5114216.stm (BBC)]
* [[Arcelor]] declares its merger with [[Mittal Steel]]. The new company will be called ''Arcelor-Mittal''. <!--Note: Merger between world's two biggest steel giants. --> [http://www.ibnlive.com/news/ln-mittal-clinches-arcelor-deal/13810-7.html (CNN-IBN)] [http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/business/5114290.stm (BBC)]
* Eight [[Palestinian people|Palestinian]] militants, including [[Hamas]] militants, infiltrate into an army post in [[Israel]] using a [[Smuggling tunnel#Smuggling tunnels in Rafah, Gaza Strip|tunnel]]. Two Israeli soldiers are killed, one [[Kidnapping|kidnapped]] and three wounded in the attack, in which at least two Palestinian militants die. [[Ehud Olmert|Israeli PM]] vows a fierce military response to the attack once the soldier, [[Gilad Shalit]], is returned. Two infantry brigades and supporting armoured regiments are deployed along the [[Gaza Strip]] border, in preparation for a major offensive.[http://www.haaretz.com/hasen/spages/730994.html (Haaretz)],[http://go.reuters.co.uk/newsArticle.jhtml?type=worldNews&storyID=1275797&section=news&src=rss/uk/worldNews (Reuters UK)],[http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/world/middle_east/5119402.stm (BBC)]
* [[José Ramos-Horta]] resigns as both Foreign and Interim Defence Minister of [[East Timor]] amidst [[2006 East Timor crisis|ongoing political turmoil]]. [http://www.smh.com.au/news/world/ramos-horta-resigns-from-government/2006/06/25/1151174067550.html (SMH)]
* Italians vote in a [[Italian referendum, 2006|referendum]] on whether to approve the modification of 53 articles in the [[Constitution of Italy|constitution]]. Approval would give more power to the [[Prime Minister of Italy|prime minister]] and to the [[Regions of Italy|regions]], making [[Italy]] a [[federal state]]. [http://news.scotsman.com/latest.cfm?id=928922006 (Scotsman)]
