* Following [[Ethiopian general elections, 2005|general elections]] in May, [[Ethiopia]] releases the first round of official results for 307 of 527 parliamentary seats. The ruling [[Ethiopian People's Revolutionary Democratic Front|EPRDF]] has won 139 seats, while opposition parties [[Coalition for Unity and Democracy|CUD]] and [[United Ethiopian Democratic Forces|UEDF]] won 93 and 42, respectively. Smaller parties and independent candidates won the remaining 33 seats.  CUD and UEDF announced plans to form a coalition government. [http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/africa/4660047.stm (BBC News)]
* [[Hurricane Dennis]], the first [[hurricane]] of the [[2005 Atlantic hurricane season]], approaches [[Cuba]].  It is heading towards the [[Gulf Coast of the United States]], with landfall expected on Sunday or Monday.
