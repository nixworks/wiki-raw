*[[2016 Pacific typhoon season]]
**The [[Philippines|Philippine]] government places more than two dozen [[Provinces of the Philippines|provinces]] on alert before [[Typhoon Haima (2016)|Typhoon Haima]] makes landfall over [[Luzon]]. Haima, downgraded by the [[Global Disaster Alert and Coordination System]] to a Category 4 storm, landfalls [[Cagayan|Cagayan province]] in the island's northeast. Weather forecasters expect the storm to impact up to 2.7 million people  before veering northwest toward the [[China|Chinese]] coast on October 21.
