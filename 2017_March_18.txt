*[[Syrian Civil War]]
**Around 1,500–2,000 people leave al-Waer district of [[Homs]] today for their preferred destination of either Northern Homs Governorate, [[Idlib Governorate]] or Turkish Army positions in Northern Aleppo Governorate. Up to 15,000 to 20,000 people including armed militants, their families and supporters will leave the besieged district in the coming weeks with the rest of the population reconciling and receiving aid. **French security forces shoot a man dead in [[Paris]]' [[Orly Airport]] during his attempt to take an assault rifle from a member of the army's [[Opération Sentinelle|"Sentinelle" operation]], which has been responsible for patrolling airports and other key sites.
