* The [[Supreme Court of Indonesia]] orders ''[[Time (magazine)|TIME]]'' magazine to pay [[United States dollar|US$]]106 million in damages to former [[President of Indonesia]] [[Suharto]]. The case had previously been rejected by two lower courts. [http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/asia-pacific/6987843.stm (BBC)]
