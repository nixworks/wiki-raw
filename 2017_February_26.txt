;Arts and culture
*[[89th Academy Awards]]
**The 89th Academy Awards are held in [[Los Angeles, California]]. [http://abcnews.go.com/Entertainment/oscars-2017-complete-winners-list/story?id=45718405 (ABC)]
**''[[Moonlight (2016 film)|Moonlight]]'' wins [[Academy Award for Best Picture|Best Picture]], moments after ''[[La La Land]]'' was mistakenly announced as the winner. It also won [[Academy Award for Best Adapted Screenplay|Best Adapted Screenplay]]. [http://www.abc.net.au/news/2017-02-27/oscars-2017-academy-awards-la-la-land-moonlight-win/8307650 (ABC News Australia)], [http://abcnews.go.com/Entertainment/oscars-2017-complete-winners-list/story?id=45718405 (ABC)]
**[[Mahershala Ali]] wins the [[Academy Award for Best Supporting Actor|Best Supporting Actor]] for his role in ''[[Moonlight (film)|Moonlight]]''. He is the first [[Islam in the United States|Muslim American]] to win the award. [https://www.theguardian.com/global/video/2017/feb/27/mahershala-ali-becomes-the-first-muslim-actor-to-win-an-oscar-video (''The Guardian'')]
**''[[La La Land (film)|La La Land]]'' 's director [[Damien Chazelle]] wins the Oscar for [[Academy Award for Best Director|Best Director]] and [[Emma Stone]] wins the [[Academy Award for Best Actress|Best Actress]]. [https://www.theguardian.com/film/2017/feb/27/oscar-winners-2017-the-full-list-academy-awards (''The Guardian'')]
**[[Casey Affleck]] wins the [[Academy Award for Best Actor|Best Actor]] for his role in ''[[Manchester by the Sea (film)|Manchester by the Sea]]'', which also won [[Academy Award for Best Original Screenplay|Best Original Screenplay]]. [http://abcnews.go.com/Entertainment/oscars-2017-complete-winners-list/story?id=45718405 (ABC)]
**[[Viola Davis]] wins the [[Academy Award for Best Supporting Actress|Best Supporting Actress]] for her role in ''[[Fences (film)|Fences]]''. [http://abcnews.go.com/Entertainment/oscars-2017-complete-winners-list/story?id=45718405 (ABC)]
**''[[The Salesman (2016 film)|The Salesman]]'' directed by [[Asghar Farhadi]] won [[Academy Award for Best Foreign Language Film|Best Foreign Language Film]]. Farhadi and other film crew did not attend the ceremony in protest of [[Donald Trump]]'s [[Executive Order 13769|visa ban]]. First Iranian in space [[Anousheh Ansari]] and scienist [[Firouz Naderi]] attended in place of Farhadi. [https://www.theguardian.com/film/2017/feb/27/the-salesman-wins-best-foreign-language-oscar-asghar-farhadi (''The Guardian'')]
**''[[O.J.: Made in America]]'' wins the [[Academy Award for Best Documentary Feature|Best Documentary Feature]]. [http://abcnews.go.com/Entertainment/oscars-2017-complete-winners-list/story?id=45718405 (ABC)]
**The [[Netflix]] documentary ''[[The White Helmets (film)|The White Helmets]]'', a film about the [[Syrian Civil War]],  wins the [[Academy Award for Best Documentary (Short Subject)|Best Documentary Short]]. Its cinematographer was unable to receive the award due his passport being canceled by the Syrian Regime.[http://www.cnn.com/2017/02/26/us/white-helmets-oscar/ (CNN)]
**''[[Zootopia]]'' wins the [[Academy Award for Best Animated Feature|Best Animated Feature]]. [http://abcnews.go.com/Entertainment/oscars-2017-complete-winners-list/story?id=45718405 (ABC)]
