;Political elections
*[[Estonian parliamentary election, 2015]]
**The ruling [[Estonian Reform Party|Reform Party]] wins the elections, but loses the majority that it has had with its current coalition partner, the [[Social Democratic Party (Estonia)|Social Democratic Party]]. For the first time in many years, two new parties cross the election threshold, the right-of-centre [[Free Party (Estonia)|Free Party]] and the eurosceptic [[Conservative People's Party of Estonia]]. [http://news.err.ee/v/main_news/4249bcba-7176-4b74-af11-9e4812c78368 (Estonian Public Broadcasting)] 
*[[Andorran parliamentary election, 2015]]
**Despite losing five seats, the [[Democrats for Andorra]] narrowly retains their absolute majority in the [[General Council (Andorra)|General Council]], winning 15 of the 28 seats. [http://www.eleccions.ad/resultats (Elections Andorra)]
