*[[2016 Croydon tram derailment]]
**At least five people have been killed and 51 hospitalised after a [[Tramlink]] light rail vehicle derailed and overturned in a tunnel close to [[Sandilands tram stop]] in the [[London Borough of Croydon]] early on Wednesday. It is the first fatal tram accident in the United Kingdom since 1959. [http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-england-london-37919658 (BBC News)]
