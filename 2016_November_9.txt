;Disasters and accidents
*[[2016 Croydon tram derailment]]
**At least seven people have been killed and 51 hospitalised after a [[Tramlink]] light rail vehicle derailed and overturned in a tunnel close to [[Sandilands tram stop]] in the [[London Borough of Croydon]] early on Wednesday. It is believed to be the first tram accident involving fatalities on the tram in the [[United Kingdom]] since 1959. [http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-england-london-37919658 (BBC News)]

;Politics and elections
*[[United States presidential election, 2016|United States presidential election]] and [[United States elections, 2016|general elections]], 2016
**Although he narrowly lost the popular vote, [[Donald Trump]], the [[Republican Party (United States)|Republican]] nominee for the [[United States presidential election, 2016|2016 presidential election]], becomes the [[president-elect of the United States]] after defeating [[Hillary Clinton]], the [[Democratic Party (United States)|Democratic]] nominee. It is the fourth time in United States history where the winning candidate lost the popular vote, the last such defeat occurring in [[United States presidential election, 2000|2000]].
