*[[War in Donbass]]
**[[Donetsk People's Republic]] commander [[Mikhail Tolstykh]], better known by his callsign "Givi", is assassinated in a bomb blast in [[Makiivka]], [[Donetsk Oblast]]. DPR officials blame [[Ukraine]]'s [[Security Service of Ukraine|secret service]] for the attack. [http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-europe-38905110 (BBC)]
*[[War in Afghanistan (2015–present)]]
**Six [[Afghanistan|Afghan]] [[International Committee of the Red Cross|Red Cross]] workers are shot dead by suspected [[Islamic State of Iraq and the Levant – Khorasan Province|ISIL]] militants in the northern [[Jowzjan Province]]. Two other Red Cross workers are reportedly abducted. [http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-asia-38908312 (BBC)]
