*[[Finland–Russia relations]]
**[[Finland]] and [[Russia]] agree to impose temporary restrictions at two Arctic border crossing points on the [[Finnish–Russian border]] following an increased flow of asylum seekers from Russia to Finland. Under a deal clinched by [[President of Russia|Russian President]] [[Vladimir Putin]] and [[President of Finland|Finnish President]] [[Sauli Niinistö]] in [[Moscow]], crossings at two popular entry points, [[Salla]] and Raja-Jooseppi, will be reserved only for Finnish, Russian and [[Belarus|Belarusian]] citizens and their family members for a period of 180 days. [http://www.reuters.com/article/us-europe-migrants-finland-russia-idUSKCN0WO32P (Reuters)]

**[[President of Brazil|Brazil President]] [[Dilma Rousseff]] says she will not resign under any circumstances because she hadn't committed a crime, and equated a bid to impeach her to a [[coup d'état]] against democratic rule. *[[Indian Wells Masters|Indian Wells]] ([[California]])  CEO and tennis tournament director [[Raymond Moore (tennis)|Raymond Moore]] resigns following reactions of outrage, including by top-ranked [[Serena Williams]] and retired legend [[Martina Navratilova]], to his comment that top-level women's players rode "on the coattails of the men" and were "very, very lucky" to have equal prize money.
