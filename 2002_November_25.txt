* President [[George W. Bush]] signs the [[Homeland Security Act]] into law. The act creates a new [[United States Department of Homeland Security|Department of Homeland Security]] and is the largest government reorganization since the [[National Security Act of 1947]] more than 50 years before.
* A group of European scientists has announced that they intend to use the [[Very Large Telescope]] to take pictures of the lunar module bases remaining on the Moon, in order to debunk the [[Apollo moon landing conspiracy theory]] that states that the [[Apollo program|Apollo moon landings]] were a [[hoax]].
