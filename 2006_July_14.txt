* The trial of seven former [[Bosnian Serb]] officers for alleged involvement in the [[Srebrenica massacre]] begins at [[The Hague]] [[International Criminal Tribunal for the Former Yugoslavia|war crimes tribunal]]. Five of the seven standing trial face [[genocide]] charges, as well as [[crimes against humanity]]. The trial is the largest yet staged at The Hague.
