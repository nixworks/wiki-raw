*[[2016-2017 Cook Inlet natural gas leak]]
**An investigation by the [[Pipeline and Hazardous Materials Safety Administration]] finds that the [[natural gas]] leak occurring in [[Alaska]]s [[Cook Inlet]] began earlier than first reported, and represents significant safety and environmental risks. The agency gave pipeline operator Hilcorp Energy until May 1, 2017 to fix the leak, or shut the pipeline down.
