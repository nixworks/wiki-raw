*[[Israeli–Palestinian conflict]]
**[[Israel]]i [[airstrike]]s targeting [[Hamas]] weapons facilities in the [[Gaza Strip]] result in an explosion that collapsed a nearby house, killing a 30-year old pregnant [[Palestinians|Palestinian woman]] and her two-year old daughter. Four others were wounded, including the woman's husband and son, the [[Health Minister of the Gaza Strip|Gaza Health Ministry]] said. The [[Israel Defense Forces|Israeli military]] said the airstrike was in response to Saturday's pair of rocket launches from Gaza at Israel, as well as two large riots that succeeded in breaching the border fence. **[[Israel]]i traffic police stop a Palestinian woman transporting a small gas canister within Israeli territory near [[Jerusalem]]. She detonates the gas canister, lightly wounding a police officer and critically wounding herself. National elections are still scheduled for November 1, 2015.
