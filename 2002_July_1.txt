* A Russian [[Tupolev Tu-154]] airliner and a [[Boeing&nbsp;757]] operated by [[DHL Aviation|DHL]] [[Bashkirian Airlines Flight 2937|collide]] at 35,000&nbsp;ft over [[Überlingen]], due to failure of correct communication from ground-to-air. The 69 people aboard the Tupolev (mainly Russian schoolchildren) and the two pilots of the Boeing are all killed.
