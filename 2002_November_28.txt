* [[Thanksgiving Day]] 2002.
* [[2002 Mombasa attacks]]: Three [[suicide bomber]]s detonated themselves at a hotel in [[Mombasa]], [[Kenya]], killing a number of people, including [[Israel]]i tourists who have been presumed to be the targets of the attack. At the same time two [[anti-aircraft]] [[missile]]s were fired at a passenger aircraft, which only narrowly missed. The two attacks are suspected to be connected, and it is suspected that [[al-Qaeda]] may be involved in the attacks.
* [[Henry Kissinger]] is appointed chairman of the [[National Commission on Terrorist Attacks Upon the United States]], commonly known as the 9/11 Commission.
