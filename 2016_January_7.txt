*[[January 2016 Paris police station attack]]
**[[Law enforcement in France|French police]] shoot dead a knife-wielding man, who was shouting "Allahu Akbar", outside a police station in [[Goutte d'Or]], located in the [[18th arrondissement of Paris|18th arrondissement]] of [[Paris]]. A piece of paper with an [[Islamic State of Iraq and the Levant|Islamic State]] flag and claim for the attack written in Arabic was found on his body. The incident is noted for taking place on the anniversary of last year's [[Charlie Hebdo shooting|''Charlie Hebdo'' shooting]]. [http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-europe-35250344 (BBC)]
