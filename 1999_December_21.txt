* The [[Spanish Civil Guard]] intercepts near [[Calatayud]] ([[Zaragoza (province)|Zaragoza]]) a [[Madrid]]-bound van driven by [[ETA (separatist group)|ETA]] and loaded with 950&nbsp;kg of [[explosive]]s. The next day, another van loaded with 750&nbsp;kg is found not far from there. The incident is known as "''la caravana de la muerte''" (the caravan of death). Shortly after [[September 11, 2001 Terrorist Attacks|9/11]], ETA confirmed their plan had been to blow down [[Torre Picasso]]
